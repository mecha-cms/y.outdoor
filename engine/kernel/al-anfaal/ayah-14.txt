### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ذَٰلِكُمْ فَذُوقُوهُ وَأَنَّ لِلْكَـٰفِرِينَ عَذَابَ ٱلنَّارِ</span>

### English

That ˹worldly punishment˺ is yours, so taste it! Then the disbelievers will suffer the torment of the Fire.
