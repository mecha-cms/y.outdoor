### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَإِمَّا تَثْقَفَنَّهُمْ فِى ٱلْحَرْبِ فَشَرِّدْ بِهِم مَّنْ خَلْفَهُمْ لَعَلَّهُمْ يَذَّكَّرُونَ</span>

### English

If you ever encounter them in battle, make a fearsome example of them, so perhaps those who would follow them may be deterred.
