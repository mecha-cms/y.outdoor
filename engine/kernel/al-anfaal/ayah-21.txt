### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا تَكُونُوا۟ كَٱلَّذِينَ قَالُوا۟ سَمِعْنَا وَهُمْ لَا يَسْمَعُونَ</span>

### English

Do not be like those who say, “We hear,” but in fact they are not listening.
