### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱعْلَمُوٓا۟ أَنَّمَآ أَمْوَٰلُكُمْ وَأَوْلَـٰدُكُمْ فِتْنَةٌ وَأَنَّ ٱللَّهَ عِندَهُۥٓ أَجْرٌ عَظِيمٌ</span>

### English

And know that your wealth and your children are only a test and that with Allah is a great reward.
