### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِن تَوَلَّوْا۟ فَٱعْلَمُوٓا۟ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ مَوْلَىٰكُمْ ۚ نِعْمَ ٱلْمَوْلَىٰ وَنِعْمَ ٱلنَّصِيرُ</span>

### English

And if they do not comply, then know that Allah is your Protector. What an excellent Protector, and what an excellent Helper!
