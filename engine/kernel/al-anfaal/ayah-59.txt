### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا يَحْسَبَنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ سَبَقُوٓا۟ ۚ إِنَّهُمْ لَا يُعْجِزُونَ</span>

### English

Do not let those disbelievers think they are not within reach. They will have no escape.
