### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يُجَـٰدِلُونَكَ فِى ٱلْحَقِّ بَعْدَ مَا تَبَيَّنَ كَأَنَّمَا يُسَاقُونَ إِلَى ٱلْمَوْتِ وَهُمْ يَنظُرُونَ</span>

### English

They disputed with you about the truth after it had been made clear, as if they were being driven to death with their eyes wide open.
