### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا كَانَ صَلَاتُهُمْ عِندَ ٱلْبَيْتِ إِلَّا مُكَآءً وَتَصْدِيَةً ۚ فَذُوقُوا۟ ٱلْعَذَابَ بِمَا كُنتُمْ تَكْفُرُونَ</span>

### English

Their prayer at the Sacred House was nothing but whistling and clapping. So taste the punishment for your disbelief.
