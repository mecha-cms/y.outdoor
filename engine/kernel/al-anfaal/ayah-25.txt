### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱتَّقُوا۟ فِتْنَةً لَّا تُصِيبَنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا۟ مِنكُمْ خَآصَّةً ۖ وَٱعْلَمُوٓا۟ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ شَدِيدُ ٱلْعِقَابِ</span>

### English

Beware of a trial that will not only affect the wrongdoers among you. And know that Allah is severe in punishment.
