### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَمَنِ ٱنتَصَرَ بَعْدَ ظُلْمِهِۦ فَأُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ مَا عَلَيْهِم مِّن سَبِيلٍ</span>

### English

There is no blame on those who enforce justice after being wronged.
