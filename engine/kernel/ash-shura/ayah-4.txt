### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَهُۥ مَا فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَمَا فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ ۖ وَهُوَ ٱلْعَلِىُّ ٱلْعَظِيمُ</span>

### English

To Him belongs whatever is in the heavens and whatever is on the earth. And He is the Most High, the Greatest.
