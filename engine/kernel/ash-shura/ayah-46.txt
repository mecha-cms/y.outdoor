### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا كَانَ لَهُم مِّنْ أَوْلِيَآءَ يَنصُرُونَهُم مِّن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ ۗ وَمَن يُضْلِلِ ٱللَّهُ فَمَا لَهُۥ مِن سَبِيلٍ</span>

### English

They will have no protectors to help them against Allah. And whoever Allah leaves to stray, for them there is no way.
