### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَآ أَنتُم بِمُعْجِزِينَ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ ۖ وَمَا لَكُم مِّن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ مِن وَلِىٍّ وَلَا نَصِيرٍ</span>

### English

You can never escape ˹Him˺ on earth, nor do you have any protector or helper besides Allah.
