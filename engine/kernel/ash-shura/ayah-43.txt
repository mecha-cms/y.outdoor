### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَمَن صَبَرَ وَغَفَرَ إِنَّ ذَٰلِكَ لَمِنْ عَزْمِ ٱلْأُمُورِ</span>

### English

And whoever endures patiently and forgives—surely this is a resolve to aspire to.
