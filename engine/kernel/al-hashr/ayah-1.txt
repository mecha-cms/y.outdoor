### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">سَبَّحَ لِلَّهِ مَا فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَمَا فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ ۖ وَهُوَ ٱلْعَزِيزُ ٱلْحَكِيمُ</span>

### English

Whatever is in the heavens and whatever is on the earth glorifies Allah. For He is the Almighty, All-Wise.
