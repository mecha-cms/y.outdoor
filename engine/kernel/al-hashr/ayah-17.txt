### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَكَانَ عَـٰقِبَتَهُمَآ أَنَّهُمَا فِى ٱلنَّارِ خَـٰلِدَيْنِ فِيهَا ۚ وَذَٰلِكَ جَزَٰٓؤُا۟ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ</span>

### English

So they will both end up in the Fire, staying there forever. That is the reward of the wrongdoers.
