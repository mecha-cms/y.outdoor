### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا تَكُونُوا۟ كَٱلَّذِينَ نَسُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ فَأَنسَىٰهُمْ أَنفُسَهُمْ ۚ أُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْفَـٰسِقُونَ</span>

### English

And do not be like those who forgot Allah, so He made them forget themselves. It is they who are ˹truly˺ rebellious.
