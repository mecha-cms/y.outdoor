### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَعَمِلُوا۟ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ لَهُمْ أَجْرٌ غَيْرُ مَمْنُونٍۭ</span>

### English

But those who believe and do good will have a never-ending reward.
