### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">هُوَ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَكُمْ فَمِنكُمْ كَافِرٌ وَمِنكُم مُّؤْمِنٌ ۚ وَٱللَّهُ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ بَصِيرٌ</span>

### English

He is the One Who created you, yet some of you are disbelievers while some are believers. And Allah is All-Seeing of what you do.
