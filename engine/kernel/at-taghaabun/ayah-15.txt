### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّمَآ أَمْوَٰلُكُمْ وَأَوْلَـٰدُكُمْ فِتْنَةٌ ۚ وَٱللَّهُ عِندَهُۥٓ أَجْرٌ عَظِيمٌ</span>

### English

Your wealth and children are only a test, but Allah ˹alone˺ has a great reward.
