### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَـَٔامِنُوا۟ بِٱللَّهِ وَرَسُولِهِۦ وَٱلنُّورِ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَنزَلْنَا ۚ وَٱللَّهُ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ خَبِيرٌ</span>

### English

So believe in Allah and His Messenger and in the Light We have revealed. And Allah is All-Aware of what you do.
