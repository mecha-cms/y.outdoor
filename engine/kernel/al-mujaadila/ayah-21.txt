### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">كَتَبَ ٱللَّهُ لَأَغْلِبَنَّ أَنَا۠ وَرُسُلِىٓ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ قَوِىٌّ عَزِيزٌ</span>

### English

Allah has decreed, “I and My messengers will certainly prevail.” Surely Allah is All-Powerful, Almighty.
