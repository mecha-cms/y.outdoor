### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَعَدَّ ٱللَّهُ لَهُمْ عَذَابًا شَدِيدًا ۖ إِنَّهُمْ سَآءَ مَا كَانُوا۟ يَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

Allah has prepared for them a severe punishment. Evil indeed is what they do.
