### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قُل لَّا تُسْـَٔلُونَ عَمَّآ أَجْرَمْنَا وَلَا نُسْـَٔلُ عَمَّا تَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

Say, “You will not be accountable for our misdeeds, nor will we be accountable for your deeds.”
