### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَوْ تَرَىٰٓ إِذْ فَزِعُوا۟ فَلَا فَوْتَ وَأُخِذُوا۟ مِن مَّكَانٍ قَرِيبٍ</span>

### English

If only you could see when they will be horrified with no escape ˹on Judgment Day˺! And they will be seized from a nearby place.
