### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قُلْ جَآءَ ٱلْحَقُّ وَمَا يُبْدِئُ ٱلْبَـٰطِلُ وَمَا يُعِيدُ</span>

### English

Say, “The truth has come, and falsehood will vanish, never to return.”
