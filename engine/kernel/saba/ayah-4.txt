### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لِّيَجْزِىَ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَعَمِلُوا۟ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ ۚ أُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ لَهُم مَّغْفِرَةٌ وَرِزْقٌ كَرِيمٌ</span>

### English

So He may reward those who believe and do good. It is they who will have forgiveness and an honourable provision.
