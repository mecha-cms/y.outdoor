### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَآ أَرْسَلْنَا فِى قَرْيَةٍ مِّن نَّذِيرٍ إِلَّا قَالَ مُتْرَفُوهَآ إِنَّا بِمَآ أُرْسِلْتُم بِهِۦ كَـٰفِرُونَ</span>

### English

Whenever We sent a warner to a society, its elite would say, “We truly reject what you have been sent with.”
