### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قُلْ يَجْمَعُ بَيْنَنَا رَبُّنَا ثُمَّ يَفْتَحُ بَيْنَنَا بِٱلْحَقِّ وَهُوَ ٱلْفَتَّاحُ ٱلْعَلِيمُ</span>

### English

Say, “Our Lord will gather us together, then He will judge between us with the truth. For He is the All-Knowing Judge.”
