### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَقَدْ جَعَلْنَا فِى ٱلسَّمَآءِ بُرُوجًا وَزَيَّنَّـٰهَا لِلنَّـٰظِرِينَ</span>

### English

Indeed, We have placed constellations in the sky, and adorned it for all to see.
