### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَتَيْنَـٰكَ بِٱلْحَقِّ وَإِنَّا لَصَـٰدِقُونَ</span>

### English

We come to you with the truth, and we are certainly truthful.
