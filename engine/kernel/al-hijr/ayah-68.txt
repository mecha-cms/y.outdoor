### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ إِنَّ هَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ ضَيْفِى فَلَا تَفْضَحُونِ</span>

### English

Lot pleaded, “Indeed, these are my guests, so do not embarrass me.
