### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَٱصْدَعْ بِمَا تُؤْمَرُ وَأَعْرِضْ عَنِ ٱلْمُشْرِكِينَ</span>

### English

So proclaim what you have been commanded, and turn away from the polytheists.
