### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ هَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ بَنَاتِىٓ إِن كُنتُمْ فَـٰعِلِينَ</span>

### English

He said, “O my people! Here are my daughters ˹so marry them˺ if you wish to do so.”
