### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقَضَيْنَآ إِلَيْهِ ذَٰلِكَ ٱلْأَمْرَ أَنَّ دَابِرَ هَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ مَقْطُوعٌ مُّصْبِحِينَ</span>

### English

We revealed to him this decree: “Those ˹sinners˺ will be uprooted in the morning.”
