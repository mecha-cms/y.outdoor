### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ لَمْ أَكُن لِّأَسْجُدَ لِبَشَرٍ خَلَقْتَهُۥ مِن صَلْصَـٰلٍ مِّنْ حَمَإٍ مَّسْنُونٍ</span>

### English

He replied, “It is not for me to prostrate to a human You created from sounding clay moulded from black mud.”
