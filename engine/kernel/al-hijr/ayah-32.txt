### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ يَـٰٓإِبْلِيسُ مَا لَكَ أَلَّا تَكُونَ مَعَ ٱلسَّـٰجِدِينَ</span>

### English

Allah asked, “O Iblîs! What is the matter with you that you did not join others in prostration?”
