### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَجَعَلْنَا عَـٰلِيَهَا سَافِلَهَا وَأَمْطَرْنَا عَلَيْهِمْ حِجَارَةً مِّن سِجِّيلٍ</span>

### English

And We turned the cities ˹of Sodom and Gomorrah˺ upside down and rained upon them stones of baked clay.
