### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَعَمْرُكَ إِنَّهُمْ لَفِى سَكْرَتِهِمْ يَعْمَهُونَ</span>

### English

By your life ˹O Prophet˺, they certainly wandered blindly, intoxicated ˹by lust˺.
