### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالُوا۟ بَشَّرْنَـٰكَ بِٱلْحَقِّ فَلَا تَكُن مِّنَ ٱلْقَـٰنِطِينَ</span>

### English

They responded, “We give you good news in all truth, so do not be one of those who despair.”
