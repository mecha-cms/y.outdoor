### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ أَبَشَّرْتُمُونِى عَلَىٰٓ أَن مَّسَّنِىَ ٱلْكِبَرُ فَبِمَ تُبَشِّرُونَ</span>

### English

He wondered, “Do you give me good news despite my old age? What unlikely news!”
