### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱلْأَرْضَ مَدَدْنَـٰهَا وَأَلْقَيْنَا فِيهَا رَوَٰسِىَ وَأَنۢبَتْنَا فِيهَا مِن كُلِّ شَىْءٍ مَّوْزُونٍ</span>

### English

As for the earth, We spread it out and placed upon it firm mountains, and caused everything to grow there in perfect balance.
