### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّا نَحْنُ نَزَّلْنَا ٱلذِّكْرَ وَإِنَّا لَهُۥ لَحَـٰفِظُونَ</span>

### English

It is certainly We Who have revealed the Reminder, and it is certainly We Who will preserve it.
