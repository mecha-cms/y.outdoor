### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَجَعَلْنَا لَكُمْ فِيهَا مَعَـٰيِشَ وَمَن لَّسْتُمْ لَهُۥ بِرَٰزِقِينَ</span>

### English

And We made in it means of sustenance for you and others, who you do not provide for.
