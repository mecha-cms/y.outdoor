### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">مَا نُنَزِّلُ ٱلْمَلَـٰٓئِكَةَ إِلَّا بِٱلْحَقِّ وَمَا كَانُوٓا۟ إِذًا مُّنظَرِينَ</span>

### English

We do not send the angels down except for a just cause, and then ˹the end of˺ the disbelievers will not be delayed.
