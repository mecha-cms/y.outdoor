### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِن مِّن شَىْءٍ إِلَّا عِندَنَا خَزَآئِنُهُۥ وَمَا نُنَزِّلُهُۥٓ إِلَّا بِقَدَرٍ مَّعْلُومٍ</span>

### English

There is not any means ˹of sustenance˺ whose reserves We do not hold, only bringing it forth in precise measure.
