### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱلْجَآنَّ خَلَقْنَـٰهُ مِن قَبْلُ مِن نَّارِ ٱلسَّمُومِ</span>

### English

As for the jinn, We created them earlier from smokeless fire.
