### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَرْسَلْنَا ٱلرِّيَـٰحَ لَوَٰقِحَ فَأَنزَلْنَا مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً فَأَسْقَيْنَـٰكُمُوهُ وَمَآ أَنتُمْ لَهُۥ بِخَـٰزِنِينَ</span>

### English

We send fertilizing winds, and bring down rain from the sky for you to drink. It is not you who hold its reserves.
