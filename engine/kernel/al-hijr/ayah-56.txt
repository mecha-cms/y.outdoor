### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ وَمَن يَقْنَطُ مِن رَّحْمَةِ رَبِّهِۦٓ إِلَّا ٱلضَّآلُّونَ</span>

### English

He exclaimed, “Who would despair of the mercy of their Lord except the misguided?”
