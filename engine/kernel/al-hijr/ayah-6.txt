### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقَالُوا۟ يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِى نُزِّلَ عَلَيْهِ ٱلذِّكْرُ إِنَّكَ لَمَجْنُونٌ</span>

### English

They say, “O you to whom the Reminder is revealed! You must be insane!
