### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَقَدْ كَذَّبَ أَصْحَـٰبُ ٱلْحِجْرِ ٱلْمُرْسَلِينَ</span>

Indeed, the residents of the Stone Valley also denied the messengers.
