### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ رَبِّ فَأَنظِرْنِىٓ إِلَىٰ يَوْمِ يُبْعَثُونَ</span>

### English

Satan appealed, “My Lord! Then delay my end until the Day of their resurrection.”
