### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَنَزَعْنَا مَا فِى صُدُورِهِم مِّنْ غِلٍّ إِخْوَٰنًا عَلَىٰ سُرُرٍ مُّتَقَـٰبِلِينَ</span>

### English

We will remove whatever bitterness they had in their hearts. In a friendly manner, they will be on thrones, facing one another.
