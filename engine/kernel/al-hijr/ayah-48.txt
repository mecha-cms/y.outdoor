### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَا يَمَسُّهُمْ فِيهَا نَصَبٌ وَمَا هُم مِّنْهَا بِمُخْرَجِينَ</span>

### English

No fatigue will touch them there, nor will they ever be asked to leave.
