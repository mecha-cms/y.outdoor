### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَدْ عَلِمْنَا مَا تَنقُصُ ٱلْأَرْضُ مِنْهُمْ ۖ وَعِندَنَا كِتَـٰبٌ حَفِيظٌۢ</span>

### English

We certainly know what the earth consumes of them ˹after their death˺, and with us is a well-preserved Record.
