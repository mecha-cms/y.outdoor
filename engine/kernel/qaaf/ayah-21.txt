### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَجَآءَتْ كُلُّ نَفْسٍ مَّعَهَا سَآئِقٌ وَشَهِيدٌ</span>

### English

Each soul will come forth with an angel to drive it and another to testify.
