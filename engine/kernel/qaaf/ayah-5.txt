### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">بَلْ كَذَّبُوا۟ بِٱلْحَقِّ لَمَّا جَآءَهُمْ فَهُمْ فِىٓ أَمْرٍ مَّرِيجٍ</span>

### English

In fact, they reject the truth when it has come to them, so they are in a confused state.
