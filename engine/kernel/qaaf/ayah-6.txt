### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَفَلَمْ يَنظُرُوٓا۟ إِلَى ٱلسَّمَآءِ فَوْقَهُمْ كَيْفَ بَنَيْنَـٰهَا وَزَيَّنَّـٰهَا وَمَا لَهَا مِن فُرُوجٍ</span>

### English

Have they not then looked at the sky above them: how We built it and adorned it ˹with stars˺, leaving it flawless?
