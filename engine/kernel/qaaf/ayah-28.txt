### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ لَا تَخْتَصِمُوا۟ لَدَىَّ وَقَدْ قَدَّمْتُ إِلَيْكُم بِٱلْوَعِيدِ</span>

### English

Allah will respond, “Do not dispute in My presence, since I had already given you a warning.
