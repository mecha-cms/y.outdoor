### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَجَآءَتْ سَكْرَةُ ٱلْمَوْتِ بِٱلْحَقِّ ۖ ذَٰلِكَ مَا كُنتَ مِنْهُ تَحِيدُ</span>

### English

˹Ultimately,˺ with the throes of death will come the truth. This is what you were trying to escape!
