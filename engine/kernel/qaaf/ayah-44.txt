### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَوْمَ تَشَقَّقُ ٱلْأَرْضُ عَنْهُمْ سِرَاعًا ۚ ذَٰلِكَ حَشْرٌ عَلَيْنَا يَسِيرٌ</span>

### English

˹Beware of˺ the Day the earth will split open, letting them rush forth. That will be an easy gathering for Us.
