### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّ فِى ذَٰلِكَ لَذِكْرَىٰ لِمَن كَانَ لَهُۥ قَلْبٌ أَوْ أَلْقَى ٱلسَّمْعَ وَهُوَ شَهِيدٌ</span>

### English

Surely in this is a reminder for whoever has a ˹mindful˺ heart and lends an attentive ear.
