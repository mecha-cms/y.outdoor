### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّا نَحْنُ نُحْىِۦ وَنُمِيتُ وَإِلَيْنَا ٱلْمَصِيرُ</span>

### English

It is certainly We Who give life and cause death. And to Us is the final return.
