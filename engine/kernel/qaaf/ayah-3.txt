### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَءِذَا مِتْنَا وَكُنَّا تُرَابًا ۖ ذَٰلِكَ رَجْعٌۢ بَعِيدٌ</span>

### English

˹Will we be returned to life,˺ when we are dead and reduced to dust? Such a return is impossible.”
