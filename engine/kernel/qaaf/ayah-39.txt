### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَٱصْبِرْ عَلَىٰ مَا يَقُولُونَ وَسَبِّحْ بِحَمْدِ رَبِّكَ قَبْلَ طُلُوعِ ٱلشَّمْسِ وَقَبْلَ ٱلْغُرُوبِ</span>

### English

So be patient ˹O Prophet˺ with what they say. And glorify the praises of your Lord before sunrise and before sunset.
