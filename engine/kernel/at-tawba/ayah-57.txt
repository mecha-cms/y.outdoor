### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَوْ يَجِدُونَ مَلْجَـًٔا أَوْ مَغَـٰرَٰتٍ أَوْ مُدَّخَلًا لَّوَلَّوْا۟ إِلَيْهِ وَهُمْ يَجْمَحُونَ</span>

### English

If only they could find a refuge, or a cave, or any hiding-place, they would rush headlong towards it.
