### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَعَدَّ ٱللَّهُ لَهُمْ جَنَّـٰتٍ تَجْرِى مِن تَحْتِهَا ٱلْأَنْهَـٰرُ خَـٰلِدِينَ فِيهَا ۚ ذَٰلِكَ ٱلْفَوْزُ ٱلْعَظِيمُ</span>

### English

Allah has prepared for them Gardens under which rivers flow, to stay there forever. That is the ultimate triumph.
