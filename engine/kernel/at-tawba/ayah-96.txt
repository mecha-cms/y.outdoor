### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَحْلِفُونَ لَكُمْ لِتَرْضَوْا۟ عَنْهُمْ ۖ فَإِن تَرْضَوْا۟ عَنْهُمْ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَا يَرْضَىٰ عَنِ ٱلْقَوْمِ ٱلْفَـٰسِقِينَ</span>

### English

They will swear to you in order to please you. And even if you are pleased with them, Allah will never be pleased with the rebellious people.
