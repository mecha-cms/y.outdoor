### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلْيَضْحَكُوا۟ قَلِيلًا وَلْيَبْكُوا۟ كَثِيرًا جَزَآءًۢ بِمَا كَانُوا۟ يَكْسِبُونَ</span>

### English

So let them laugh a little—they will weep much as a reward for what they have committed.
