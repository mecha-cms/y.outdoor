### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَيَحْلِفُونَ بِٱللَّهِ إِنَّهُمْ لَمِنكُمْ وَمَا هُم مِّنكُمْ وَلَـٰكِنَّهُمْ قَوْمٌ يَفْرَقُونَ</span>

### English

They swear by Allah that they are part of you, but they are not. They only say so out of fear.
