### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَا يَرْقُبُونَ فِى مُؤْمِنٍ إِلًّا وَلَا ذِمَّةً ۚ وَأُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْمُعْتَدُونَ</span>

### English

They do not honour the bonds of kinship or treaties with the believers. It is they who are the transgressors.
