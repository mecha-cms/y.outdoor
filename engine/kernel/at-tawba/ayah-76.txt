### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلَمَّآ ءَاتَىٰهُم مِّن فَضْلِهِۦ بَخِلُوا۟ بِهِۦ وَتَوَلَّوا۟ وَّهُم مُّعْرِضُونَ</span>

### English

But when He gave them out of His bounty, they withheld it and turned away indifferently.
