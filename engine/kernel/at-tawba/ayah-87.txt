### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">رَضُوا۟ بِأَن يَكُونُوا۟ مَعَ ٱلْخَوَالِفِ وَطُبِعَ عَلَىٰ قُلُوبِهِمْ فَهُمْ لَا يَفْقَهُونَ</span>

### English

They preferred to stay behind with the helpless, and their hearts have been sealed so they do not comprehend.
