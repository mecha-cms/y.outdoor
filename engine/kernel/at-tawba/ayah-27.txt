### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ثُمَّ يَتُوبُ ٱللَّهُ مِنۢ بَعْدِ ذَٰلِكَ عَلَىٰ مَن يَشَآءُ ۗ وَٱللَّهُ غَفُورٌ رَّحِيمٌ</span>

### English

Then afterwards Allah will turn in grace to whoever He wills. And Allah is All-Forgiving, Most Merciful.
