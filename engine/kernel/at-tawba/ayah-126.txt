### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَوَلَا يَرَوْنَ أَنَّهُمْ يُفْتَنُونَ فِى كُلِّ عَامٍ مَّرَّةً أَوْ مَرَّتَيْنِ ثُمَّ لَا يَتُوبُونَ وَلَا هُمْ يَذَّكَّرُونَ</span>

### English

Do they not see that they are tried once or twice every year? Yet they neither repent nor do they learn a lesson.
