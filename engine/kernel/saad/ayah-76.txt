### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ أَنَا۠ خَيْرٌ مِّنْهُ ۖ خَلَقْتَنِى مِن نَّارٍ وَخَلَقْتَهُۥ مِن طِينٍ</span>

### English

He replied, “I am better than he is: You created me from fire and him from clay.”
