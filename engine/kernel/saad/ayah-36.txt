### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَسَخَّرْنَا لَهُ ٱلرِّيحَ تَجْرِى بِأَمْرِهِۦ رُخَآءً حَيْثُ أَصَابَ</span>

### English

So We subjected to him the wind, blowing gently at his command to wherever he pleased.
