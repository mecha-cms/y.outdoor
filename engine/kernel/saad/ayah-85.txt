### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَأَمْلَأَنَّ جَهَنَّمَ مِنكَ وَمِمَّن تَبِعَكَ مِنْهُمْ أَجْمَعِينَ</span>

### English

I will surely fill up Hell with you and whoever follows you from among them, all together.”
