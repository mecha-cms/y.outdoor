### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّا سَخَّرْنَا ٱلْجِبَالَ مَعَهُۥ يُسَبِّحْنَ بِٱلْعَشِىِّ وَٱلْإِشْرَاقِ</span>

### English

We truly subjected the mountains to hymn ˹Our praises˺ along with him in the evening and after sunrise.
