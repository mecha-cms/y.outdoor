### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">مَا سَمِعْنَا بِهَـٰذَا فِى ٱلْمِلَّةِ ٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ إِنْ هَـٰذَآ إِلَّا ٱخْتِلَـٰقٌ</span>

### English

We have never heard of this in the previous faith. This is nothing but a fabrication.
