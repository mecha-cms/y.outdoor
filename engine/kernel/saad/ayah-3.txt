### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">كَمْ أَهْلَكْنَا مِن قَبْلِهِم مِّن قَرْنٍ فَنَادَوا۟ وَّلَاتَ حِينَ مَنَاصٍ</span>

### English

˹Imagine˺ how many peoples We destroyed before them, and they cried out when it was too late to escape.
