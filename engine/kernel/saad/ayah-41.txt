### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱذْكُرْ عَبْدَنَآ أَيُّوبَ إِذْ نَادَىٰ رَبَّهُۥٓ أَنِّى مَسَّنِىَ ٱلشَّيْطَـٰنُ بِنُصْبٍ وَعَذَابٍ</span>

### English

And remember Our servant Job, when he cried out to his Lord, “Satan has afflicted me with distress and suffering.”
