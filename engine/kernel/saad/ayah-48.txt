### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱذْكُرْ إِسْمَـٰعِيلَ وَٱلْيَسَعَ وَذَا ٱلْكِفْلِ ۖ وَكُلٌّ مِّنَ ٱلْأَخْيَارِ</span>

### English

Also remember Ishmael, Elisha, and Ⱬul-Kifl. All are among the best.
