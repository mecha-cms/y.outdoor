### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَمْ نَجْعَلُ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَعَمِلُوا۟ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ كَٱلْمُفْسِدِينَ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ أَمْ نَجْعَلُ ٱلْمُتَّقِينَ كَٱلْفُجَّارِ</span>

### English

Or should We treat those who believe and do good like those who make mischief throughout the land? Or should We treat the righteous like the wicked?
