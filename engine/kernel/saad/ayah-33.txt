### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">رُدُّوهَا عَلَىَّ ۖ فَطَفِقَ مَسْحًۢا بِٱلسُّوقِ وَٱلْأَعْنَاقِ</span>

### English

˹He ordered,˺ “Bring them back to me!” Then he began to rub down their legs and necks.
