### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱذْكُرْ عِبَـٰدَنَآ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ وَإِسْحَـٰقَ وَيَعْقُوبَ أُو۟لِى ٱلْأَيْدِى وَٱلْأَبْصَـٰرِ</span>

### English

And remember Our servants: Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob—the men of strength and insight.
