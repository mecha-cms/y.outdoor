### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَشَدَدْنَا مُلْكَهُۥ وَءَاتَيْنَـٰهُ ٱلْحِكْمَةَ وَفَصْلَ ٱلْخِطَابِ</span>

### English

We strengthened his kingship, and gave him wisdom and sound judgment.
