### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِذْ قَالَ رَبُّكَ لِلْمَلَـٰٓئِكَةِ إِنِّى خَـٰلِقٌۢ بَشَرًا مِّن طِينٍ</span>

### English

˹Remember, O  Prophet˺ when your Lord said to the angels, “I am going to create a human being from clay.
