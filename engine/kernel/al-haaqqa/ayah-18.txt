### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَوْمَئِذٍ تُعْرَضُونَ لَا تَخْفَىٰ مِنكُمْ خَافِيَةٌ</span>

### English

You will then be presented ˹before Him for judgment˺, and none of your secrets will stay hidden.
