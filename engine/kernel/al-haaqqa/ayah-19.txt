### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَمَّا مَنْ أُوتِىَ كِتَـٰبَهُۥ بِيَمِينِهِۦ فَيَقُولُ هَآؤُمُ ٱقْرَءُوا۟ كِتَـٰبِيَهْ</span>

### English

As for those given their records in their right hand, they will cry ˹happily˺, “Here ˹everyone˺! Read my record!
