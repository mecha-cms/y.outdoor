### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا بِقَوْلِ كَاهِنٍ ۚ قَلِيلًا مَّا تَذَكَّرُونَ</span>

### English

Nor is it the mumbling of a fortune-teller, ˹yet˺ you are hardly mindful.
