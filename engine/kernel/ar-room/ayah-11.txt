### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱللَّهُ يَبْدَؤُا۟ ٱلْخَلْقَ ثُمَّ يُعِيدُهُۥ ثُمَّ إِلَيْهِ تُرْجَعُونَ</span>

### English

It is Allah Who originates the creation, and will resurrect it. And then to Him you will ˹all˺ be returned.
