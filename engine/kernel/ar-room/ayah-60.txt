### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَٱصْبِرْ إِنَّ وَعْدَ ٱللَّهِ حَقٌّ ۖ وَلَا يَسْتَخِفَّنَّكَ ٱلَّذِينَ لَا يُوقِنُونَ</span>

### English

So be patient, for the promise of Allah certainly is true. And do not be disturbed by those who have no sure faith.
