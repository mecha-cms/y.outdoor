### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَمْ أَنزَلْنَا عَلَيْهِمْ سُلْطَـٰنًا فَهُوَ يَتَكَلَّمُ بِمَا كَانُوا۟ بِهِۦ يُشْرِكُونَ</span>

### English

Or have We sent down to them an authority which attests to what they associate ˹with Him˺?
