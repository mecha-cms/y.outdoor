### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">كَذَٰلِكَ يَطْبَعُ ٱللَّهُ عَلَىٰ قُلُوبِ ٱلَّذِينَ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ</span>

### English

This is how Allah seals the hearts of those unwilling to know ˹the truth˺.
