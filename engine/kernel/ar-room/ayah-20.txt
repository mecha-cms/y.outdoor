### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمِنْ ءَايَـٰتِهِۦٓ أَنْ خَلَقَكُم مِّن تُرَابٍ ثُمَّ إِذَآ أَنتُم بَشَرٌ تَنتَشِرُونَ</span>

### English

One of His signs is that He created you from dust, then—behold!—you are human beings spreading over ˹the earth˺.
