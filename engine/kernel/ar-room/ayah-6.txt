### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَعْدَ ٱللَّهِ ۖ لَا يُخْلِفُ ٱللَّهُ وَعْدَهُۥ وَلَـٰكِنَّ أَكْثَرَ ٱلنَّاسِ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ</span>

### English

˹This is˺ the promise of Allah. ˹And˺ Allah never fails in His promise. But most people do not know.
