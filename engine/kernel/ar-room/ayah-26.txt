### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَهُۥ مَن فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ۖ كُلٌّ لَّهُۥ قَـٰنِتُونَ</span>

### English

And to Him belong all those in the heavens and the earth—all are subject to His Will.
