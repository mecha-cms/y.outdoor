### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَمَّا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَعَمِلُوا۟ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ فَهُمْ فِى رَوْضَةٍ يُحْبَرُونَ</span>

### English

As for those who believed and did good, they will be rejoicing in a Garden.
