### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَمْ يَكُن لَّهُم مِّن شُرَكَآئِهِمْ شُفَعَـٰٓؤُا۟ وَكَانُوا۟ بِشُرَكَآئِهِمْ كَـٰفِرِينَ</span>

### English

There will be no intercessors for them from among their associate-gods, and they will ˹totally˺ deny their associate-gods.
