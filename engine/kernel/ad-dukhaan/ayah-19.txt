### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَن لَّا تَعْلُوا۟ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ ۖ إِنِّىٓ ءَاتِيكُم بِسُلْطَـٰنٍ مُّبِينٍ</span>

### English

And do not be arrogant with Allah. I have certainly come to you with a compelling proof.
