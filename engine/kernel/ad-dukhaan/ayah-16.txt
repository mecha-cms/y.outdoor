### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَوْمَ نَبْطِشُ ٱلْبَطْشَةَ ٱلْكُبْرَىٰٓ إِنَّا مُنتَقِمُونَ</span>

### English

˹Then˺ on the Day We will deal ˹you˺ the fiercest blow, We will surely inflict punishment.
