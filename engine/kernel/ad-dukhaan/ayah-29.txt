### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَمَا بَكَتْ عَلَيْهِمُ ٱلسَّمَآءُ وَٱلْأَرْضُ وَمَا كَانُوا۟ مُنظَرِينَ</span>

### English

Neither heaven nor earth wept over them, nor was their fate delayed.
