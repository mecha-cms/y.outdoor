### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَنْ أَدُّوٓا۟ إِلَىَّ عِبَادَ ٱللَّهِ ۖ إِنِّى لَكُمْ رَسُولٌ أَمِينٌ</span>

### English

˹proclaiming,˺ “Hand over the servants of Allah to me. I am truly a trustworthy messenger to you.
