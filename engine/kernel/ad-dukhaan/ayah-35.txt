### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنْ هِىَ إِلَّا مَوْتَتُنَا ٱلْأُولَىٰ وَمَا نَحْنُ بِمُنشَرِينَ</span>

### English

“There is nothing beyond our first death, and we will never be resurrected.
