### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَن لَّمْ يُؤْمِنۢ بِٱللَّهِ وَرَسُولِهِۦ فَإِنَّآ أَعْتَدْنَا لِلْكَـٰفِرِينَ سَعِيرًا</span>

### English

And whoever does not believe in Allah and His Messenger, then We surely have prepared for the disbelievers a blazing Fire.
