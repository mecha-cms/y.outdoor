### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَوْ قَـٰتَلَكُمُ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ لَوَلَّوُا۟ ٱلْأَدْبَـٰرَ ثُمَّ لَا يَجِدُونَ وَلِيًّا وَلَا نَصِيرًا</span>

### English

If the disbelievers were to fight you, they would certainly flee. Then they would never find any protector or helper.
