### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">سُنَّةَ ٱللَّهِ ٱلَّتِى قَدْ خَلَتْ مِن قَبْلُ ۖ وَلَن تَجِدَ لِسُنَّةِ ٱللَّهِ تَبْدِيلًا</span>

### English

˹This is˺ Allah’s way, already long established ˹in the past˺. And you will find no change in Allah’s way.
