### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَقِيمُوا۟ ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ وَءَاتُوا۟ ٱلزَّكَوٰةَ وَأَطِيعُوا۟ ٱلرَّسُولَ لَعَلَّكُمْ تُرْحَمُونَ</span>

### English

Moreover, establish prayer, pay alms-tax, and obey the Messenger, so you may be shown mercy.
