### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِن يَكُن لَّهُمُ ٱلْحَقُّ يَأْتُوٓا۟ إِلَيْهِ مُذْعِنِينَ</span>

### English

But if the truth is in their favour, they come to him, fully submitting.
