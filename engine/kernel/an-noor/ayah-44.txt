### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يُقَلِّبُ ٱللَّهُ ٱلَّيْلَ وَٱلنَّهَارَ ۚ إِنَّ فِى ذَٰلِكَ لَعِبْرَةً لِّأُو۟لِى ٱلْأَبْصَـٰرِ</span>

### English

Allah alternates the day and night. Surely in this is a lesson for people of insight.
