### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِذَا دُعُوٓا۟ إِلَى ٱللَّهِ وَرَسُولِهِۦ لِيَحْكُمَ بَيْنَهُمْ إِذَا فَرِيقٌ مِّنْهُم مُّعْرِضُونَ</span>

### English

And as soon as they are called to Allah and His Messenger so he may judge between them, a group of them turns away.
