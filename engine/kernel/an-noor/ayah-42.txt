### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلِلَّهِ مُلْكُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ۖ وَإِلَى ٱللَّهِ ٱلْمَصِيرُ</span>

### English

To Allah ˹alone˺ belongs the kingdom of the heavens and the earth. And to Allah is the final return.
