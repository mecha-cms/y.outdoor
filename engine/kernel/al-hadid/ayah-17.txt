### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱعْلَمُوٓا۟ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ يُحْىِ ٱلْأَرْضَ بَعْدَ مَوْتِهَا ۚ قَدْ بَيَّنَّا لَكُمُ ٱلْـَٔايَـٰتِ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَعْقِلُونَ</span>

### English

Know that Allah revives the earth after its death. We have certainly made the signs clear for you so perhaps you will understand.
