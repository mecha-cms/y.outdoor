### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">سَبَّحَ لِلَّهِ مَا فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ۖ وَهُوَ ٱلْعَزِيزُ ٱلْحَكِيمُ</span>

### English

Whatever is in the heavens and the earth glorifies Allah, for He is the Almighty, All-Wise.
