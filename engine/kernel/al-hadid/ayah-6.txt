### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يُولِجُ ٱلَّيْلَ فِى ٱلنَّهَارِ وَيُولِجُ ٱلنَّهَارَ فِى ٱلَّيْلِ ۚ وَهُوَ عَلِيمٌۢ بِذَاتِ ٱلصُّدُورِ</span>

### English

He merges the night into day and the day into night. And He knows best what is ˹hidden˺ in the heart.
