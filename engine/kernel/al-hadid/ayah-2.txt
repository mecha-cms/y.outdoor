### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَهُۥ مُلْكُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ۖ يُحْىِۦ وَيُمِيتُ ۖ وَهُوَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَىْءٍ قَدِيرٌ</span>

### English

To Him belongs the kingdom of the heavens and the earth. He gives life and causes death. And He is Most Capable of everything.
