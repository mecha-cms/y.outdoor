### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَّهُۥ مُلْكُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ۚ وَإِلَى ٱللَّهِ تُرْجَعُ ٱلْأُمُورُ</span>

### English

To Him belongs the kingdom of the heavens and the earth. And to Allah all matters are returned.
