### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقَالَتْ لِأُخْتِهِۦ قُصِّيهِ ۖ فَبَصُرَتْ بِهِۦ عَن جُنُبٍ وَهُمْ لَا يَشْعُرُونَ</span>

### English

And she said to his sister, “Keep track of him!” So she watched him from a distance, while they were unaware.
