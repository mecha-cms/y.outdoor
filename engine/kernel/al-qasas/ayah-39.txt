### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱسْتَكْبَرَ هُوَ وَجُنُودُهُۥ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ بِغَيْرِ ٱلْحَقِّ وَظَنُّوٓا۟ أَنَّهُمْ إِلَيْنَا لَا يُرْجَعُونَ</span>

### English

And so he and his soldiers behaved arrogantly in the land with no right, thinking they would never be returned to Us.
