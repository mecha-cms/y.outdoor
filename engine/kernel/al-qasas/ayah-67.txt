### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَمَّا مَن تَابَ وَءَامَنَ وَعَمِلَ صَـٰلِحًا فَعَسَىٰٓ أَن يَكُونَ مِنَ ٱلْمُفْلِحِينَ</span>

### English

As for those who repent, believe, and do good ˹in this world˺, it is right to hope that they will be among the successful.
