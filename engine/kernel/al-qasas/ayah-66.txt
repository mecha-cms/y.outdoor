### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَعَمِيَتْ عَلَيْهِمُ ٱلْأَنۢبَآءُ يَوْمَئِذٍ فَهُمْ لَا يَتَسَآءَلُونَ</span>

### English

They will be too dumbstruck on that Day to ask one another ˹for answers˺.
