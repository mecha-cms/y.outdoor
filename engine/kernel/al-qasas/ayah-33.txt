### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ رَبِّ إِنِّى قَتَلْتُ مِنْهُمْ نَفْسًا فَأَخَافُ أَن يَقْتُلُونِ</span>

### English

Moses appealed, “My Lord! I have indeed killed a man from them, so I fear they may kill me.
