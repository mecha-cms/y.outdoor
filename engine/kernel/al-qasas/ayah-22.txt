### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَمَّا تَوَجَّهَ تِلْقَآءَ مَدْيَنَ قَالَ عَسَىٰ رَبِّىٓ أَن يَهْدِيَنِى سَوَآءَ ٱلسَّبِيلِ</span>

### English

And as he made his way towards Midian, he said, “I trust my Lord will guide me to the right way.”
