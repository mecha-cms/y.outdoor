### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَخَسَفْنَا بِهِۦ وَبِدَارِهِ ٱلْأَرْضَ فَمَا كَانَ لَهُۥ مِن فِئَةٍ يَنصُرُونَهُۥ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ وَمَا كَانَ مِنَ ٱلْمُنتَصِرِينَ</span>

### English

Then We caused the earth to swallow him up, along with his home. There was no one to help him against Allah, nor could he even help himself.
