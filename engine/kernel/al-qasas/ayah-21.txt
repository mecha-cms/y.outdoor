### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَخَرَجَ مِنْهَا خَآئِفًا يَتَرَقَّبُ ۖ قَالَ رَبِّ نَجِّنِى مِنَ ٱلْقَوْمِ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ</span>

### English

So Moses left the city in a state of fear and caution, praying, “My Lord! Deliver me from the wrongdoing people.”
