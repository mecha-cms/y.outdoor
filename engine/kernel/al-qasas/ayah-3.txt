### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">نَتْلُوا۟ عَلَيْكَ مِن نَّبَإِ مُوسَىٰ وَفِرْعَوْنَ بِٱلْحَقِّ لِقَوْمٍ يُؤْمِنُونَ</span>

### English

We narrate to you ˹O Prophet˺ part of the story of Moses and Pharaoh in truth for people who believe.
