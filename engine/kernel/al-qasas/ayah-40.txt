### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَخَذْنَـٰهُ وَجُنُودَهُۥ فَنَبَذْنَـٰهُمْ فِى ٱلْيَمِّ ۖ فَٱنظُرْ كَيْفَ كَانَ عَـٰقِبَةُ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ</span>

### English

So We seized him and his soldiers, casting them into the sea. See then what was the end of the wrongdoers!
