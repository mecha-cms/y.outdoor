### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَمَّا بَلَغَ أَشُدَّهُۥ وَٱسْتَوَىٰٓ ءَاتَيْنَـٰهُ حُكْمًا وَعِلْمًا ۚ وَكَذَٰلِكَ نَجْزِى ٱلْمُحْسِنِينَ</span>

### English

And when he reached full strength and maturity, We gave him wisdom and knowledge. This is how We reward the good-doers.
