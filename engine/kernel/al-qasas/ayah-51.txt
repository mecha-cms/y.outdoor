### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ وَلَقَدْ وَصَّلْنَا لَهُمُ ٱلْقَوْلَ لَعَلَّهُمْ يَتَذَكَّرُونَ</span>

### English

Indeed, We have steadily delivered the Word ˹of Allah˺ to the people so they may be mindful.
