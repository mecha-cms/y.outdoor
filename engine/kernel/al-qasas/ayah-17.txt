### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ رَبِّ بِمَآ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَىَّ فَلَنْ أَكُونَ ظَهِيرًا لِّلْمُجْرِمِينَ</span>

### English

Moses pledged, “My Lord! For all Your favours upon me, I will never side with the wicked.”
