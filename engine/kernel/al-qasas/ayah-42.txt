### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَتْبَعْنَـٰهُمْ فِى هَـٰذِهِ ٱلدُّنْيَا لَعْنَةً ۖ وَيَوْمَ ٱلْقِيَـٰمَةِ هُم مِّنَ ٱلْمَقْبُوحِينَ</span>

### English

We caused a curse to follow them in this world. And on the Day of Judgment they will be among the outcasts.
