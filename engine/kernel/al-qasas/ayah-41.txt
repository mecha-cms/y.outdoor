### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَجَعَلْنَـٰهُمْ أَئِمَّةً يَدْعُونَ إِلَى ٱلنَّارِ ۖ وَيَوْمَ ٱلْقِيَـٰمَةِ لَا يُنصَرُونَ</span>

### English

We made them leaders inviting ˹others˺ to the Fire. And on the Day of Judgment they will not be helped.
