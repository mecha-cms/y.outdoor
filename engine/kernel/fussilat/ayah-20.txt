### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَا مَا جَآءُوهَا شَهِدَ عَلَيْهِمْ سَمْعُهُمْ وَأَبْصَـٰرُهُمْ وَجُلُودُهُم بِمَا كَانُوا۟ يَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

When they reach it, their ears, eyes, and skin will testify against what they used to do.
