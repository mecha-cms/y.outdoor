### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَلَآ إِنَّهُمْ فِى مِرْيَةٍ مِّن لِّقَآءِ رَبِّهِمْ ۗ أَلَآ إِنَّهُۥ بِكُلِّ شَىْءٍ مُّحِيطٌۢ</span>

### English

They are truly in doubt of the meeting with their Lord! ˹But˺ He is indeed Fully Aware of everything.
