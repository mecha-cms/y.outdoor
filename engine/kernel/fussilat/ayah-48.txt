### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَضَلَّ عَنْهُم مَّا كَانُوا۟ يَدْعُونَ مِن قَبْلُ ۖ وَظَنُّوا۟ مَا لَهُم مِّن مَّحِيصٍ</span>

### English

Whatever ˹idols˺ they used to invoke besides Allah will fail them. And they will realize that they will have no escape.
