### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلَنُذِيقَنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ عَذَابًا شَدِيدًا وَلَنَجْزِيَنَّهُمْ أَسْوَأَ ٱلَّذِى كَانُوا۟ يَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

So We will certainly make the disbelievers taste a severe punishment, and We will surely repay them according to the worst of their deeds.
