### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا يُلَقَّىٰهَآ إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ صَبَرُوا۟ وَمَا يُلَقَّىٰهَآ إِلَّا ذُو حَظٍّ عَظِيمٍ</span>

### English

But this cannot be attained except by those who are patient and who are truly fortunate.
