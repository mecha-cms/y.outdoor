### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَّا يَسْـَٔمُ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنُ مِن دُعَآءِ ٱلْخَيْرِ وَإِن مَّسَّهُ ٱلشَّرُّ فَيَـُٔوسٌ قَنُوطٌ</span>

### English

One never tires of praying for good. And if touched with evil, they become desperate and hopeless.
