### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَذَٰلِكُمْ ظَنُّكُمُ ٱلَّذِى ظَنَنتُم بِرَبِّكُمْ أَرْدَىٰكُمْ فَأَصْبَحْتُم مِّنَ ٱلْخَـٰسِرِينَ</span>

### English

It was that ˹false˺ assumption you entertained about your Lord that has brought about your doom, so you have become losers.”
