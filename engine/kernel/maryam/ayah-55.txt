### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَكَانَ يَأْمُرُ أَهْلَهُۥ بِٱلصَّلَوٰةِ وَٱلزَّكَوٰةِ وَكَانَ عِندَ رَبِّهِۦ مَرْضِيًّا</span>

### English

He used to urge his people to pray and give alms-tax. And his Lord was well pleased with him.
