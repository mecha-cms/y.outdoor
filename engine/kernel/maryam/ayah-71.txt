### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِن مِّنكُمْ إِلَّا وَارِدُهَا ۚ كَانَ عَلَىٰ رَبِّكَ حَتْمًا مَّقْضِيًّا</span>

### English

There is none of you who will not pass over it. ˹This is˺ a decree your Lord must fulfil.
