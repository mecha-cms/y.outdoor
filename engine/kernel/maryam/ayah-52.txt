### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَنَـٰدَيْنَـٰهُ مِن جَانِبِ ٱلطُّورِ ٱلْأَيْمَنِ وَقَرَّبْنَـٰهُ نَجِيًّا</span>

### English

We called him from the right side of Mount Ṭûr, and drew him near, speaking ˹with him˺ directly.
