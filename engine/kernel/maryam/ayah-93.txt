### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِن كُلُّ مَن فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ إِلَّآ ءَاتِى ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ عَبْدًا</span>

### English

There is none in the heavens or the earth who will not return to the Most Compassionate in full submission.
