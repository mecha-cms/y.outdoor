### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَهُزِّىٓ إِلَيْكِ بِجِذْعِ ٱلنَّخْلَةِ تُسَـٰقِطْ عَلَيْكِ رُطَبًا جَنِيًّا</span>

### English

And shake the trunk of this palm tree towards you, it will drop fresh, ripe dates upon you.
