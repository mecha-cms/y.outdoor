### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱلسَّلَـٰمُ عَلَىَّ يَوْمَ وُلِدتُّ وَيَوْمَ أَمُوتُ وَيَوْمَ أُبْعَثُ حَيًّا</span>

### English

Peace be upon me the day I was born, the day I die, and the day I will be raised back to life!”
