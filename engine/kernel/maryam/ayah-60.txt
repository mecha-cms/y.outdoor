### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِلَّا مَن تَابَ وَءَامَنَ وَعَمِلَ صَـٰلِحًا فَأُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ يَدْخُلُونَ ٱلْجَنَّةَ وَلَا يُظْلَمُونَ شَيْـًٔا</span>

### English

As for those who repent, believe, and do good, it is they who will be admitted into Paradise, never being denied any reward.
