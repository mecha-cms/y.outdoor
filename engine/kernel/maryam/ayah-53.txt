### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَوَهَبْنَا لَهُۥ مِن رَّحْمَتِنَآ أَخَاهُ هَـٰرُونَ نَبِيًّا</span>

### English

And We appointed for him—out of Our grace—his brother, Aaron, as a prophet.
