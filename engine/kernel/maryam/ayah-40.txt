### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّا نَحْنُ نَرِثُ ٱلْأَرْضَ وَمَنْ عَلَيْهَا وَإِلَيْنَا يُرْجَعُونَ</span>

### English

Indeed, it is We Who will succeed the earth and whoever is on it. And to Us they will ˹all˺ be returned.
