### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَخَرَجَ عَلَىٰ قَوْمِهِۦ مِنَ ٱلْمِحْرَابِ فَأَوْحَىٰٓ إِلَيْهِمْ أَن سَبِّحُوا۟ بُكْرَةً وَعَشِيًّا</span>

### English

So he came out to his people from the sanctuary, signalling to them to glorify ˹Allah˺ morning and evening.
