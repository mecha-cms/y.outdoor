### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَكَمْ أَهْلَكْنَا قَبْلَهُم مِّن قَرْنٍ هُمْ أَحْسَنُ أَثَـٰثًا وَرِءْيًا</span>

### English

˹Imagine, O  Prophet˺ how many peoples We have destroyed before them, who were far better in luxury and splendour!
