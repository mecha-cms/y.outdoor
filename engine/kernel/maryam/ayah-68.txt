### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَوَرَبِّكَ لَنَحْشُرَنَّهُمْ وَٱلشَّيَـٰطِينَ ثُمَّ لَنُحْضِرَنَّهُمْ حَوْلَ جَهَنَّمَ جِثِيًّا</span>

### English

By your Lord ˹O Prophet˺! We will surely gather them along with the devils, and then set them around Hell on their knees.
