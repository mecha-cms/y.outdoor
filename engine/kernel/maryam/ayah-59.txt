### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ فَخَلَفَ مِنۢ بَعْدِهِمْ خَلْفٌ أَضَاعُوا۟ ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ وَٱتَّبَعُوا۟ ٱلشَّهَوَٰتِ ۖ فَسَوْفَ يَلْقَوْنَ غَيًّا</span>

### English

But they were succeeded by generations who neglected prayer and followed their lusts and so will soon face the evil consequences.
