### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَتْ أَنَّىٰ يَكُونُ لِى غُلَـٰمٌ وَلَمْ يَمْسَسْنِى بَشَرٌ وَلَمْ أَكُ بَغِيًّا</span>

### English

She wondered, “How can I have a son when no man has ever touched me, nor am I unchaste?”
