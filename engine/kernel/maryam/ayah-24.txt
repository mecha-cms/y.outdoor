### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَنَادَىٰهَا مِن تَحْتِهَآ أَلَّا تَحْزَنِى قَدْ جَعَلَ رَبُّكِ تَحْتَكِ سَرِيًّا</span>

### English

So a voice reassured her from below her, “Do not grieve! Your Lord has provided a stream at your feet.
