### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">تِلْكَ ٱلْجَنَّةُ ٱلَّتِى نُورِثُ مِنْ عِبَادِنَا مَن كَانَ تَقِيًّا</span>

### English

That is Paradise, which We will grant to whoever is devout among Our servants.
