### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَسَلَـٰمٌ عَلَيْهِ يَوْمَ وُلِدَ وَيَوْمَ يَمُوتُ وَيَوْمَ يُبْعَثُ حَيًّا</span>

### English

Peace be upon him the day he was born, and the day of his death, and the day he will be raised back to life!
