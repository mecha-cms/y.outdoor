### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَشَارَتْ إِلَيْهِ ۖ قَالُوا۟ كَيْفَ نُكَلِّمُ مَن كَانَ فِى ٱلْمَهْدِ صَبِيًّا</span>

### English

So she pointed to the baby. They exclaimed, “How can we talk to someone who is an infant in the cradle?”
