### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَطَّلَعَ ٱلْغَيْبَ أَمِ ٱتَّخَذَ عِندَ ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ عَهْدًا</span>

### English

Has he looked into the unseen or taken a pledge from the Most Compassionate?
