### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ثُمَّ لَنَنزِعَنَّ مِن كُلِّ شِيعَةٍ أَيُّهُمْ أَشَدُّ عَلَى ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ عِتِيًّا</span>

### English

Then We will certainly begin by dragging out of every group the ones most defiant to the Most Compassionate.
