### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَّا يَمْلِكُونَ ٱلشَّفَـٰعَةَ إِلَّا مَنِ ٱتَّخَذَ عِندَ ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ عَهْدًا</span>

### English

None will have the right to intercede, except those who have taken a covenant from the Most Compassionate.
