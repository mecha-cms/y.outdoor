### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَّا يَسْمَعُونَ فِيهَا لَغْوًا إِلَّا سَلَـٰمًا ۖ وَلَهُمْ رِزْقُهُمْ فِيهَا بُكْرَةً وَعَشِيًّا</span>

### English

There they will never hear any idle talk—only ˹greetings of˺ peace. And there they will have their provisions morning and evening.
