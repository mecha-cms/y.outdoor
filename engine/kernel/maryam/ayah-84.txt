### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلَا تَعْجَلْ عَلَيْهِمْ ۖ إِنَّمَا نَعُدُّ لَهُمْ عَدًّا</span>

### English

So do not be in haste against them, for indeed We are ˹closely˺ counting down their days.
