### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ثُمَّ نُنَجِّى ٱلَّذِينَ ٱتَّقَوا۟ وَّنَذَرُ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ فِيهَا جِثِيًّا</span>

### English

Then We will deliver those who were devout, leaving the wrongdoers there on their knees.
