### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَسْمِعْ بِهِمْ وَأَبْصِرْ يَوْمَ يَأْتُونَنَا ۖ لَـٰكِنِ ٱلظَّـٰلِمُونَ ٱلْيَوْمَ فِى ضَلَـٰلٍ مُّبِينٍ</span>

### English

How clearly will they hear and see on the Day they will come to Us! But today the wrongdoers are clearly astray.
