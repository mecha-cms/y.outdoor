### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَوَهَبْنَا لَهُم مِّن رَّحْمَتِنَا وَجَعَلْنَا لَهُمْ لِسَانَ صِدْقٍ عَلِيًّا</span>

### English

We showered them with Our mercy, and blessed them with honourable mention.
