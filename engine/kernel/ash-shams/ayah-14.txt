### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَكَذَّبُوهُ فَعَقَرُوهَا فَدَمْدَمَ عَلَيْهِمْ رَبُّهُم بِذَنۢبِهِمْ فَسَوَّىٰهَا</span>

### English

Still they defied him and slaughtered her. So their Lord crushed them for their crime, levelling all to the ground.
