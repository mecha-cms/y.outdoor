### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَقَدْ أَنزَلْنَآ إِلَيْكُمْ كِتَـٰبًا فِيهِ ذِكْرُكُمْ ۖ أَفَلَا تَعْقِلُونَ</span>

### English

We have surely revealed to you a Book, in which there is glory for you. Will you not then understand?
