### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِذْ قَالَ لِأَبِيهِ وَقَوْمِهِۦ مَا هَـٰذِهِ ٱلتَّمَاثِيلُ ٱلَّتِىٓ أَنتُمْ لَهَا عَـٰكِفُونَ</span>

### English

˹Remember˺ when he questioned his father and his people, “What are these statues to which you are so devoted?”
