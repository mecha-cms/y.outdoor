### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَهُمْ فِيهَا زَفِيرٌ وَهُمْ فِيهَا لَا يَسْمَعُونَ</span>

### English

In it they will groan, and will not be able to hear.
