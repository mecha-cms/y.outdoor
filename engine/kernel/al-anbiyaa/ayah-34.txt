### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا جَعَلْنَا لِبَشَرٍ مِّن قَبْلِكَ ٱلْخُلْدَ ۖ أَفَإِي۟ن مِّتَّ فَهُمُ ٱلْخَـٰلِدُونَ</span>

### English

We have not granted immortality to any human before you ˹O Prophet˺: so if you die, will they live forever?
