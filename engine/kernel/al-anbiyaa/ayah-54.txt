### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ لَقَدْ كُنتُمْ أَنتُمْ وَءَابَآؤُكُمْ فِى ضَلَـٰلٍ مُّبِينٍ</span>

### English

He responded, “Indeed, you and your forefathers have been clearly astray.”
