### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أُفٍّ لَّكُمْ وَلِمَا تَعْبُدُونَ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ ۖ أَفَلَا تَعْقِلُونَ</span>

### English

Shame on you and whatever you worship instead of Allah! Do you not have any sense?”
