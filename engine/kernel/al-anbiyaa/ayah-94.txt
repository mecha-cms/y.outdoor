### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَمَن يَعْمَلْ مِنَ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ وَهُوَ مُؤْمِنٌ فَلَا كُفْرَانَ لِسَعْيِهِۦ وَإِنَّا لَهُۥ كَـٰتِبُونَ</span>

### English

So whoever does good and is a believer will never be denied ˹the reward for˺ their striving, for We are recording it all.
