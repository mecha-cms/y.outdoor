### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّهُۥ يَعْلَمُ ٱلْجَهْرَ مِنَ ٱلْقَوْلِ وَيَعْلَمُ مَا تَكْتُمُونَ</span>

### English

Allah surely knows what you say openly and whatever you hide.
