### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَٱسْتَجَبْنَا لَهُۥ وَنَجَّيْنَـٰهُ مِنَ ٱلْغَمِّ ۚ وَكَذَٰلِكَ نُـۨجِى ٱلْمُؤْمِنِينَ</span>

### English

So We answered his prayer and rescued him from anguish. And so do We save the ˹true˺ believers.
