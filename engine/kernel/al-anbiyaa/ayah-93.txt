### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَتَقَطَّعُوٓا۟ أَمْرَهُم بَيْنَهُمْ ۖ كُلٌّ إِلَيْنَا رَٰجِعُونَ</span>

### English

Yet the people have divided it into sects. But to Us they will all return.
