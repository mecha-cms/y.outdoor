### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَعَلَّمْنَـٰهُ صَنْعَةَ لَبُوسٍ لَّكُمْ لِتُحْصِنَكُم مِّنۢ بَأْسِكُمْ ۖ فَهَلْ أَنتُمْ شَـٰكِرُونَ</span>

### English

We taught him the art of making body armour to protect you in battle. Will you then be grateful?
