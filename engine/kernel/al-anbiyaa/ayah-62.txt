### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالُوٓا۟ ءَأَنتَ فَعَلْتَ هَـٰذَا بِـَٔالِهَتِنَا يَـٰٓإِبْرَٰهِيمُ</span>

### English

They asked, “Was it you who did this to our gods, O  Abraham?”
