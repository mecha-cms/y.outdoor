### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَتَٱللَّهِ لَأَكِيدَنَّ أَصْنَـٰمَكُم بَعْدَ أَن تُوَلُّوا۟ مُدْبِرِينَ</span>

### English

˹Then he said to himself,˺ “By Allah! I will surely plot against your idols after you have turned your backs and gone away.”
