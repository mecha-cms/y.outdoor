### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَنَصَرْنَـٰهُ مِنَ ٱلْقَوْمِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَذَّبُوا۟ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَآ ۚ إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوا۟ قَوْمَ سَوْءٍ فَأَغْرَقْنَـٰهُمْ أَجْمَعِينَ</span>

### English

And We made him prevail over those who had rejected Our signs. They were truly an evil people, so We drowned them all.
