### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَدْخَلْنَـٰهُمْ فِى رَحْمَتِنَآ ۖ إِنَّهُم مِّنَ ٱلصَّـٰلِحِينَ</span>

### English

We admitted them into Our mercy, for they were truly of the righteous.
