### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا جَعَلْنَـٰهُمْ جَسَدًا لَّا يَأْكُلُونَ ٱلطَّعَامَ وَمَا كَانُوا۟ خَـٰلِدِينَ</span>

### English

We did not give those messengers ˹supernatural˺ bodies that did not need food, nor were they immortal.
