### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالُوٓا۟ أَجِئْتَنَا بِٱلْحَقِّ أَمْ أَنتَ مِنَ ٱللَّـٰعِبِينَ</span>

### English

They asked, “Have you come to us with the truth, or is this a joke?”
