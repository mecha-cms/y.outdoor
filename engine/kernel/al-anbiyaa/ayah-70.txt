### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَرَادُوا۟ بِهِۦ كَيْدًا فَجَعَلْنَـٰهُمُ ٱلْأَخْسَرِينَ</span>

### English

They had sought to harm him, but We made them the worst losers.
