### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قُلْنَا يَـٰنَارُ كُونِى بَرْدًا وَسَلَـٰمًا عَلَىٰٓ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ</span>

### English

We ordered, “O fire! Be cool and safe for Abraham!”
