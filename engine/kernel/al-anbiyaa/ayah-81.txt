### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلِسُلَيْمَـٰنَ ٱلرِّيحَ عَاصِفَةً تَجْرِى بِأَمْرِهِۦٓ إِلَى ٱلْأَرْضِ ٱلَّتِى بَـٰرَكْنَا فِيهَا ۚ وَكُنَّا بِكُلِّ شَىْءٍ عَـٰلِمِينَ</span>

### English

And to Solomon We subjected the raging winds, blowing by his command to the land We had showered with blessings. It is We Who know everything.
