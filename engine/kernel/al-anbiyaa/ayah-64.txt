### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَرَجَعُوٓا۟ إِلَىٰٓ أَنفُسِهِمْ فَقَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّكُمْ أَنتُمُ ٱلظَّـٰلِمُونَ</span>

### English

So they came back to their senses, saying ˹to one another˺, “You yourselves are truly the wrongdoers!”
