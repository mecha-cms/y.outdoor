### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِى خَلَقَ ٱلَّيْلَ وَٱلنَّهَارَ وَٱلشَّمْسَ وَٱلْقَمَرَ ۖ كُلٌّ فِى فَلَكٍ يَسْبَحُونَ</span>

### English

And He is the One Who created the day and the night, the sun and the moon—each travelling in an orbit.
