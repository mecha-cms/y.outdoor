### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ أَفَتَعْبُدُونَ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ مَا لَا يَنفَعُكُمْ شَيْـًٔا وَلَا يَضُرُّكُمْ</span>

### English

He rebuked ˹them˺, “Do you then worship—instead of Allah—what can neither benefit nor harm you in any way?
