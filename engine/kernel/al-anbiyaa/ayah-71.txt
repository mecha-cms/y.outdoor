### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَنَجَّيْنَـٰهُ وَلُوطًا إِلَى ٱلْأَرْضِ ٱلَّتِى بَـٰرَكْنَا فِيهَا لِلْعَـٰلَمِينَ</span>

### English

Then We delivered him, along with Lot, to the land We had showered with blessings for all people.
