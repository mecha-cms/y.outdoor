### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَوْ أَرَدْنَآ أَن نَّتَّخِذَ لَهْوًا لَّٱتَّخَذْنَـٰهُ مِن لَّدُنَّآ إِن كُنَّا فَـٰعِلِينَ</span>

### English

Had We intended to take ˹some˺ amusement, We could have found it in Our presence, if that had been Our Will.
