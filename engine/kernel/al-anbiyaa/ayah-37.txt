### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">خُلِقَ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنُ مِنْ عَجَلٍ ۚ سَأُو۟رِيكُمْ ءَايَـٰتِى فَلَا تَسْتَعْجِلُونِ</span>

### English

Humankind is made of haste. I will soon show you My signs, so do not ask Me to hasten them.
