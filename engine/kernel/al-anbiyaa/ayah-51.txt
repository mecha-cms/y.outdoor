### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ وَلَقَدْ ءَاتَيْنَآ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ رُشْدَهُۥ مِن قَبْلُ وَكُنَّا بِهِۦ عَـٰلِمِينَ</span>

### English

And indeed, We had granted Abraham sound judgment early on, for We knew him well ˹to be worthy of it˺.
