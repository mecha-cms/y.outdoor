### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَنُوحًا إِذْ نَادَىٰ مِن قَبْلُ فَٱسْتَجَبْنَا لَهُۥ فَنَجَّيْنَـٰهُ وَأَهْلَهُۥ مِنَ ٱلْكَرْبِ ٱلْعَظِيمِ</span>

### English

And ˹remember˺ when Noah had cried out to Us earlier, so We responded to him and delivered him and his family from the great distress.
