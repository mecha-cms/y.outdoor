### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ بَلْ فَعَلَهُۥ كَبِيرُهُمْ هَـٰذَا فَسْـَٔلُوهُمْ إِن كَانُوا۟ يَنطِقُونَ</span>

### English

He replied ˹sarcastically˺, “No, this one—the biggest of them—did it! So ask them, if they can talk!”
