### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَكَمْ قَصَمْنَا مِن قَرْيَةٍ كَانَتْ ظَالِمَةً وَأَنشَأْنَا بَعْدَهَا قَوْمًا ءَاخَرِينَ</span>

### English

˹Imagine˺ how many societies of wrongdoers We have destroyed, raising up other people after them!
