### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا خَلَقْنَا ٱلسَّمَآءَ وَٱلْأَرْضَ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَا لَـٰعِبِينَ</span>

### English

We did not create the heavens and the earth and everything in between for sport.
