### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَإِن تَوَلَّوْا۟ فَقُلْ ءَاذَنتُكُمْ عَلَىٰ سَوَآءٍ ۖ وَإِنْ أَدْرِىٓ أَقَرِيبٌ أَم بَعِيدٌ مَّا تُوعَدُونَ</span>

### English

If they turn away, then say, “I have warned you all equally. I do not know if what you are threatened with is near or far.
