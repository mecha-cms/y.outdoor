### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَجَعَلْنَا فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ رَوَٰسِىَ أَن تَمِيدَ بِهِمْ وَجَعَلْنَا فِيهَا فِجَاجًا سُبُلًا لَّعَلَّهُمْ يَهْتَدُونَ</span>

### English

And We have placed firm mountains upon the earth so it does not shake with them, and made in it broad pathways so they may find their way.
