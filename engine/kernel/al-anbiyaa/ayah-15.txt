### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَمَا زَالَت تِّلْكَ دَعْوَىٰهُمْ حَتَّىٰ جَعَلْنَـٰهُمْ حَصِيدًا خَـٰمِدِينَ</span>

### English

They kept repeating their cry until We mowed them down, ˹leaving them˺ lifeless.
