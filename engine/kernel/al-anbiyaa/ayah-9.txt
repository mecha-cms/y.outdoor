### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ثُمَّ صَدَقْنَـٰهُمُ ٱلْوَعْدَ فَأَنجَيْنَـٰهُمْ وَمَن نَّشَآءُ وَأَهْلَكْنَا ٱلْمُسْرِفِينَ</span>

### English

Then We fulfilled Our promise to them, saving them along with whoever We willed and destroying the transgressors.
