### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَوْ كَانَ هَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ ءَالِهَةً مَّا وَرَدُوهَا ۖ وَكُلٌّ فِيهَا خَـٰلِدُونَ</span>

### English

Had those idols been ˹true˺ gods, they would not have entered it. And they will be there forever.
