### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَمِ ٱتَّخَذُوٓا۟ ءَالِهَةً مِّنَ ٱلْأَرْضِ هُمْ يُنشِرُونَ</span>

### English

Or have they taken gods from the earth, who can raise the dead?
