### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَجَعَلَهُمْ جُذَٰذًا إِلَّا كَبِيرًا لَّهُمْ لَعَلَّهُمْ إِلَيْهِ يَرْجِعُونَ</span>

### English

So he smashed them into pieces, except the biggest of them, so they might turn to it ˹for answers˺.
