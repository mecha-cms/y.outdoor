### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ وَصَدُّوا۟ عَن سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ أَضَلَّ أَعْمَـٰلَهُمْ</span>

### English

Those who disbelieve and hinder ˹others˺ from the Way of Allah, He will render their deeds void.
