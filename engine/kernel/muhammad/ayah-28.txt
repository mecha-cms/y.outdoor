### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ذَٰلِكَ بِأَنَّهُمُ ٱتَّبَعُوا۟ مَآ أَسْخَطَ ٱللَّهَ وَكَرِهُوا۟ رِضْوَٰنَهُۥ فَأَحْبَطَ أَعْمَـٰلَهُمْ</span>

### English

This is because they follow whatever displeases Allah and hate whatever pleases Him, so He has rendered their deeds void.
