### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ذَٰلِكَ بِأَنَّهُمْ كَرِهُوا۟ مَآ أَنزَلَ ٱللَّهُ فَأَحْبَطَ أَعْمَـٰلَهُمْ</span>

### English

That is because they detest what Allah has revealed, so He has rendered their deeds void.
