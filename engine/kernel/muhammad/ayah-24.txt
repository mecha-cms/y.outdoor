### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَفَلَا يَتَدَبَّرُونَ ٱلْقُرْءَانَ أَمْ عَلَىٰ قُلُوبٍ أَقْفَالُهَآ</span>

### English

Do they not then reflect on the Quran? Or are there locks upon their hearts?
