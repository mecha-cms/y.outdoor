### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلَا تَهِنُوا۟ وَتَدْعُوٓا۟ إِلَى ٱلسَّلْمِ وَأَنتُمُ ٱلْأَعْلَوْنَ وَٱللَّهُ مَعَكُمْ وَلَن يَتِرَكُمْ أَعْمَـٰلَكُمْ</span>

### English

So do not falter or cry for peace, for you will have the upper hand and Allah is with you. And He will never let your deeds go to waste.
