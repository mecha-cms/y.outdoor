### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِن يَسْـَٔلْكُمُوهَا فَيُحْفِكُمْ تَبْخَلُوا۟ وَيُخْرِجْ أَضْغَـٰنَكُمْ</span>

### English

If He were to do so and pressure you, you would withhold and He would bring out your resentment.
