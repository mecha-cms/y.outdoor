### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓا۟ أَطِيعُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ وَأَطِيعُوا۟ ٱلرَّسُولَ وَلَا تُبْطِلُوٓا۟ أَعْمَـٰلَكُمْ</span>

### English

O believers! Obey Allah and obey the Messenger, and do not let your deeds be in vain.
