### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَذَٰلِكُمُ ٱللَّهُ رَبُّكُمُ ٱلْحَقُّ ۖ فَمَاذَا بَعْدَ ٱلْحَقِّ إِلَّا ٱلضَّلَـٰلُ ۖ فَأَنَّىٰ تُصْرَفُونَ</span>

### English

That is Allah—your True Lord. So what is beyond the truth except falsehood? How can you then be turned away?”
