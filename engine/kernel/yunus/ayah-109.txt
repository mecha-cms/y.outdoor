### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱتَّبِعْ مَا يُوحَىٰٓ إِلَيْكَ وَٱصْبِرْ حَتَّىٰ يَحْكُمَ ٱللَّهُ ۚ وَهُوَ خَيْرُ ٱلْحَـٰكِمِينَ</span>

### English

And follow what is revealed to you, and be patient until Allah passes His judgment. For He is the Best of Judges.
