### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَيُحِقُّ ٱللَّهُ ٱلْحَقَّ بِكَلِمَـٰتِهِۦ وَلَوْ كَرِهَ ٱلْمُجْرِمُونَ</span>

### English

And Allah establishes the truth by His Words—even to the dismay of the wicked.”
