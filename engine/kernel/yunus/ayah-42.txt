### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمِنْهُم مَّن يَسْتَمِعُونَ إِلَيْكَ ۚ أَفَأَنتَ تُسْمِعُ ٱلصُّمَّ وَلَوْ كَانُوا۟ لَا يَعْقِلُونَ</span>

### English

Some of them listen to what you say, but can you make the deaf hear even though they do not understand?
