### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ مُوسَىٰٓ أَتَقُولُونَ لِلْحَقِّ لَمَّا جَآءَكُمْ ۖ أَسِحْرٌ هَـٰذَا وَلَا يُفْلِحُ ٱلسَّـٰحِرُونَ</span>

### English

Moses responded, “Is this what you say about the truth when it has come to you? Is this magic? Magicians will never succeed.”
