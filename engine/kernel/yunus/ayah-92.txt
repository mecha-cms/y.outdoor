### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَٱلْيَوْمَ نُنَجِّيكَ بِبَدَنِكَ لِتَكُونَ لِمَنْ خَلْفَكَ ءَايَةً ۚ وَإِنَّ كَثِيرًا مِّنَ ٱلنَّاسِ عَنْ ءَايَـٰتِنَا لَغَـٰفِلُونَ</span>

### English

Today We will preserve your corpse so that you may become an example for those who come after you. And surely most people are heedless of Our examples!”
