### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ثُمَّ نُنَجِّى رُسُلَنَا وَٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ ۚ كَذَٰلِكَ حَقًّا عَلَيْنَا نُنجِ ٱلْمُؤْمِنِينَ</span>

### English

Then We saved Our messengers and those who believed. For it is Our duty to save the believers.
