### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَمَنْ أَظْلَمُ مِمَّنِ ٱفْتَرَىٰ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ كَذِبًا أَوْ كَذَّبَ بِـَٔايَـٰتِهِۦٓ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ لَا يُفْلِحُ ٱلْمُجْرِمُونَ</span>

### English

Who does more wrong than those who fabricate lies against Allah or deny His revelations? Indeed, the wicked will never succeed.
