### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَثُمَّ إِذَا مَا وَقَعَ ءَامَنتُم بِهِۦٓ ۚ ءَآلْـَٔـٰنَ وَقَدْ كُنتُم بِهِۦ تَسْتَعْجِلُونَ</span>

### English

Will you believe in it only after it has overtaken you? Now? But you always wanted to hasten it!”
