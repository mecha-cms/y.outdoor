### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقَالَ مُوسَىٰ يَـٰقَوْمِ إِن كُنتُمْ ءَامَنتُم بِٱللَّهِ فَعَلَيْهِ تَوَكَّلُوٓا۟ إِن كُنتُم مُّسْلِمِينَ</span>

### English

Moses said, “O my people! If you do believe in Allah and submit ˹to His Will˺, then put your trust in Him.”
