### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمِنْهُم مَّن يَنظُرُ إِلَيْكَ ۚ أَفَأَنتَ تَهْدِى ٱلْعُمْىَ وَلَوْ كَانُوا۟ لَا يُبْصِرُونَ</span>

### English

And some of them look at you, but can you guide the blind even though they cannot see?
