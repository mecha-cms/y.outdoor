### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَلَآ إِنَّ أَوْلِيَآءَ ٱللَّهِ لَا خَوْفٌ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا هُمْ يَحْزَنُونَ</span>

### English

There will certainly be no fear for the close servants of Allah, nor will they grieve.
