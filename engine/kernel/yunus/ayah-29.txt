### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَكَفَىٰ بِٱللَّهِ شَهِيدًۢا بَيْنَنَا وَبَيْنَكُمْ إِن كُنَّا عَنْ عِبَادَتِكُمْ لَغَـٰفِلِينَ</span>

### English

Allah is sufficient as a Witness between each of us that we were totally unaware of your worship.”
