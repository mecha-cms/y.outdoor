### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ثُمَّ جَعَلْنَـٰكُمْ خَلَـٰٓئِفَ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ مِنۢ بَعْدِهِمْ لِنَنظُرَ كَيْفَ تَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

Then We made you their successors in the land to see how you would act.
