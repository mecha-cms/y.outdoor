### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قُلْ إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ يَفْتَرُونَ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ ٱلْكَذِبَ لَا يُفْلِحُونَ</span>

### English

Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “Indeed, those who fabricate lies against Allah will never succeed.”
