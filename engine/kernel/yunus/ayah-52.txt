### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ثُمَّ قِيلَ لِلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا۟ ذُوقُوا۟ عَذَابَ ٱلْخُلْدِ هَلْ تُجْزَوْنَ إِلَّا بِمَا كُنتُمْ تَكْسِبُونَ</span>

### English

Then the wrongdoers will be told, “Taste the torment of eternity! Are you not rewarded except for what you used to commit?”
