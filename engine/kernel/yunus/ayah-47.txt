### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلِكُلِّ أُمَّةٍ رَّسُولٌ ۖ فَإِذَا جَآءَ رَسُولُهُمْ قُضِىَ بَيْنَهُم بِٱلْقِسْطِ وَهُمْ لَا يُظْلَمُونَ</span>

### English

And for every community there is a messenger. After their messenger has come, judgment is passed on them in all fairness, and they are not wronged.
