### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِن كَذَّبُوكَ فَقُل لِّى عَمَلِى وَلَكُمْ عَمَلُكُمْ ۖ أَنتُم بَرِيٓـُٔونَ مِمَّآ أَعْمَلُ وَأَنَا۠ بَرِىٓءٌ مِّمَّا تَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

If they deny you, then say, “My deeds are mine and your deeds are yours. You are free of what I do and I am free of what you do!”
