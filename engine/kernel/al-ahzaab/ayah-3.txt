### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَتَوَكَّلْ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ ۚ وَكَفَىٰ بِٱللَّهِ وَكِيلًا</span>

### English

And put your trust in Allah, for Allah is sufficient as a Trustee of Affairs.
