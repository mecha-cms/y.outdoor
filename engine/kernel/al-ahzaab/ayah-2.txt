### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱتَّبِعْ مَا يُوحَىٰٓ إِلَيْكَ مِن رَّبِّكَ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ كَانَ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ خَبِيرًا</span>

### English

Follow what is revealed to you from your Lord. Surely Allah is All-Aware of what you ˹all˺ do.
