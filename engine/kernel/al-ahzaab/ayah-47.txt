### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَبَشِّرِ ٱلْمُؤْمِنِينَ بِأَنَّ لَهُم مِّنَ ٱللَّهِ فَضْلًا كَبِيرًا</span>

### English

Give good news to the believers that they will have a great bounty from Allah.
