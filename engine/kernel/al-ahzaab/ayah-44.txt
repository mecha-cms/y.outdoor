### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">تَحِيَّتُهُمْ يَوْمَ يَلْقَوْنَهُۥ سَلَـٰمٌ ۚ وَأَعَدَّ لَهُمْ أَجْرًا كَرِيمًا</span>

### English

Their greeting on the Day they meet Him will be, “Peace!” And He has prepared for them an honourable reward.
