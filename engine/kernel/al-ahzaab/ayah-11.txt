### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">هُنَالِكَ ٱبْتُلِىَ ٱلْمُؤْمِنُونَ وَزُلْزِلُوا۟ زِلْزَالًا شَدِيدًا</span>

### English

Then and there the believers were put to the test, and were violently shaken.
