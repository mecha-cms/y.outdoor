### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِن تُبْدُوا۟ شَيْـًٔا أَوْ تُخْفُوهُ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ كَانَ بِكُلِّ شَىْءٍ عَلِيمًا</span>

### English

Whether you reveal something or conceal it, surely Allah has ˹perfect˺ knowledge of all things.
