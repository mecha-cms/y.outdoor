### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَوْ دُخِلَتْ عَلَيْهِم مِّنْ أَقْطَارِهَا ثُمَّ سُئِلُوا۟ ٱلْفِتْنَةَ لَـَٔاتَوْهَا وَمَا تَلَبَّثُوا۟ بِهَآ إِلَّا يَسِيرًا</span>

### English

Had their city been sacked from all sides and they had been asked to abandon faith, they would have done so with little hesitation.
