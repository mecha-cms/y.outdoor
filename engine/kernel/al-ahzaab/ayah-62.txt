### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">سُنَّةَ ٱللَّهِ فِى ٱلَّذِينَ خَلَوْا۟ مِن قَبْلُ ۖ وَلَن تَجِدَ لِسُنَّةِ ٱللَّهِ تَبْدِيلًا</span>

### English

That was Allah’s way with those ˹hypocrites˺ who have gone before. And you will find no change in Allah’s way.
