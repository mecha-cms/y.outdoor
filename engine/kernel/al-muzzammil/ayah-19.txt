### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّ هَـٰذِهِۦ تَذْكِرَةٌ ۖ فَمَن شَآءَ ٱتَّخَذَ إِلَىٰ رَبِّهِۦ سَبِيلًا</span>

### English

Surely this is a reminder. So let whoever wills take the ˹Right˺ Way to their Lord.
