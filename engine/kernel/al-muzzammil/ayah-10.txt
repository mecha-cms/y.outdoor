### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱصْبِرْ عَلَىٰ مَا يَقُولُونَ وَٱهْجُرْهُمْ هَجْرًا جَمِيلًا</span>

Be patient ˹O Prophet˺ with what they say, and depart from them courteously.
