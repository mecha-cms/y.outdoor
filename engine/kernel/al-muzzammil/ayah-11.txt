### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَذَرْنِى وَٱلْمُكَذِّبِينَ أُو۟لِى ٱلنَّعْمَةِ وَمَهِّلْهُمْ قَلِيلًا</span>

### English

And leave to Me the deniers—the people of luxury—and bear with them for a little while.
