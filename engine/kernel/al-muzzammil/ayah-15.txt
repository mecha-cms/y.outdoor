### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّآ أَرْسَلْنَآ إِلَيْكُمْ رَسُولًا شَـٰهِدًا عَلَيْكُمْ كَمَآ أَرْسَلْنَآ إِلَىٰ فِرْعَوْنَ رَسُولًا</span>

### English

Indeed, We have sent to you a messenger as a witness over you, just as We sent a messenger to Pharaoh.
