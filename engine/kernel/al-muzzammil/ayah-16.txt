### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَعَصَىٰ فِرْعَوْنُ ٱلرَّسُولَ فَأَخَذْنَـٰهُ أَخْذًا وَبِيلًا</span>

### English

But Pharaoh disobeyed the messenger, so We seized him with a stern grip.
