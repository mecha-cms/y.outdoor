### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَعْرِضْ عَنْهُمْ وَٱنتَظِرْ إِنَّهُم مُّنتَظِرُونَ</span>

### English

So turn away from them, and wait! They too are waiting.
