### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّ رَبَّكَ هُوَ يَفْصِلُ بَيْنَهُمْ يَوْمَ ٱلْقِيَـٰمَةِ فِيمَا كَانُوا۟ فِيهِ يَخْتَلِفُونَ</span>

### English

Indeed, your Lord will decide between them on the Day of Judgment regarding their differences.
