### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">تَتَجَافَىٰ جُنُوبُهُمْ عَنِ ٱلْمَضَاجِعِ يَدْعُونَ رَبَّهُمْ خَوْفًا وَطَمَعًا وَمِمَّا رَزَقْنَـٰهُمْ يُنفِقُونَ</span>

### English

They abandon their beds, invoking their Lord with hope and fear, and donate from what We have provided for them.
