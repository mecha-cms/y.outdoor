### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلَا تَعْلَمُ نَفْسٌ مَّآ أُخْفِىَ لَهُم مِّن قُرَّةِ أَعْيُنٍ جَزَآءًۢ بِمَا كَانُوا۟ يَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

No soul can imagine what delights are kept in store for them as a reward for what they used to do.
