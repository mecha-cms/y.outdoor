### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">تَنزِيلُ ٱلْكِتَـٰبِ لَا رَيْبَ فِيهِ مِن رَّبِّ ٱلْعَـٰلَمِينَ</span>

### English

The revelation of this Book is—beyond doubt—from the Lord of all worlds.
