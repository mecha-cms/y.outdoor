### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا يُكَذِّبُ بِهِۦٓ إِلَّا كُلُّ مُعْتَدٍ أَثِيمٍ</span>

### English

None would deny it except every evildoing transgressor.
