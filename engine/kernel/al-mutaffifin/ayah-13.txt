### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِذَا تُتْلَىٰ عَلَيْهِ ءَايَـٰتُنَا قَالَ أَسَـٰطِيرُ ٱلْأَوَّلِينَ</span>

### English

Whenever Our revelations are recited to them, they say, “Ancient fables!”
