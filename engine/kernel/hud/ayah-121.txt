### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقُل لِّلَّذِينَ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ ٱعْمَلُوا۟ عَلَىٰ مَكَانَتِكُمْ إِنَّا عَـٰمِلُونَ</span>

### English

Say to those who disbelieve, “Persist in your ways; we will certainly persist in ours.
