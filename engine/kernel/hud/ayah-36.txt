### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأُوحِىَ إِلَىٰ نُوحٍ أَنَّهُۥ لَن يُؤْمِنَ مِن قَوْمِكَ إِلَّا مَن قَدْ ءَامَنَ فَلَا تَبْتَئِسْ بِمَا كَانُوا۟ يَفْعَلُونَ</span>

### English

And it was revealed to Noah, “None of your people will believe except those who already have. So do not be distressed by what they have been doing.
