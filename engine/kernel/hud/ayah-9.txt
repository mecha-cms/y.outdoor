### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَئِنْ أَذَقْنَا ٱلْإِنسَـٰنَ مِنَّا رَحْمَةً ثُمَّ نَزَعْنَـٰهَا مِنْهُ إِنَّهُۥ لَيَـُٔوسٌ كَفُورٌ</span>

### English

If We give people a taste of Our mercy then take it away from them, they become utterly desperate, ungrateful.
