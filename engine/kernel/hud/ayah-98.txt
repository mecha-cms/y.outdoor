### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَقْدُمُ قَوْمَهُۥ يَوْمَ ٱلْقِيَـٰمَةِ فَأَوْرَدَهُمُ ٱلنَّارَ ۖ وَبِئْسَ ٱلْوِرْدُ ٱلْمَوْرُودُ</span>

### English

He will be before his people on the Day of Judgment and will lead them into the Fire. What an evil place to be led into!
