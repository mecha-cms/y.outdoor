### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِنَّ كُلًّا لَّمَّا لَيُوَفِّيَنَّهُمْ رَبُّكَ أَعْمَـٰلَهُمْ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ بِمَا يَعْمَلُونَ خَبِيرٌ</span>

### English

And surely your Lord will fully pay all for their deeds. He is certainly All-Aware of what they do.
