### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ لَوْ أَنَّ لِى بِكُمْ قُوَّةً أَوْ ءَاوِىٓ إِلَىٰ رُكْنٍ شَدِيدٍ</span>

### English

He responded, “If only I had the strength ˹to resist you˺ or could rely on a strong supporter.”
