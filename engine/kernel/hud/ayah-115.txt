### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱصْبِرْ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَا يُضِيعُ أَجْرَ ٱلْمُحْسِنِينَ</span>

### English

And be patient! Certainly Allah does not discount the reward of the good-doers.
