### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالُوا۟ لَقَدْ عَلِمْتَ مَا لَنَا فِى بَنَاتِكَ مِنْ حَقٍّ وَإِنَّكَ لَتَعْلَمُ مَا نُرِيدُ</span>

### English

They argued, “You certainly know that we have no need for your daughters. You already know what we desire!”
