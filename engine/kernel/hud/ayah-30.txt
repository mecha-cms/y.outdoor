### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَيَـٰقَوْمِ مَن يَنصُرُنِى مِنَ ٱللَّهِ إِن طَرَدتُّهُمْ ۚ أَفَلَا تَذَكَّرُونَ</span>

### English

O  my people! Who would protect me from Allah if I were to dismiss them? Will you not then be mindful?
