### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ إِنَّمَا يَأْتِيكُم بِهِ ٱللَّهُ إِن شَآءَ وَمَآ أَنتُم بِمُعْجِزِينَ</span>

### English

He responded, “It is Allah Who can bring it upon you if He wills, and then you will have no escape!
