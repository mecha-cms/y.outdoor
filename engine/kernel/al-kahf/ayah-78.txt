### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ هَـٰذَا فِرَاقُ بَيْنِى وَبَيْنِكَ ۚ سَأُنَبِّئُكَ بِتَأْوِيلِ مَا لَمْ تَسْتَطِع عَّلَيْهِ صَبْرًا</span>

### English

He replied, “This is the parting of our ways. I will explain to you what you could not bear patiently.
