### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱتْلُ مَآ أُوحِىَ إِلَيْكَ مِن كِتَابِ رَبِّكَ ۖ لَا مُبَدِّلَ لِكَلِمَـٰتِهِۦ وَلَن تَجِدَ مِن دُونِهِۦ مُلْتَحَدًا</span>

### English

Recite what has been revealed to you from the Book of your Lord. None can change His Words, nor can you find any refuge besides Him.
