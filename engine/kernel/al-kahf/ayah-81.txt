### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَرَدْنَآ أَن يُبْدِلَهُمَا رَبُّهُمَا خَيْرًا مِّنْهُ زَكَوٰةً وَأَقْرَبَ رُحْمًا</span>

### English

So we hoped that their Lord would give them another, more virtuous and caring in his place.
