### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">كِلْتَا ٱلْجَنَّتَيْنِ ءَاتَتْ أُكُلَهَا وَلَمْ تَظْلِم مِّنْهُ شَيْـًٔا ۚ وَفَجَّرْنَا خِلَـٰلَهُمَا نَهَرًا</span>

### English

Each garden yielded ˹all˺ its produce, never falling short. And We caused a river to flow between them.
