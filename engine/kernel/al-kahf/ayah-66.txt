### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ لَهُۥ مُوسَىٰ هَلْ أَتَّبِعُكَ عَلَىٰٓ أَن تُعَلِّمَنِ مِمَّا عُلِّمْتَ رُشْدًا</span>

### English

Moses said to him, “May I follow you, provided that you teach me some of the right guidance you have been taught?”
