### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ فَإِنِ ٱتَّبَعْتَنِى فَلَا تَسْـَٔلْنِى عَن شَىْءٍ حَتَّىٰٓ أُحْدِثَ لَكَ مِنْهُ ذِكْرًا</span>

### English

He responded, “Then if you follow me, do not question me about anything until I ˹myself˺ clarify it for you.”
