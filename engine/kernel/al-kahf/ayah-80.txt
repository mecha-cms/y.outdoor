### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَمَّا ٱلْغُلَـٰمُ فَكَانَ أَبَوَاهُ مُؤْمِنَيْنِ فَخَشِينَآ أَن يُرْهِقَهُمَا طُغْيَـٰنًا وَكُفْرًا</span>

### English

“And as for the boy, his parents were ˹true˺ believers, and we feared that he would pressure them into defiance and disbelief.
