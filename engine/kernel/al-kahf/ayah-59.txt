### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَتِلْكَ ٱلْقُرَىٰٓ أَهْلَكْنَـٰهُمْ لَمَّا ظَلَمُوا۟ وَجَعَلْنَا لِمَهْلِكِهِم مَّوْعِدًا</span>

### English

Those ˹are the˺ societies We destroyed when they persisted in wrong, and We had set a time for their destruction.
