### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">هُنَالِكَ ٱلْوَلَـٰيَةُ لِلَّهِ ٱلْحَقِّ ۚ هُوَ خَيْرٌ ثَوَابًا وَخَيْرٌ عُقْبًا</span>

### English

At this time, support comes ˹only˺ from Allah—the True ˹Lord˺. He is best in reward and best in outcome.
