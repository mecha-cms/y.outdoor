### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَرَءَا ٱلْمُجْرِمُونَ ٱلنَّارَ فَظَنُّوٓا۟ أَنَّهُم مُّوَاقِعُوهَا وَلَمْ يَجِدُوا۟ عَنْهَا مَصْرِفًا</span>

### English

The wicked will see the Fire and realize that they are bound to fall into it, and will find no way to avoid it.
