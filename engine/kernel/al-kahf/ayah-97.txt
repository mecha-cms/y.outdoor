### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَمَا ٱسْطَـٰعُوٓا۟ أَن يَظْهَرُوهُ وَمَا ٱسْتَطَـٰعُوا۟ لَهُۥ نَقْبًا</span>

And so the enemies could neither scale nor tunnel through it.
