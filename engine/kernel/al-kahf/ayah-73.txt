### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ لَا تُؤَاخِذْنِى بِمَا نَسِيتُ وَلَا تُرْهِقْنِى مِنْ أَمْرِى عُسْرًا</span>

### English

Moses pleaded, “Excuse me for forgetting, and do not be hard on me.”
