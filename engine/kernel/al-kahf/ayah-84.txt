### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّا مَكَّنَّا لَهُۥ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ وَءَاتَيْنَـٰهُ مِن كُلِّ شَىْءٍ سَبَبًا</span>

### English

Surely We established him in the land, and gave him the means to all things.
