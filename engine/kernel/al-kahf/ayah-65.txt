### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَوَجَدَا عَبْدًا مِّنْ عِبَادِنَآ ءَاتَيْنَـٰهُ رَحْمَةً مِّنْ عِندِنَا وَعَلَّمْنَـٰهُ مِن لَّدُنَّا عِلْمًا</span>

### English

There they found a servant of Ours, to whom We had granted mercy from Us and enlightened with knowledge of Our Own.
