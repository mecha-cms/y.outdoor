### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ مَا مَكَّنِّى فِيهِ رَبِّى خَيْرٌ فَأَعِينُونِى بِقُوَّةٍ أَجْعَلْ بَيْنَكُمْ وَبَيْنَهُمْ رَدْمًا</span>

### English

He responded, “What my Lord has provided for me is far better. But assist me with resources, and I will build a barrier between you and them.
