### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَبِثُوا۟ فِى كَهْفِهِمْ ثَلَـٰثَ مِا۟ئَةٍ سِنِينَ وَٱزْدَادُوا۟ تِسْعًا</span>

### English

They had remained in their cave for three hundred years, adding nine.
