### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّا جَعَلْنَا مَا عَلَى ٱلْأَرْضِ زِينَةً لَّهَا لِنَبْلُوَهُمْ أَيُّهُمْ أَحْسَنُ عَمَلًا</span>

### English

We have indeed made whatever is on earth as an adornment for it, in order to test which of them is best in deeds.
