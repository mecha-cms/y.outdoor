### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ سَتَجِدُنِىٓ إِن شَآءَ ٱللَّهُ صَابِرًا وَلَآ أَعْصِى لَكَ أَمْرًا</span>

### English

Moses assured ˹him˺, “You will find me patient, Allah willing, and I will not disobey any of your orders.”
