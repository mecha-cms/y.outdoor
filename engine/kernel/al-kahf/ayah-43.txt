### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَمْ تَكُن لَّهُۥ فِئَةٌ يَنصُرُونَهُۥ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ وَمَا كَانَ مُنتَصِرًا</span>

### English

And he had no manpower to help him against Allah, nor could he ˹even˺ help himself.
