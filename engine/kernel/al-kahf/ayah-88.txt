### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَمَّا مَنْ ءَامَنَ وَعَمِلَ صَـٰلِحًا فَلَهُۥ جَزَآءً ٱلْحُسْنَىٰ ۖ وَسَنَقُولُ لَهُۥ مِنْ أَمْرِنَا يُسْرًا</span>

### English

As for those who believe and do good, they will have the finest reward, and we will assign them easy commands.”
