### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَّـٰكِنَّا۠ هُوَ ٱللَّهُ رَبِّى وَلَآ أُشْرِكُ بِرَبِّىٓ أَحَدًا</span>

### English

But as for me: He is Allah, my Lord, and I will never associate anyone with my Lord ˹in worship˺.
