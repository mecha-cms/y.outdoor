### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ قَالَ أَلَمْ أَقُل لَّكَ إِنَّكَ لَن تَسْتَطِيعَ مَعِىَ صَبْرًا</span>

### English

He answered, “Did I not tell you that you cannot have patience with me?”
