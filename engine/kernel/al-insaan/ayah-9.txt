### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّمَا نُطْعِمُكُمْ لِوَجْهِ ٱللَّهِ لَا نُرِيدُ مِنكُمْ جَزَآءً وَلَا شُكُورًا</span>

### English

˹saying to themselves,˺ “We feed you only for the sake of Allah, seeking neither reward nor thanks from you.
