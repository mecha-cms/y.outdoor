### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّا هَدَيْنَـٰهُ ٱلسَّبِيلَ إِمَّا شَاكِرًا وَإِمَّا كَفُورًا</span>

### English

We already showed them the Way, whether they ˹choose to˺ be grateful or ungrateful.
