### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">هَلْ أَتَىٰ عَلَى ٱلْإِنسَـٰنِ حِينٌ مِّنَ ٱلدَّهْرِ لَمْ يَكُن شَيْـًٔا مَّذْكُورًا</span>

### English

Is there not a period of time when each human is nothing yet worth mentioning?
