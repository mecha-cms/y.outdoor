### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَدَانِيَةً عَلَيْهِمْ ظِلَـٰلُهَا وَذُلِّلَتْ قُطُوفُهَا تَذْلِيلًا</span>

### English

The Garden’s shade will be right above them, and its fruit will be made very easy to reach.
