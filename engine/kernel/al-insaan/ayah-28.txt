### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">نَّحْنُ خَلَقْنَـٰهُمْ وَشَدَدْنَآ أَسْرَهُمْ ۖ وَإِذَا شِئْنَا بَدَّلْنَآ أَمْثَـٰلَهُمْ تَبْدِيلًا</span>

### English

It is We Who created them and perfected their ˹physical˺ form. But if We will, We can easily replace them with others.
