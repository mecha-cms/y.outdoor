### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ وَيَطُوفُ عَلَيْهِمْ وِلْدَٰنٌ مُّخَلَّدُونَ إِذَا رَأَيْتَهُمْ حَسِبْتَهُمْ لُؤْلُؤًا مَّنثُورًا</span>

### English

They will be waited on by eternal youths. If you saw them, you would think they were scattered pearls.
