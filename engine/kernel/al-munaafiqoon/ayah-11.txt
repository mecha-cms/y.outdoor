### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَن يُؤَخِّرَ ٱللَّهُ نَفْسًا إِذَا جَآءَ أَجَلُهَا ۚ وَٱللَّهُ خَبِيرٌۢ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

But Allah never delays a soul when its appointed time comes. And Allah is All-Aware of what you do.
