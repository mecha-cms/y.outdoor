### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَعْرِفُونَ نِعْمَتَ ٱللَّهِ ثُمَّ يُنكِرُونَهَا وَأَكْثَرُهُمُ ٱلْكَـٰفِرُونَ</span>

### English

They are aware of Allah’s favours, but still deny them. And most of them are ˹truly˺ ungrateful.
