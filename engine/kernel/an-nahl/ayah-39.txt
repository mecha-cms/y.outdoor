### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لِيُبَيِّنَ لَهُمُ ٱلَّذِى يَخْتَلِفُونَ فِيهِ وَلِيَعْلَمَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوٓا۟ أَنَّهُمْ كَانُوا۟ كَـٰذِبِينَ</span>

### English

˹He will do that˺ to make clear to them what they disagreed on, and for the disbelievers to know that they were liars.
