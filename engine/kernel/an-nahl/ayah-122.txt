### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَءَاتَيْنَـٰهُ فِى ٱلدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً ۖ وَإِنَّهُۥ فِى ٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ لَمِنَ ٱلصَّـٰلِحِينَ</span>

### English

We blessed him with all goodness in this world, and in the Hereafter he will certainly be among the righteous.
