### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِذَا رَءَا ٱلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا۟ ٱلْعَذَابَ فَلَا يُخَفَّفُ عَنْهُمْ وَلَا هُمْ يُنظَرُونَ</span>

### English

And when the wrongdoers face the punishment, it will not be lightened for them, nor will they be delayed ˹from it˺.
