### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَفَمَن يَخْلُقُ كَمَن لَّا يَخْلُقُ ۗ أَفَلَا تَذَكَّرُونَ</span>

### English

Can the One Who creates be equal to those who do not? Will you not then be mindful?
