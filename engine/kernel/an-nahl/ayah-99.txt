### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّهُۥ لَيْسَ لَهُۥ سُلْطَـٰنٌ عَلَى ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَعَلَىٰ رَبِّهِمْ يَتَوَكَّلُونَ</span>

### English

He certainly has no authority over those who believe and put their trust in their Lord.
