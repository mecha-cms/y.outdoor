### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا تَشْتَرُوا۟ بِعَهْدِ ٱللَّهِ ثَمَنًا قَلِيلًا ۚ إِنَّمَا عِندَ ٱللَّهِ هُوَ خَيْرٌ لَّكُمْ إِن كُنتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ</span>

### English

And do not trade Allah’s covenant for a fleeting gain. What is with Allah is certainly far better for you, if only you knew.
