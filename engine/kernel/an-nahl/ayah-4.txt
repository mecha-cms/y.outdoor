### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">خَلَقَ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنَ مِن نُّطْفَةٍ فَإِذَا هُوَ خَصِيمٌ مُّبِينٌ</span>

### English

He created humans from a sperm-drop, then—behold!—they openly challenge ˹Him˺.
