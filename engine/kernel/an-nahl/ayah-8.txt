### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱلْخَيْلَ وَٱلْبِغَالَ وَٱلْحَمِيرَ لِتَرْكَبُوهَا وَزِينَةً ۚ وَيَخْلُقُ مَا لَا تَعْلَمُونَ</span>

### English

˹He also created˺ horses, mules, and donkeys for your transportation and adornment. And He creates what you do not know.
