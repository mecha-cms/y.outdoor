### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَخَافُونَ رَبَّهُم مِّن فَوْقِهِمْ وَيَفْعَلُونَ مَا يُؤْمَرُونَ ۩</span>

### English

They fear their Lord above them, and do whatever they are commanded.
