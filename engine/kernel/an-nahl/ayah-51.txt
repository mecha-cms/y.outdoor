### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ وَقَالَ ٱللَّهُ لَا تَتَّخِذُوٓا۟ إِلَـٰهَيْنِ ٱثْنَيْنِ ۖ إِنَّمَا هُوَ إِلَـٰهٌ وَٰحِدٌ ۖ فَإِيَّـٰىَ فَٱرْهَبُونِ</span>

### English

And Allah has said, “Do not take two gods. There is only One God. So be in awe of Me ˹alone˺.”
