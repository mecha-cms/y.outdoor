### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَكُمْ فِيهَا جَمَالٌ حِينَ تُرِيحُونَ وَحِينَ تَسْرَحُونَ</span>

### English

They are also pleasing to you when you bring them home and when you take them out to graze.
