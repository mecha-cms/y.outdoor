### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَٱدْخُلُوٓا۟ أَبْوَٰبَ جَهَنَّمَ خَـٰلِدِينَ فِيهَا ۖ فَلَبِئْسَ مَثْوَى ٱلْمُتَكَبِّرِينَ</span>

### English

So enter the gates of Hell, to stay there forever. Indeed, what an evil home for the arrogant!”
