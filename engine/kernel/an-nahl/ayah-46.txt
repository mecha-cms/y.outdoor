### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَوْ يَأْخُذَهُمْ فِى تَقَلُّبِهِمْ فَمَا هُم بِمُعْجِزِينَ</span>

### English

Or that He will not seize them while they go about ˹their day˺, for then they will have no escape?
