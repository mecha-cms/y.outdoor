### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">خَلَقَ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضَ بِٱلْحَقِّ ۚ تَعَـٰلَىٰ عَمَّا يُشْرِكُونَ</span>

### English

He created the heavens and the earth for a purpose. Exalted is He above what they associate with Him ˹in worship˺!
