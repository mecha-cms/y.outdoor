### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَصَابَهُمْ سَيِّـَٔاتُ مَا عَمِلُوا۟ وَحَاقَ بِهِم مَّا كَانُوا۟ بِهِۦ يَسْتَهْزِءُونَ</span>

### English

Then the evil ˹consequences˺ of their deeds overtook them, and they were overwhelmed by what they used to ridicule.
