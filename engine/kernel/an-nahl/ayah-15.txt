### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَلْقَىٰ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ رَوَٰسِىَ أَن تَمِيدَ بِكُمْ وَأَنْهَـٰرًا وَسُبُلًا لَّعَلَّكُمْ تَهْتَدُونَ</span>

### English

He has placed into the earth firm mountains, so it does not shake with you, as well as rivers, and pathways so you may find your way.
