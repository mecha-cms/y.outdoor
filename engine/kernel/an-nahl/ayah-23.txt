### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَا جَرَمَ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ يَعْلَمُ مَا يُسِرُّونَ وَمَا يُعْلِنُونَ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ لَا يُحِبُّ ٱلْمُسْتَكْبِرِينَ</span>

### English

Without a doubt, Allah knows what they conceal and what they reveal. He certainly does not like those who are too proud.
