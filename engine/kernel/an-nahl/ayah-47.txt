### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَوْ يَأْخُذَهُمْ عَلَىٰ تَخَوُّفٍ فَإِنَّ رَبَّكُمْ لَرَءُوفٌ رَّحِيمٌ</span>

### English

Or that He will not destroy them gradually? But your Lord is truly Ever Gracious, Most Merciful.
