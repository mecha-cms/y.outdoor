### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَإِذَا قَرَأْتَ ٱلْقُرْءَانَ فَٱسْتَعِذْ بِٱللَّهِ مِنَ ٱلشَّيْطَـٰنِ ٱلرَّجِيمِ</span>

### English

When you recite the Quran, seek refuge with Allah from Satan, the accursed.
