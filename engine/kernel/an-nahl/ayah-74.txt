### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلَا تَضْرِبُوا۟ لِلَّهِ ٱلْأَمْثَالَ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ يَعْلَمُ وَأَنتُمْ لَا تَعْلَمُونَ</span>

### English

So do not set up equals to Allah, for Allah certainly knows and you do not know.
