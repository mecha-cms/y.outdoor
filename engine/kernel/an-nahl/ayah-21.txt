### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَمْوَٰتٌ غَيْرُ أَحْيَآءٍ ۖ وَمَا يَشْعُرُونَ أَيَّانَ يُبْعَثُونَ</span>

### English

They are dead, not alive—not even knowing when their followers will be resurrected.
