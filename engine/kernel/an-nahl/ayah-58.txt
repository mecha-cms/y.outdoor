### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِذَا بُشِّرَ أَحَدُهُم بِٱلْأُنثَىٰ ظَلَّ وَجْهُهُۥ مُسْوَدًّا وَهُوَ كَظِيمٌ</span>

### English

Whenever one of them is given the good news of a baby girl, his face grows gloomy, as he suppresses his rage.
