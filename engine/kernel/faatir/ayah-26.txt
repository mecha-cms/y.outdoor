### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ثُمَّ أَخَذْتُ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ ۖ فَكَيْفَ كَانَ نَكِيرِ</span>

### English

Then I seized those who persisted in disbelief. How severe was My response!
