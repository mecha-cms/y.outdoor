### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّآ أَرْسَلْنَـٰكَ بِٱلْحَقِّ بَشِيرًا وَنَذِيرًا ۚ وَإِن مِّنْ أُمَّةٍ إِلَّا خَلَا فِيهَا نَذِيرٌ</span>

### English

We have surely sent you with the truth as a deliverer of good news and a warner. There is no community that has not had a warner.
