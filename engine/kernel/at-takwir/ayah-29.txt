### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا تَشَآءُونَ إِلَّآ أَن يَشَآءَ ٱللَّهُ رَبُّ ٱلْعَـٰلَمِينَ</span>

### English

But you cannot will ˹to do so˺, except by the Will of Allah, the Lord of all worlds.
