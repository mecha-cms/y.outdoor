### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ أَوْسَطُهُمْ أَلَمْ أَقُل لَّكُمْ لَوْلَا تُسَبِّحُونَ</span>

### English

The most sensible of them said, “Did I not urge you to say, ‘Allah willing.’?”
