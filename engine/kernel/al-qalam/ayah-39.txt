### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَمْ لَكُمْ أَيْمَـٰنٌ عَلَيْنَا بَـٰلِغَةٌ إِلَىٰ يَوْمِ ٱلْقِيَـٰمَةِ ۙ إِنَّ لَكُمْ لَمَا تَحْكُمُونَ</span>

### English

Or do you have oaths binding on Us until the Day of Judgment that you will have whatever you decide?
