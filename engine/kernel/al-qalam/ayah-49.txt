### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَّوْلَآ أَن تَدَٰرَكَهُۥ نِعْمَةٌ مِّن رَّبِّهِۦ لَنُبِذَ بِٱلْعَرَآءِ وَهُوَ مَذْمُومٌ</span>

### English

Had he not been shown grace by his Lord, he would have certainly been cast onto the open ˹shore˺, still blameworthy.
