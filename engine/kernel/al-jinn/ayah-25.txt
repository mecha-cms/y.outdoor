### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قُلْ إِنْ أَدْرِىٓ أَقَرِيبٌ مَّا تُوعَدُونَ أَمْ يَجْعَلُ لَهُۥ رَبِّىٓ أَمَدًا</span>

### English

Say, “I do not know if what you are promised is near or my Lord has set a distant time for it.
