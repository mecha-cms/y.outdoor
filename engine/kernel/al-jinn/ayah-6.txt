### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَنَّهُۥ كَانَ رِجَالٌ مِّنَ ٱلْإِنسِ يَعُوذُونَ بِرِجَالٍ مِّنَ ٱلْجِنِّ فَزَادُوهُمْ رَهَقًا</span>

### English

And some men used to seek refuge with some jinn—so they increased each other in wickedness.
