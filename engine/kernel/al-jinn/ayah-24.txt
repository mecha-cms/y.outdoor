### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَا رَأَوْا۟ مَا يُوعَدُونَ فَسَيَعْلَمُونَ مَنْ أَضْعَفُ نَاصِرًا وَأَقَلُّ عَدَدًا</span>

### English

Only when they see what they have been threatened with will they know who is weaker in helpers and inferior in manpower.
