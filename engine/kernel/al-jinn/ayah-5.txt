### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَنَّا ظَنَنَّآ أَن لَّن تَقُولَ ٱلْإِنسُ وَٱلْجِنُّ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ كَذِبًا</span>

### English

We certainly thought that humans and jinn would never speak lies about Allah.
