### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَنَّا لَا نَدْرِىٓ أَشَرٌّ أُرِيدَ بِمَن فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ أَمْ أَرَادَ بِهِمْ رَبُّهُمْ رَشَدًا</span>

### English

Now, we have no clue whether evil is intended for those on earth, or their Lord intends for them what is right.
