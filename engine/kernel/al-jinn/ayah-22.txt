### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قُلْ إِنِّى لَن يُجِيرَنِى مِنَ ٱللَّهِ أَحَدٌ وَلَنْ أَجِدَ مِن دُونِهِۦ مُلْتَحَدًا</span>

### English

Say, “No one can protect me from Allah ˹if I were to disobey Him˺, nor can I find any refuge other than Him.
