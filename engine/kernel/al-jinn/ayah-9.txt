### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَنَّا كُنَّا نَقْعُدُ مِنْهَا مَقَـٰعِدَ لِلسَّمْعِ ۖ فَمَن يَسْتَمِعِ ٱلْـَٔانَ يَجِدْ لَهُۥ شِهَابًا رَّصَدًا</span>

### English

We used to take up positions there for eavesdropping, but whoever dares eavesdrop now will find a flare lying in wait for them.
