### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قُلْ إِنَّمَآ أَدْعُوا۟ رَبِّى وَلَآ أُشْرِكُ بِهِۦٓ أَحَدًا</span>

### English

Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “I call only upon my Lord, associating none with Him ˹in worship˺.”
