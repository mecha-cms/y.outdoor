### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَنَّهُۥ لَمَّا قَامَ عَبْدُ ٱللَّهِ يَدْعُوهُ كَادُوا۟ يَكُونُونَ عَلَيْهِ لِبَدًا</span>

### English

Yet when the servant of Allah stood up calling upon Him ˹alone˺, the pagans almost swarmed over him.
