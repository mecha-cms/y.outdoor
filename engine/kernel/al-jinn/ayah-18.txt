### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَنَّ ٱلْمَسَـٰجِدَ لِلَّهِ فَلَا تَدْعُوا۟ مَعَ ٱللَّهِ أَحَدًا</span>

### English

The places of worship are ˹only˺ for Allah, so do not invoke anyone besides Him.
