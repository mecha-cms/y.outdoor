### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَا يَغُرَّنَّكَ تَقَلُّبُ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ فِى ٱلْبِلَـٰدِ</span>

### English

Do not be deceived by the prosperity of the disbelievers throughout the land.
