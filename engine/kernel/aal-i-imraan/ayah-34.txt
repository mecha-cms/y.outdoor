### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ذُرِّيَّةًۢ بَعْضُهَا مِنۢ بَعْضٍ ۗ وَٱللَّهُ سَمِيعٌ عَلِيمٌ</span>

### English

They are descendants of one another. And Allah is All-Hearing, All-Knowing.
