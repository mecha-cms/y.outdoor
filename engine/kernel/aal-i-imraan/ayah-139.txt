### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا تَهِنُوا۟ وَلَا تَحْزَنُوا۟ وَأَنتُمُ ٱلْأَعْلَوْنَ إِن كُنتُم مُّؤْمِنِينَ</span>

### English

Do not falter or grieve, for you will have the upper hand, if you are ˹true˺ believers.
