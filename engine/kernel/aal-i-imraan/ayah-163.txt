### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">هُمْ دَرَجَـٰتٌ عِندَ ٱللَّهِ ۗ وَٱللَّهُ بَصِيرٌۢ بِمَا يَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

They ˹each˺ have varying degrees in the sight of Allah. And Allah is All-Seeing of what they do.
