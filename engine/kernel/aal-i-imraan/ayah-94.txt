### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَمَنِ ٱفْتَرَىٰ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ ٱلْكَذِبَ مِنۢ بَعْدِ ذَٰلِكَ فَأُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلظَّـٰلِمُونَ</span>

### English

Then whoever still fabricates lies about Allah, they will be the ˹true˺ wrongdoers.
