### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَئِن مُّتُّمْ أَوْ قُتِلْتُمْ لَإِلَى ٱللَّهِ تُحْشَرُونَ</span>

### English

Whether you die or are martyred—all of you will be gathered before Allah.
