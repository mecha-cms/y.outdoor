### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱلْحَقُّ مِن رَّبِّكَ فَلَا تَكُن مِّنَ ٱلْمُمْتَرِينَ</span>

### English

This is the truth from your Lord, so do not be one of those who doubt.
