### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَطِيعُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ وَٱلرَّسُولَ لَعَلَّكُمْ تُرْحَمُونَ</span>

### English

Obey Allah and the Messenger, so you may be shown mercy.
