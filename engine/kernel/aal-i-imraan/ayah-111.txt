### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَن يَضُرُّوكُمْ إِلَّآ أَذًى ۖ وَإِن يُقَـٰتِلُوكُمْ يُوَلُّوكُمُ ٱلْأَدْبَارَ ثُمَّ لَا يُنصَرُونَ</span>

### English

They can never inflict harm on you, except a little annoyance. But if they meet you in battle, they will flee and they will have no helpers.
