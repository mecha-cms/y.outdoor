### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَإِن كَذَّبُوكَ فَقَدْ كُذِّبَ رُسُلٌ مِّن قَبْلِكَ جَآءُو بِٱلْبَيِّنَـٰتِ وَٱلزُّبُرِ وَٱلْكِتَـٰبِ ٱلْمُنِيرِ</span>

### English

If you are rejected by them, so too were messengers before you who came with clear proofs, divine Books, and enlightening Scriptures.
