### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">بَلِ ٱللَّهُ مَوْلَىٰكُمْ ۖ وَهُوَ خَيْرُ ٱلنَّـٰصِرِينَ</span>

### English

But no! Allah is your Guardian, and He is the best Helper.
