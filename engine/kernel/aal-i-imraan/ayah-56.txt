### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَمَّا ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ فَأُعَذِّبُهُمْ عَذَابًا شَدِيدًا فِى ٱلدُّنْيَا وَٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ وَمَا لَهُم مِّن نَّـٰصِرِينَ</span>

### English

As for those who disbelieve, I will subject them to a severe punishment in this life and the Hereafter, and they will have no helpers.
