### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَدْ خَلَتْ مِن قَبْلِكُمْ سُنَنٌ فَسِيرُوا۟ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ فَٱنظُرُوا۟ كَيْفَ كَانَ عَـٰقِبَةُ ٱلْمُكَذِّبِينَ</span>

### English

Similar situations came to pass before you, so travel throughout the land and see the fate of the deniers.
