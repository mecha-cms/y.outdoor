### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَخْتَصُّ بِرَحْمَتِهِۦ مَن يَشَآءُ ۗ وَٱللَّهُ ذُو ٱلْفَضْلِ ٱلْعَظِيمِ</span>

### English

He chooses whoever He wills to receive His mercy. And Allah is the Lord of infinite bounty.
