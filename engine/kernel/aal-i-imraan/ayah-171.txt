### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ يَسْتَبْشِرُونَ بِنِعْمَةٍ مِّنَ ٱللَّهِ وَفَضْلٍ وَأَنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَا يُضِيعُ أَجْرَ ٱلْمُؤْمِنِينَ</span>

### English

They are joyful for receiving Allah’s grace and bounty, and that Allah does not deny the reward of the believers.
