### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">مَتَـٰعٌ قَلِيلٌ ثُمَّ مَأْوَىٰهُمْ جَهَنَّمُ ۚ وَبِئْسَ ٱلْمِهَادُ</span>

### English

It is only a brief enjoyment. Then Hell will be their home—what an evil place to rest!
