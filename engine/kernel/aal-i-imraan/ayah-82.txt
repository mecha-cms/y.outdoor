### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَمَن تَوَلَّىٰ بَعْدَ ذَٰلِكَ فَأُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْفَـٰسِقُونَ</span>

### English

Whoever turns back after this, they will be the rebellious.
