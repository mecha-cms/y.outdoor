### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَـَٔاتَىٰهُمُ ٱللَّهُ ثَوَابَ ٱلدُّنْيَا وَحُسْنَ ثَوَابِ ٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ ۗ وَٱللَّهُ يُحِبُّ ٱلْمُحْسِنِينَ</span>

### English

So Allah gave them the reward of this world and the excellent reward of the Hereafter. For Allah loves the good-doers.
