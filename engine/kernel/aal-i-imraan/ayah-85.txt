### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَن يَبْتَغِ غَيْرَ ٱلْإِسْلَـٰمِ دِينًا فَلَن يُقْبَلَ مِنْهُ وَهُوَ فِى ٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ مِنَ ٱلْخَـٰسِرِينَ</span>

### English

Whoever seeks a way other than Islam, it will never be accepted from them, and in the Hereafter they will be among the losers.
