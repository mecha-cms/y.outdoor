### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">هَـٰذَا بَيَانٌ لِّلنَّاسِ وَهُدًى وَمَوْعِظَةٌ لِّلْمُتَّقِينَ</span>

### English

This is an insight to humanity—a guide and a lesson to the God-fearing.
