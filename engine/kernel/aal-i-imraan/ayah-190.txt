### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّ فِى خَلْقِ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ وَٱخْتِلَـٰفِ ٱلَّيْلِ وَٱلنَّهَارِ لَـَٔايَـٰتٍ لِّأُو۟لِى ٱلْأَلْبَـٰبِ</span>

### English

Indeed, in the creation of the heavens and the earth and the alternation of the day and night there are signs for people of reason.
