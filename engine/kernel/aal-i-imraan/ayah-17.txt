### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱلصَّـٰبِرِينَ وَٱلصَّـٰدِقِينَ وَٱلْقَـٰنِتِينَ وَٱلْمُنفِقِينَ وَٱلْمُسْتَغْفِرِينَ بِٱلْأَسْحَارِ</span>

### English

˹It is they˺ who are patient, sincere, obedient, and charitable, and who pray for forgiveness before dawn.
