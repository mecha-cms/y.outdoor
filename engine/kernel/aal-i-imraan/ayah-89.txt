### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ تَابُوا۟ مِنۢ بَعْدِ ذَٰلِكَ وَأَصْلَحُوا۟ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ غَفُورٌ رَّحِيمٌ</span>

### English

As for those who repent afterwards and mend their ways, then surely Allah is All-Forgiving, Most Merciful.
