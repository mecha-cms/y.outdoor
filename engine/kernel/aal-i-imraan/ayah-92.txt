### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَن تَنَالُوا۟ ٱلْبِرَّ حَتَّىٰ تُنفِقُوا۟ مِمَّا تُحِبُّونَ ۚ وَمَا تُنفِقُوا۟ مِن شَىْءٍ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ بِهِۦ عَلِيمٌ</span>

### English

You will never achieve righteousness until you donate some of what you cherish. And whatever you give is certainly well known to Allah.
