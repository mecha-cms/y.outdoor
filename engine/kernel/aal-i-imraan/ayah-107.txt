### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَمَّا ٱلَّذِينَ ٱبْيَضَّتْ وُجُوهُهُمْ فَفِى رَحْمَةِ ٱللَّهِ هُمْ فِيهَا خَـٰلِدُونَ</span>

### English

As for the bright-faced, they will be in Allah’s mercy, where they will remain forever.
