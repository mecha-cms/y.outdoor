### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَكَرُوا۟ وَمَكَرَ ٱللَّهُ ۖ وَٱللَّهُ خَيْرُ ٱلْمَـٰكِرِينَ</span>

### English

And the disbelievers made a plan ˹against Jesus˺, but Allah also planned—and Allah is the best of planners.
