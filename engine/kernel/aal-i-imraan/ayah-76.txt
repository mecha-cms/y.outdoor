### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">بَلَىٰ مَنْ أَوْفَىٰ بِعَهْدِهِۦ وَٱتَّقَىٰ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ يُحِبُّ ٱلْمُتَّقِينَ</span>

### English

Absolutely! Those who honour their trusts and shun evil—surely Allah loves those who are mindful ˹of Him˺.
