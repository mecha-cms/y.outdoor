### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">مَا كَانَ إِبْرَٰهِيمُ يَهُودِيًّا وَلَا نَصْرَانِيًّا وَلَـٰكِن كَانَ حَنِيفًا مُّسْلِمًا وَمَا كَانَ مِنَ ٱلْمُشْرِكِينَ</span>

### English

Abraham was neither a Jew nor a Christian; he submitted in all uprightness and was not a polytheist.
