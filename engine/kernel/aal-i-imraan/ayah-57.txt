### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَمَّا ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَعَمِلُوا۟ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ فَيُوَفِّيهِمْ أُجُورَهُمْ ۗ وَٱللَّهُ لَا يُحِبُّ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ</span>

### English

And as for those who believe and do good, they will be rewarded in full. And Allah does not like the wrongdoers.”
