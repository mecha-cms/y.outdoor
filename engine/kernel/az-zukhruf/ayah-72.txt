### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَتِلْكَ ٱلْجَنَّةُ ٱلَّتِىٓ أُورِثْتُمُوهَا بِمَا كُنتُمْ تَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

That is the Paradise which you will be awarded for what you used to do.
