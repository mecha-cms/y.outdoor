### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَمْ أَنَا۠ خَيْرٌ مِّنْ هَـٰذَا ٱلَّذِى هُوَ مَهِينٌ وَلَا يَكَادُ يُبِينُ</span>

### English

Am I not better than this nobody who can hardly express himself?
