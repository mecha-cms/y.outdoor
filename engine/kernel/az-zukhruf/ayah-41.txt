### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَإِمَّا نَذْهَبَنَّ بِكَ فَإِنَّا مِنْهُم مُّنتَقِمُونَ</span>

### English

Even if We take you away ˹from this world˺, We will surely inflict punishment upon them.
