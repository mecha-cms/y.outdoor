### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَقَدْ جِئْنَـٰكُم بِٱلْحَقِّ وَلَـٰكِنَّ أَكْثَرَكُمْ لِلْحَقِّ كَـٰرِهُونَ</span>

### English

We certainly brought the truth to you, but most of you were resentful of the truth.
