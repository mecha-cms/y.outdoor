### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا يَصُدَّنَّكُمُ ٱلشَّيْطَـٰنُ ۖ إِنَّهُۥ لَكُمْ عَدُوٌّ مُّبِينٌ</span>

### English

And do not let Satan hinder you, ˹for˺ he is certainly your sworn enemy.
