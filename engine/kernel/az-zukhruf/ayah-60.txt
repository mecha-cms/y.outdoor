### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَوْ نَشَآءُ لَجَعَلْنَا مِنكُم مَّلَـٰٓئِكَةً فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ يَخْلُفُونَ</span>

### English

Had We willed, We could have easily replaced you ˹all˺ with angels, succeeding one another on earth.
