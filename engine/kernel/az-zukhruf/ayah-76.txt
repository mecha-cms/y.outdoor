### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا ظَلَمْنَـٰهُمْ وَلَـٰكِن كَانُوا۟ هُمُ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ</span>

### English

We did not wrong them, but it was they who were the wrongdoers.
