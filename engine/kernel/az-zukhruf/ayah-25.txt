### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَٱنتَقَمْنَا مِنْهُمْ ۖ فَٱنظُرْ كَيْفَ كَانَ عَـٰقِبَةُ ٱلْمُكَذِّبِينَ</span>

### English

So We inflicted punishment upon them. See then what was the fate of the deniers!
