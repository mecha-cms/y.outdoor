### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَوْ نُرِيَنَّكَ ٱلَّذِى وَعَدْنَـٰهُمْ فَإِنَّا عَلَيْهِم مُّقْتَدِرُونَ</span>

### English

Or if We show you what We threaten them with, We certainly have full power over them.
