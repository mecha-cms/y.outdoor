### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَمْ ءَاتَيْنَـٰهُمْ كِتَـٰبًا مِّن قَبْلِهِۦ فَهُم بِهِۦ مُسْتَمْسِكُونَ</span>

### English

Or have We given them a Book ˹for proof˺, before this ˹Quran˺, to which they are holding firm?
