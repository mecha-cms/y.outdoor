### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلَوْلَآ أُلْقِىَ عَلَيْهِ أَسْوِرَةٌ مِّن ذَهَبٍ أَوْ جَآءَ مَعَهُ ٱلْمَلَـٰٓئِكَةُ مُقْتَرِنِينَ</span>

### English

Why then have no golden bracelets ˹of kingship˺ been granted to him or angels come with him as escorts!”
