### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَٱصْفَحْ عَنْهُمْ وَقُلْ سَلَـٰمٌ ۚ فَسَوْفَ يَعْلَمُونَ</span>

### English

So bear with them and respond with peace. They will soon come to know.
