### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَجَعَلُوا۟ لَهُۥ مِنْ عِبَادِهِۦ جُزْءًا ۚ إِنَّ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنَ لَكَفُورٌ مُّبِينٌ</span>

### English

Still the pagans have made some of His creation out to be a part of Him. Indeed, humankind is clearly ungrateful.
