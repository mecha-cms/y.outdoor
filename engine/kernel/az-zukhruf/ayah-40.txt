### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَفَأَنتَ تُسْمِعُ ٱلصُّمَّ أَوْ تَهْدِى ٱلْعُمْىَ وَمَن كَانَ فِى ضَلَـٰلٍ مُّبِينٍ</span>

### English

Can you make the deaf hear, or guide the blind or those clearly astray?
