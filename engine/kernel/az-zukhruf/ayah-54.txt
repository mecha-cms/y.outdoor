### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَٱسْتَخَفَّ قَوْمَهُۥ فَأَطَاعُوهُ ۚ إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوا۟ قَوْمًا فَـٰسِقِينَ</span>

### English

And so he fooled his people, and they obeyed him. They were truly a rebellious people.
