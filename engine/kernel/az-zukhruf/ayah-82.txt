### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">سُبْحَـٰنَ رَبِّ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ رَبِّ ٱلْعَرْشِ عَمَّا يَصِفُونَ</span>

### English

Glorified is the Lord of the heavens and the earth, the Lord of the Throne, far above what they claim.
