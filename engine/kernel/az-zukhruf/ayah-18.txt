### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَوَمَن يُنَشَّؤُا۟ فِى ٱلْحِلْيَةِ وَهُوَ فِى ٱلْخِصَامِ غَيْرُ مُبِينٍ</span>

### English

˹Do they attribute to Him˺ those who are brought up in fineries and are not commanding in disputes?
