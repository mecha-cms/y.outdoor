### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَنَادَوْا۟ يَـٰمَـٰلِكُ لِيَقْضِ عَلَيْنَا رَبُّكَ ۖ قَالَ إِنَّكُم مَّـٰكِثُونَ</span>

### English

They will cry, “O Mâlik! Let your Lord finish us off.” He will answer, “You are definitely here to stay.”
