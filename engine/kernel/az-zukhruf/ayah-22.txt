### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">بَلْ قَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّا وَجَدْنَآ ءَابَآءَنَا عَلَىٰٓ أُمَّةٍ وَإِنَّا عَلَىٰٓ ءَاثَـٰرِهِم مُّهْتَدُونَ</span>

### English

In fact, they say, “We found our forefathers following a ˹particular˺ way, and we are following in their footsteps.”
