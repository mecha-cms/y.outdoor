### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنْ هُوَ إِلَّا عَبْدٌ أَنْعَمْنَا عَلَيْهِ وَجَعَلْنَـٰهُ مَثَلًا لِّبَنِىٓ إِسْرَٰٓءِيلَ</span>

### English

He was only a servant We showed favour to, and made as an example for the Children of Israel.
