### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلَمَّآ ءَاسَفُونَا ٱنتَقَمْنَا مِنْهُمْ فَأَغْرَقْنَـٰهُمْ أَجْمَعِينَ</span>

### English

So when they enraged Us, We inflicted punishment upon them, drowning them all.
