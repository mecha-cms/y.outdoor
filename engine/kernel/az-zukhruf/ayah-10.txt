### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱلَّذِى جَعَلَ لَكُمُ ٱلْأَرْضَ مَهْدًا وَجَعَلَ لَكُمْ فِيهَا سُبُلًا لَّعَلَّكُمْ تَهْتَدُونَ</span>

### English

˹He is the One˺ Who has laid out the earth for you, and set in it pathways for you so that you may find your way.
