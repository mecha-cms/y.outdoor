### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّهُۥ مَن يَأْتِ رَبَّهُۥ مُجْرِمًا فَإِنَّ لَهُۥ جَهَنَّمَ لَا يَمُوتُ فِيهَا وَلَا يَحْيَىٰ</span>

### English

Whoever comes to their Lord as an evildoer will certainly have Hell, where they can neither live nor die.
