### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَا رَبَّنَآ إِنَّنَا نَخَافُ أَن يَفْرُطَ عَلَيْنَآ أَوْ أَن يَطْغَىٰ</span>

### English

They both pleaded, “Our Lord! We fear that he may be quick to harm us or act tyrannically.”
