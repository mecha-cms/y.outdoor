### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ خُذْهَا وَلَا تَخَفْ ۖ سَنُعِيدُهَا سِيرَتَهَا ٱلْأُولَىٰ</span>

### English

Allah said, “Take it, and have no fear. We will return it to its former state.
