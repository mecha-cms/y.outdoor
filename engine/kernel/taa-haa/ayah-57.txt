### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ أَجِئْتَنَا لِتُخْرِجَنَا مِنْ أَرْضِنَا بِسِحْرِكَ يَـٰمُوسَىٰ</span>

### English

He said, “Have you come to drive us out of our land with your magic, O  Moses?
