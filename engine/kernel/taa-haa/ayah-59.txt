### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ مَوْعِدُكُمْ يَوْمُ ٱلزِّينَةِ وَأَن يُحْشَرَ ٱلنَّاسُ ضُحًى</span>

### English

Moses said, “Your appointment is on the Day of the Festival, and let the people be gathered mid-morning.”
