### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِنِّى لَغَفَّارٌ لِّمَن تَابَ وَءَامَنَ وَعَمِلَ صَـٰلِحًا ثُمَّ ٱهْتَدَىٰ</span>

### English

But I am truly Most Forgiving to whoever repents, believes, and does good, then persists on ˹true˺ guidance.”
