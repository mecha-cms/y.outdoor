### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلَا يَصُدَّنَّكَ عَنْهَا مَن لَّا يُؤْمِنُ بِهَا وَٱتَّبَعَ هَوَىٰهُ فَتَرْدَىٰ</span>

### English

So do not let those who disbelieve in it and follow their desires distract you from it, or you will be doomed.’”
