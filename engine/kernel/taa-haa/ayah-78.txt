### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَتْبَعَهُمْ فِرْعَوْنُ بِجُنُودِهِۦ فَغَشِيَهُم مِّنَ ٱلْيَمِّ مَا غَشِيَهُمْ</span>

### English

Then Pharaoh pursued them with his soldiers—but how overwhelming were the waters that submerged them!
