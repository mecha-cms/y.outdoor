### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنِّىٓ أَنَا۠ رَبُّكَ فَٱخْلَعْ نَعْلَيْكَ ۖ إِنَّكَ بِٱلْوَادِ ٱلْمُقَدَّسِ طُوًى</span>

### English

It is truly I. I am your Lord! So take off your sandals, for you are in the sacred valley of Ṭuwa.
