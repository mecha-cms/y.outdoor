### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلَنَأْتِيَنَّكَ بِسِحْرٍ مِّثْلِهِۦ فَٱجْعَلْ بَيْنَنَا وَبَيْنَكَ مَوْعِدًا لَّا نُخْلِفُهُۥ نَحْنُ وَلَآ أَنتَ مَكَانًا سُوًى</span>

### English

We can surely meet you with similar magic. So set for us an appointment that neither of us will fail to keep, in a central place.”
