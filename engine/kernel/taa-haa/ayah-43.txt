### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱذْهَبَآ إِلَىٰ فِرْعَوْنَ إِنَّهُۥ طَغَىٰ</span>

### English

Go, both of you, to Pharaoh, for he has truly transgressed ˹all bounds˺.
