### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَتَخَـٰفَتُونَ بَيْنَهُمْ إِن لَّبِثْتُمْ إِلَّا عَشْرًا</span>

### English

They will whisper among themselves, “You stayed no more than ten days ˹on the earth˺.”
