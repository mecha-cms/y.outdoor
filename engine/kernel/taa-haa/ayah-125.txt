### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ رَبِّ لِمَ حَشَرْتَنِىٓ أَعْمَىٰ وَقَدْ كُنتُ بَصِيرًا</span>

### English

They will cry, “My Lord! Why have you raised me up blind, although I used to see?”
