### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ كَذَٰلِكَ أَتَتْكَ ءَايَـٰتُنَا فَنَسِيتَهَا ۖ وَكَذَٰلِكَ ٱلْيَوْمَ تُنسَىٰ</span>

### English

Allah will respond, “It is so, just as Our revelations came to you and you neglected them, so Today you are neglected.”
