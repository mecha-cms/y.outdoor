### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَهُۥ مَا فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَمَا فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَا وَمَا تَحْتَ ٱلثَّرَىٰ</span>

### English

To Him belongs whatever is in the heavens and whatever is on the earth and whatever is in between and whatever is underground.
