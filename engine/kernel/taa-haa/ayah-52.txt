### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ عِلْمُهَا عِندَ رَبِّى فِى كِتَـٰبٍ ۖ لَّا يَضِلُّ رَبِّى وَلَا يَنسَى</span>

### English

He replied, “That knowledge is with my Lord in a Record. My Lord neither falters nor forgets ˹anything˺.”
