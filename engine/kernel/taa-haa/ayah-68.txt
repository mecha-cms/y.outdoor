### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قُلْنَا لَا تَخَفْ إِنَّكَ أَنتَ ٱلْأَعْلَىٰ</span>

### English

We reassured ˹him˺, “Do not fear! It is certainly you who will prevail.
