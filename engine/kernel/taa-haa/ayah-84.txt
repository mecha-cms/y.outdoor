### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ هُمْ أُو۟لَآءِ عَلَىٰٓ أَثَرِى وَعَجِلْتُ إِلَيْكَ رَبِّ لِتَرْضَىٰ</span>

### English

He replied, “They are close on my tracks. And I have hastened to You, my Lord, so You will be pleased.”
