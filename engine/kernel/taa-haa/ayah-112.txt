### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَن يَعْمَلْ مِنَ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ وَهُوَ مُؤْمِنٌ فَلَا يَخَافُ ظُلْمًا وَلَا هَضْمًا</span>

### English

But whoever does good and is a believer will have no fear of being wronged or denied ˹their reward˺.
