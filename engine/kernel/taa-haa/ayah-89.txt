### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَفَلَا يَرَوْنَ أَلَّا يَرْجِعُ إِلَيْهِمْ قَوْلًا وَلَا يَمْلِكُ لَهُمْ ضَرًّا وَلَا نَفْعًا</span>

### English

Did they not see that it did not respond to them, nor could it protect or benefit them?
