### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">نَّحْنُ أَعْلَمُ بِمَا يَقُولُونَ إِذْ يَقُولُ أَمْثَلُهُمْ طَرِيقَةً إِن لَّبِثْتُمْ إِلَّا يَوْمًا</span>

### English

We know best what they will say—the most reasonable of them will say, “You stayed no more than a day.”
