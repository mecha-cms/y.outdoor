### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالُوٓا۟ إِنْ هَـٰذَٰنِ لَسَـٰحِرَٰنِ يُرِيدَانِ أَن يُخْرِجَاكُم مِّنْ أَرْضِكُم بِسِحْرِهِمَا وَيَذْهَبَا بِطَرِيقَتِكُمُ ٱلْمُثْلَىٰ</span>

### English

They concluded, “These two are only magicians who want to drive you out of your land with their magic, and do away with your most cherished traditions.
