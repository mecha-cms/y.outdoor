### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ مِنْهَا خَلَقْنَـٰكُمْ وَفِيهَا نُعِيدُكُمْ وَمِنْهَا نُخْرِجُكُمْ تَارَةً أُخْرَىٰ</span>

### English

From the earth We created you, and into it We will return you, and from it We will bring you back again.
