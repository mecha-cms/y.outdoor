### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَقَدْ أَرَيْنَـٰهُ ءَايَـٰتِنَا كُلَّهَا فَكَذَّبَ وَأَبَىٰ</span>

### English

And We certainly showed Pharaoh all of Our signs, but he denied them and refused ˹to believe˺.
