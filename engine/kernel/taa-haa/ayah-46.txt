### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ لَا تَخَافَآ ۖ إِنَّنِى مَعَكُمَآ أَسْمَعُ وَأَرَىٰ</span>

### English

Allah reassured ˹them˺, “Have no fear! I am with you, hearing and seeing.
