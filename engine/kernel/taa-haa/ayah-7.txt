### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِن تَجْهَرْ بِٱلْقَوْلِ فَإِنَّهُۥ يَعْلَمُ ٱلسِّرَّ وَأَخْفَى</span>

### English

Whether you speak openly ˹or not˺, He certainly knows what is secret and what is even more hidden.
