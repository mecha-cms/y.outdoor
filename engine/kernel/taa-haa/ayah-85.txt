### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ فَإِنَّا قَدْ فَتَنَّا قَوْمَكَ مِنۢ بَعْدِكَ وَأَضَلَّهُمُ ٱلسَّامِرِىُّ</span>

### English

Allah responded, “We have indeed tested your people in your absence, and the Sâmiri has led them astray.”
