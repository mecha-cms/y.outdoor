### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأُلْقِىَ ٱلسَّحَرَةُ سُجَّدًا قَالُوٓا۟ ءَامَنَّا بِرَبِّ هَـٰرُونَ وَمُوسَىٰ</span>

### English

So the magicians fell down in prostration, declaring, “We believe in the Lord of Aaron and Moses.”
