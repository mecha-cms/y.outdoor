### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱذْهَبْ أَنتَ وَأَخُوكَ بِـَٔايَـٰتِى وَلَا تَنِيَا فِى ذِكْرِى</span>

### English

Go forth, you and your brother, with My signs and never falter in remembering Me.
