### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ذَٰلِكَ فَضْلُ ٱللَّهِ يُؤْتِيهِ مَن يَشَآءُ ۚ وَٱللَّهُ ذُو ٱلْفَضْلِ ٱلْعَظِيمِ</span>

### English

This is the favour of Allah. He grants it to whoever He wills. And Allah is the Lord of infinite bounty.
