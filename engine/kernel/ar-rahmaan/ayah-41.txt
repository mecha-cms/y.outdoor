### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يُعْرَفُ ٱلْمُجْرِمُونَ بِسِيمَـٰهُمْ فَيُؤْخَذُ بِٱلنَّوَٰصِى وَٱلْأَقْدَامِ</span>

### English

The wicked will be recognized by their appearance, then will be seized by ˹their˺ forelocks and feet.
