### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يُرْسَلُ عَلَيْكُمَا شُوَاظٌ مِّن نَّارٍ وَنُحَاسٌ فَلَا تَنتَصِرَانِ</span>

### English

Flames of fire and ˹molten˺ copper will be sent against you, and you will not be able to defend one another.
