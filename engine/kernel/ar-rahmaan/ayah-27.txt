### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَيَبْقَىٰ وَجْهُ رَبِّكَ ذُو ٱلْجَلَـٰلِ وَٱلْإِكْرَامِ</span>

### English

Only your Lord Himself, full of Majesty and Honour, will remain ˹forever˺.
