### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَهُ ٱلْجَوَارِ ٱلْمُنشَـَٔاتُ فِى ٱلْبَحْرِ كَٱلْأَعْلَـٰمِ</span>

### English

To Him belong the ships with raised sails, sailing through the seas like mountains.
