### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَسْـَٔلُهُۥ مَن فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ۚ كُلَّ يَوْمٍ هُوَ فِى شَأْنٍ</span>

### English

All those in the heavens and the earth are dependent on Him. Day in and day out He has something to bring about.
