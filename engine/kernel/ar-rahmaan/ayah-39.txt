### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَيَوْمَئِذٍ لَّا يُسْـَٔلُ عَن ذَنۢبِهِۦٓ إِنسٌ وَلَا جَآنٌّ</span>

### English

On that Day there will be no need for any human or jinn to be asked about their sins.
