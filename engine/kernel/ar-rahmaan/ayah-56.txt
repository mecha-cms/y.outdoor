### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فِيهِنَّ قَـٰصِرَٰتُ ٱلطَّرْفِ لَمْ يَطْمِثْهُنَّ إِنسٌ قَبْلَهُمْ وَلَا جَآنٌّ</span>

### English

In both ˹Gardens˺ will be maidens of modest gaze, who no human or jinn has ever touched before.
