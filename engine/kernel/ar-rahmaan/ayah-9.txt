### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَقِيمُوا۟ ٱلْوَزْنَ بِٱلْقِسْطِ وَلَا تُخْسِرُوا۟ ٱلْمِيزَانَ</span>

### English

Weigh with justice, and do not give short measure.
