### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَنَحْنُ أَقْرَبُ إِلَيْهِ مِنكُمْ وَلَـٰكِن لَّا تُبْصِرُونَ</span>

### English

And We are nearer to such a person than you, but you cannot see.
