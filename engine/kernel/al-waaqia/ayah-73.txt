### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">نَحْنُ جَعَلْنَـٰهَا تَذْكِرَةً وَمَتَـٰعًا لِّلْمُقْوِينَ</span>

### English

We have made it ˹as˺ a reminder ˹of the Hellfire˺ and a provision for the travellers.
