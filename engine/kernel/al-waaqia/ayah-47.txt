### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَكَانُوا۟ يَقُولُونَ أَئِذَا مِتْنَا وَكُنَّا تُرَابًا وَعِظَـٰمًا أَءِنَّا لَمَبْعُوثُونَ</span>

### English

They used to ask ˹mockingly˺, “When we are dead and reduced to dust and bones, will we really be resurrected?
