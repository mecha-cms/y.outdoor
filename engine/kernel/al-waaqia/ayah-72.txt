### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ءَأَنتُمْ أَنشَأْتُمْ شَجَرَتَهَآ أَمْ نَحْنُ ٱلْمُنشِـُٔونَ</span>

### English

Is it you who produce its trees, or is it We Who do so?
