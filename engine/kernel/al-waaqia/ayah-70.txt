### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَوْ نَشَآءُ جَعَلْنَـٰهُ أُجَاجًا فَلَوْلَا تَشْكُرُونَ</span>

### English

If We willed, We could make it salty. Will you not then give thanks?
