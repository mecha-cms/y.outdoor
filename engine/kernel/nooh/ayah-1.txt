### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّآ أَرْسَلْنَا نُوحًا إِلَىٰ قَوْمِهِۦٓ أَنْ أَنذِرْ قَوْمَكَ مِن قَبْلِ أَن يَأْتِيَهُمْ عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌ</span>

### English

Indeed, We sent Noah to his people ˹saying to him˺, “Warn your people before a painful punishment comes to them.”
