### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">مِّمَّا خَطِيٓـَٔـٰتِهِمْ أُغْرِقُوا۟ فَأُدْخِلُوا۟ نَارًا فَلَمْ يَجِدُوا۟ لَهُم مِّن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ أَنصَارًا</span>

### English

So because of their sins, they were drowned, then admitted into the Fire. And they found none to help them against Allah.
