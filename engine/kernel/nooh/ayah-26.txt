### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقَالَ نُوحٌ رَّبِّ لَا تَذَرْ عَلَى ٱلْأَرْضِ مِنَ ٱلْكَـٰفِرِينَ دَيَّارًا</span>

### English

Noah had prayed, “My Lord! Do not leave a single disbeliever on earth.
