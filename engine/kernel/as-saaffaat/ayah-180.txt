### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">سُبْحَـٰنَ رَبِّكَ رَبِّ ٱلْعِزَّةِ عَمَّا يَصِفُونَ</span>

### English

Glorified is your Lord—the Lord of Honour and Power—above what they claim!
