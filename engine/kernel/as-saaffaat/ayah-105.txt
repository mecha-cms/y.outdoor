### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَدْ صَدَّقْتَ ٱلرُّءْيَآ ۚ إِنَّا كَذَٰلِكَ نَجْزِى ٱلْمُحْسِنِينَ</span>

### English

You have already fulfilled the vision.” Indeed, this is how We reward the good-doers.
