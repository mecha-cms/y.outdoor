### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَبَـٰرَكْنَا عَلَيْهِ وَعَلَىٰٓ إِسْحَـٰقَ ۚ وَمِن ذُرِّيَّتِهِمَا مُحْسِنٌ وَظَالِمٌ لِّنَفْسِهِۦ مُبِينٌ</span>

### English

We blessed him and Isaac as well. Some of their descendants did good, while others clearly wronged themselves.
