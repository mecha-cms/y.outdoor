### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَسَاهَمَ فَكَانَ مِنَ ٱلْمُدْحَضِينَ</span>

### English

Then ˹to save it from sinking,˺ he drew straws ˹with other passengers˺. He lost ˹and was thrown overboard˺.
