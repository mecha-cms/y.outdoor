### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّكُمْ كُنتُمْ تَأْتُونَنَا عَنِ ٱلْيَمِينِ</span>

### English

The misled will say, “It was you who deluded us away from what is right.”
