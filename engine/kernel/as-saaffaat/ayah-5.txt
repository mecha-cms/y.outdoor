### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">رَّبُّ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَا وَرَبُّ ٱلْمَشَـٰرِقِ</span>

### English

˹He is˺ the Lord of the heavens and the earth and everything in between, and the Lord of all points of sunrise.
