### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا كَانَ لَنَا عَلَيْكُم مِّن سُلْطَـٰنٍۭ ۖ بَلْ كُنتُمْ قَوْمًا طَـٰغِينَ</span>

### English

We had no authority over you. In fact, you yourselves were a transgressing people.
