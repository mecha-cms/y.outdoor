### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَبَشَّرْنَـٰهُ بِإِسْحَـٰقَ نَبِيًّا مِّنَ ٱلصَّـٰلِحِينَ</span>

### English

We ˹later˺ gave him good news of Isaac—a prophet, and one of the righteous.
