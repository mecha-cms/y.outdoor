### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَحَقَّ عَلَيْنَا قَوْلُ رَبِّنَآ ۖ إِنَّا لَذَآئِقُونَ</span>

The decree of our Lord has come to pass against us ˹all˺: we will certainly taste ˹the punishment˺.
