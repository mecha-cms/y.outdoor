### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَإِذَا نَزَلَ بِسَاحَتِهِمْ فَسَآءَ صَبَاحُ ٱلْمُنذَرِينَ</span>

### English

Yet when it descends upon them: how evil will that morning be for those who had been warned!
