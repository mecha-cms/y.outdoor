### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَا فِيهَا غَوْلٌ وَلَا هُمْ عَنْهَا يُنزَفُونَ</span>

### English

It will neither harm ˹them˺, nor will they be intoxicated by it.
