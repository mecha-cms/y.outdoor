### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">بَلِ ٱلسَّاعَةُ مَوْعِدُهُمْ وَٱلسَّاعَةُ أَدْهَىٰ وَأَمَرُّ</span>

### English

Better yet, the Hour is their appointed time—and the Hour will be most catastrophic and most bitter.
