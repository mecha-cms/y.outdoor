### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّا مُرْسِلُوا۟ ٱلنَّاقَةِ فِتْنَةً لَّهُمْ فَٱرْتَقِبْهُمْ وَٱصْطَبِرْ</span>

### English

We are sending the she-camel as a test for them. So watch them ˹closely˺, and have patience.
