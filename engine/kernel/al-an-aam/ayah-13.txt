### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ وَلَهُۥ مَا سَكَنَ فِى ٱلَّيْلِ وَٱلنَّهَارِ ۚ وَهُوَ ٱلسَّمِيعُ ٱلْعَلِيمُ</span>

### English

To Him belongs whatever exists in the day and night. And He is the All-Hearing, All-Knowing.
