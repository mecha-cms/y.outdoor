### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنِّى وَجَّهْتُ وَجْهِىَ لِلَّذِى فَطَرَ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضَ حَنِيفًا ۖ وَمَآ أَنَا۠ مِنَ ٱلْمُشْرِكِينَ</span>

### English

I have turned my face towards the One Who has originated the heavens and the earth—being upright—and I am not one of the polytheists.”
