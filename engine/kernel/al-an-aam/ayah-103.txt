### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَّا تُدْرِكُهُ ٱلْأَبْصَـٰرُ وَهُوَ يُدْرِكُ ٱلْأَبْصَـٰرَ ۖ وَهُوَ ٱللَّطِيفُ ٱلْخَبِيرُ</span>

### English

No vision can encompass Him, but He encompasses all vision. For He is the Most Subtle, All-Aware.
