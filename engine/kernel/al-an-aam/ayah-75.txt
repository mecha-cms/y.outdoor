### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَكَذَٰلِكَ نُرِىٓ إِبْرَٰهِيمَ مَلَكُوتَ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ وَلِيَكُونَ مِنَ ٱلْمُوقِنِينَ</span>

### English

We also showed Abraham the wonders of the heavens and the earth, so he would be sure in faith.
