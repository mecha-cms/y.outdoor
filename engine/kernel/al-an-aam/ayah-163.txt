### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُۥ ۖ وَبِذَٰلِكَ أُمِرْتُ وَأَنَا۠ أَوَّلُ ٱلْمُسْلِمِينَ</span>

### English

He has no partner. So I am commanded, and so I am the first to submit.”
