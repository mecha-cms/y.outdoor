### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قُلْ إِنِّىٓ أَخَافُ إِنْ عَصَيْتُ رَبِّى عَذَابَ يَوْمٍ عَظِيمٍ</span>

### English

Say, “I truly fear—if I were to disobey my Lord—the torment of a tremendous Day.”
