### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَكُلُوا۟ مِمَّا ذُكِرَ ٱسْمُ ٱللَّهِ عَلَيْهِ إِن كُنتُم بِـَٔايَـٰتِهِۦ مُؤْمِنِينَ</span>

### English

So eat only of what is slaughtered in Allah’s Name if you truly believe in His revelations.
