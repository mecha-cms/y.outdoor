### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلِكُلٍّ دَرَجَـٰتٌ مِّمَّا عَمِلُوا۟ ۚ وَمَا رَبُّكَ بِغَـٰفِلٍ عَمَّا يَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

They will each be assigned ranks according to their deeds. And your Lord is not unaware of what they do.
