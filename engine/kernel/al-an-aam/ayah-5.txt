### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَقَدْ كَذَّبُوا۟ بِٱلْحَقِّ لَمَّا جَآءَهُمْ ۖ فَسَوْفَ يَأْتِيهِمْ أَنۢبَـٰٓؤُا۟ مَا كَانُوا۟ بِهِۦ يَسْتَهْزِءُونَ</span>

### English

They have indeed rejected the truth when it came to them, so they will soon face the consequences of their ridicule.
