### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَهَـٰذَا صِرَٰطُ رَبِّكَ مُسْتَقِيمًا ۗ قَدْ فَصَّلْنَا ٱلْـَٔايَـٰتِ لِقَوْمٍ يَذَّكَّرُونَ</span>

### English

That is your Lord’s Path—perfectly straight. We have already made the signs clear to those who are mindful.
