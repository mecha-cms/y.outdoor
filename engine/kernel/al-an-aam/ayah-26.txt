### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَهُمْ يَنْهَوْنَ عَنْهُ وَيَنْـَٔوْنَ عَنْهُ ۖ وَإِن يُهْلِكُونَ إِلَّآ أَنفُسَهُمْ وَمَا يَشْعُرُونَ</span>

### English

They turn others away from the Prophet and distance themselves as well. They ruin none but themselves, yet they fail to perceive it.
