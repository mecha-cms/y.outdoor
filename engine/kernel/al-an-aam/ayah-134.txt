### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّ مَا تُوعَدُونَ لَـَٔاتٍ ۖ وَمَآ أَنتُم بِمُعْجِزِينَ</span>

### English

Indeed, what you have been promised will certainly come to pass. And you will have no escape.
