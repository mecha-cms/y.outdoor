### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قُلْ سِيرُوا۟ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ ثُمَّ ٱنظُرُوا۟ كَيْفَ كَانَ عَـٰقِبَةُ ٱلْمُكَذِّبِينَ</span>

### English

Say, “Travel throughout the land and see the fate of the deniers.”
