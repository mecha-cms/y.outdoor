### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قُلْ فَلِلَّهِ ٱلْحُجَّةُ ٱلْبَـٰلِغَةُ ۖ فَلَوْ شَآءَ لَهَدَىٰكُمْ أَجْمَعِينَ</span>

### English

Say, “Allah has the most conclusive argument. Had it been His Will, He would have easily imposed guidance upon all of you.”
