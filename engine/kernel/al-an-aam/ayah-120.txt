### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَذَرُوا۟ ظَـٰهِرَ ٱلْإِثْمِ وَبَاطِنَهُۥٓ ۚ إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ يَكْسِبُونَ ٱلْإِثْمَ سَيُجْزَوْنَ بِمَا كَانُوا۟ يَقْتَرِفُونَ</span>

### English

Shun all sin—open and secret. Indeed, those who commit sin will be punished for what they earn.
