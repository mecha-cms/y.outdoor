### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">مَّن يُصْرَفْ عَنْهُ يَوْمَئِذٍ فَقَدْ رَحِمَهُۥ ۚ وَذَٰلِكَ ٱلْفَوْزُ ٱلْمُبِينُ</span>

### English

Whoever is spared the torment of that Day will have certainly been shown Allah’s mercy. And that is the absolute triumph.
