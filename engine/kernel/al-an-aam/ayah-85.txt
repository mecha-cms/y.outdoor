### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَزَكَرِيَّا وَيَحْيَىٰ وَعِيسَىٰ وَإِلْيَاسَ ۖ كُلٌّ مِّنَ ٱلصَّـٰلِحِينَ</span>

### English

Likewise, ˹We guided˺ Zachariah, John, Jesus, and Elias, who were all of the righteous.
