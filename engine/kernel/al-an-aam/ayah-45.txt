### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَقُطِعَ دَابِرُ ٱلْقَوْمِ ٱلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا۟ ۚ وَٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ ٱلْعَـٰلَمِينَ</span>

### English

So the wrongdoers were utterly uprooted. And all praise is for Allah—Lord of all worlds.
