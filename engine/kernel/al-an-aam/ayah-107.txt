### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَوْ شَآءَ ٱللَّهُ مَآ أَشْرَكُوا۟ ۗ وَمَا جَعَلْنَـٰكَ عَلَيْهِمْ حَفِيظًا ۖ وَمَآ أَنتَ عَلَيْهِم بِوَكِيلٍ</span>

### English

Had Allah willed, they would not have been polytheists. We have not appointed you as their keeper, nor are you their maintainer.
