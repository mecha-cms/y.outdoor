### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَهَـٰذَا كِتَـٰبٌ أَنزَلْنَـٰهُ مُبَارَكٌ فَٱتَّبِعُوهُ وَٱتَّقُوا۟ لَعَلَّكُمْ تُرْحَمُونَ</span>

### English

This is a blessed Book We have revealed. So follow it and be mindful ˹of Allah˺, so you may be shown mercy.
