### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ لَهُمْ دَارُ ٱلسَّلَـٰمِ عِندَ رَبِّهِمْ ۖ وَهُوَ وَلِيُّهُم بِمَا كَانُوا۟ يَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

They will have the Home of Peace with their Lord, Who will be their Guardian because of what they used to do.
