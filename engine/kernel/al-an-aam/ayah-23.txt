### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ثُمَّ لَمْ تَكُن فِتْنَتُهُمْ إِلَّآ أَن قَالُوا۟ وَٱللَّهِ رَبِّنَا مَا كُنَّا مُشْرِكِينَ</span>

### English

Their only argument will be: “By Allah, our Lord! We were never polytheists.”
