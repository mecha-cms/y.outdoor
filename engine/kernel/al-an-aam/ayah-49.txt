### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱلَّذِينَ كَذَّبُوا۟ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَا يَمَسُّهُمُ ٱلْعَذَابُ بِمَا كَانُوا۟ يَفْسُقُونَ</span>

### English

But those who deny Our signs will be afflicted with punishment for their rebelliousness.
