### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقَالُوٓا۟ إِنْ هِىَ إِلَّا حَيَاتُنَا ٱلدُّنْيَا وَمَا نَحْنُ بِمَبْعُوثِينَ</span>

### English

They insisted, “There is nothing beyond this worldly life and we will never be resurrected.”
