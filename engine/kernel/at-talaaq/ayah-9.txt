### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَذَاقَتْ وَبَالَ أَمْرِهَا وَكَانَ عَـٰقِبَةُ أَمْرِهَا خُسْرًا</span>

### English

So they tasted the evil consequences of their doings, and the outcome of their doings was ˹total˺ loss.
