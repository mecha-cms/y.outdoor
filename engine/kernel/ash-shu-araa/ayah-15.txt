### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ كَلَّا ۖ فَٱذْهَبَا بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَآ ۖ إِنَّا مَعَكُم مُّسْتَمِعُونَ</span>

### English

Allah responded, “Certainly not! So go, both of you, with Our signs. We will be with you, listening.
