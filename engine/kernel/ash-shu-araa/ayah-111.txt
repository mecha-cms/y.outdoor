### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ قَالُوٓا۟ أَنُؤْمِنُ لَكَ وَٱتَّبَعَكَ ٱلْأَرْذَلُونَ</span>

### English

They argued, “How can we believe in you, when you are followed ˹only˺ by the lowest of the low?”
