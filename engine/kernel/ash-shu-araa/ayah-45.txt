### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَلْقَىٰ مُوسَىٰ عَصَاهُ فَإِذَا هِىَ تَلْقَفُ مَا يَأْفِكُونَ</span>

### English

Then Moses threw down his staff, and—behold!—it devoured the objects of their illusion!
