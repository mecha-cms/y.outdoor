### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَفَرَرْتُ مِنكُمْ لَمَّا خِفْتُكُمْ فَوَهَبَ لِى رَبِّى حُكْمًا وَجَعَلَنِى مِنَ ٱلْمُرْسَلِينَ</span>

### English

So I fled from you when I feared you. Then my Lord granted me wisdom and made me one of the messengers.
