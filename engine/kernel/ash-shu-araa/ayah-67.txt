### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّ فِى ذَٰلِكَ لَـَٔايَةً ۖ وَمَا كَانَ أَكْثَرُهُم مُّؤْمِنِينَ</span>

### English

Surely in this is a sign. Yet most of them would not believe.
