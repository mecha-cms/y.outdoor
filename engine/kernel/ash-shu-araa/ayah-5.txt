### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا يَأْتِيهِم مِّن ذِكْرٍ مِّنَ ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ مُحْدَثٍ إِلَّا كَانُوا۟ عَنْهُ مُعْرِضِينَ</span>

### English

Whatever new reminder comes to them from the Most Compassionate, they always turn away from it.
