### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَوَلَمْ يَكُن لَّهُمْ ءَايَةً أَن يَعْلَمَهُۥ عُلَمَـٰٓؤُا۟ بَنِىٓ إِسْرَٰٓءِيلَ</span>

### English

Was it not sufficient proof for the deniers that it has been recognized by the knowledgeable among the Children of Israel?
