### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَنجَيْنَـٰهُ وَمَن مَّعَهُۥ فِى ٱلْفُلْكِ ٱلْمَشْحُونِ</span>

### English

So We saved him and those with him in the fully loaded Ark.
