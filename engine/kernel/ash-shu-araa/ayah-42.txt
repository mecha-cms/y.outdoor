### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ نَعَمْ وَإِنَّكُمْ إِذًا لَّمِنَ ٱلْمُقَرَّبِينَ</span>

### English

He replied, “Yes, and you will then certainly be among those closest to me.”
