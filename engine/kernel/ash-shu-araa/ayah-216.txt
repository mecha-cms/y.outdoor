### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَإِنْ عَصَوْكَ فَقُلْ إِنِّى بَرِىٓءٌ مِّمَّا تَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

But if they disobey you, say, “I am certainly free of what you do.”
