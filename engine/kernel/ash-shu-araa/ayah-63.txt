### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَوْحَيْنَآ إِلَىٰ مُوسَىٰٓ أَنِ ٱضْرِب بِّعَصَاكَ ٱلْبَحْرَ ۖ فَٱنفَلَقَ فَكَانَ كُلُّ فِرْقٍ كَٱلطَّوْدِ ٱلْعَظِيمِ</span>

### English

So We inspired Moses: “Strike the sea with your staff,” and the sea was split, each part was like a huge mountain.
