### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَقَدْ كَذَّبُوا۟ فَسَيَأْتِيهِمْ أَنۢبَـٰٓؤُا۟ مَا كَانُوا۟ بِهِۦ يَسْتَهْزِءُونَ</span>

### English

They have certainly denied ˹the truth˺, so they will soon face the consequences of their ridicule.
