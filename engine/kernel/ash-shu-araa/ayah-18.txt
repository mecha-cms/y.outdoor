### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ أَلَمْ نُرَبِّكَ فِينَا وَلِيدًا وَلَبِثْتَ فِينَا مِنْ عُمُرِكَ سِنِينَ</span>

### English

Pharaoh protested, “Did we not raise you among us as a child, and you stayed several years of your life in our care?
