### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَآ أَسْـَٔلُكُمْ عَلَيْهِ مِنْ أَجْرٍ ۖ إِنْ أَجْرِىَ إِلَّا عَلَىٰ رَبِّ ٱلْعَـٰلَمِينَ</span>

### English

I do not ask you for any reward for this ˹message˺. My reward is only from the Lord of all worlds.
