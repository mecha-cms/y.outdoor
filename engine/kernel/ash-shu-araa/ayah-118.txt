### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَٱفْتَحْ بَيْنِى وَبَيْنَهُمْ فَتْحًا وَنَجِّنِى وَمَن مَّعِىَ مِنَ ٱلْمُؤْمِنِينَ</span>

### English

So judge between me and them decisively, and save me and the believers with me.”
