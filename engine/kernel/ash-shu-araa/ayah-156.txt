### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا تَمَسُّوهَا بِسُوٓءٍ فَيَأْخُذَكُمْ عَذَابُ يَوْمٍ عَظِيمٍ</span>

### English

And do not ever touch her with harm, or you will be overtaken by the torment of a tremendous day.”
