### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَلْقَوْا۟ حِبَالَهُمْ وَعِصِيَّهُمْ وَقَالُوا۟ بِعِزَّةِ فِرْعَوْنَ إِنَّا لَنَحْنُ ٱلْغَـٰلِبُونَ</span>

### English

So they cast down their ropes and staffs, saying, “By Pharaoh’s might, it is we who will surely prevail.”
