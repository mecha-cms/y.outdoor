### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنْ حِسَابُهُمْ إِلَّا عَلَىٰ رَبِّى ۖ لَوْ تَشْعُرُونَ</span>

### English

Their judgment is with my Lord, if you had any sense!
