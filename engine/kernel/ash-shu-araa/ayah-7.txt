### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَوَلَمْ يَرَوْا۟ إِلَى ٱلْأَرْضِ كَمْ أَنۢبَتْنَا فِيهَا مِن كُلِّ زَوْجٍ كَرِيمٍ</span>

### English

Have they failed to look at the earth, ˹to see˺ how many types of fine plants We have caused to grow in it?
