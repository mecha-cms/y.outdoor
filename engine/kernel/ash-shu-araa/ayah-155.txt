### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ هَـٰذِهِۦ نَاقَةٌ لَّهَا شِرْبٌ وَلَكُمْ شِرْبُ يَوْمٍ مَّعْلُومٍ</span>

### English

Ṣâliḥ said, “Here is a camel. She will have her turn to drink as you have yours, each on an appointed day.
