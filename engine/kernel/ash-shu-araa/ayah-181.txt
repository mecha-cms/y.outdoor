### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ أَوْفُوا۟ ٱلْكَيْلَ وَلَا تَكُونُوا۟ مِنَ ٱلْمُخْسِرِينَ</span>

### English

Give full measure, and cause no loss ˹to others˺.
