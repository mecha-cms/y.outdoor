### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلَا تَدْعُ مَعَ ٱللَّهِ إِلَـٰهًا ءَاخَرَ فَتَكُونَ مِنَ ٱلْمُعَذَّبِينَ</span>

### English

So do not ever call upon any other god besides Allah, or you will be one of the punished.
