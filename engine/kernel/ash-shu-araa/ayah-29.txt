### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ لَئِنِ ٱتَّخَذْتَ إِلَـٰهًا غَيْرِى لَأَجْعَلَنَّكَ مِنَ ٱلْمَسْجُونِينَ</span>

### English

Pharaoh threatened, “If you take any other god besides me, I will certainly have you imprisoned.”
