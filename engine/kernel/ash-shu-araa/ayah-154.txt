### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">مَآ أَنتَ إِلَّا بَشَرٌ مِّثْلُنَا فَأْتِ بِـَٔايَةٍ إِن كُنتَ مِنَ ٱلصَّـٰدِقِينَ</span>

### English

You are only a human being like us, so bring forth a sign if what you say is true.”
