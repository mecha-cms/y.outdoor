### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَلَا يَعْلَمُ مَنْ خَلَقَ وَهُوَ ٱللَّطِيفُ ٱلْخَبِيرُ</span>

### English

How could He not know His Own creation? For He ˹alone˺ is the Most Subtle, All-Aware.
