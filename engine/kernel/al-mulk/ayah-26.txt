### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قُلْ إِنَّمَا ٱلْعِلْمُ عِندَ ٱللَّهِ وَإِنَّمَآ أَنَا۠ نَذِيرٌ مُّبِينٌ</span>

### English

Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “That knowledge is with Allah alone, and I am only sent with a clear warning.”
