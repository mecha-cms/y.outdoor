### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ءَأَمِنتُم مَّن فِى ٱلسَّمَآءِ أَن يَخْسِفَ بِكُمُ ٱلْأَرْضَ فَإِذَا هِىَ تَمُورُ</span>

### English

Do you feel secure that the One Who is in heaven will not cause the earth to swallow you up as it quakes violently?
