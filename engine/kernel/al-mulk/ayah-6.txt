### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلِلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ بِرَبِّهِمْ عَذَابُ جَهَنَّمَ ۖ وَبِئْسَ ٱلْمَصِيرُ</span>

### English

Those who disbelieve in their Lord will suffer the punishment of Hell. What an evil destination!
