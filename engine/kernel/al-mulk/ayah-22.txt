### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَفَمَن يَمْشِى مُكِبًّا عَلَىٰ وَجْهِهِۦٓ أَهْدَىٰٓ أَمَّن يَمْشِى سَوِيًّا عَلَىٰ صِرَٰطٍ مُّسْتَقِيمٍ</span>

### English

Who is ˹rightly˺ guided: the one who crawls facedown or the one who walks upright on the Straight Path?
