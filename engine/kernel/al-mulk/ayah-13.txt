### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَسِرُّوا۟ قَوْلَكُمْ أَوِ ٱجْهَرُوا۟ بِهِۦٓ ۖ إِنَّهُۥ عَلِيمٌۢ بِذَاتِ ٱلصُّدُورِ</span>

### English

Whether you speak secretly or openly—He surely knows best what is ˹hidden˺ in the heart.
