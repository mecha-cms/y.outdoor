### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">تَبَـٰرَكَ ٱلَّذِى بِيَدِهِ ٱلْمُلْكُ وَهُوَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَىْءٍ قَدِيرٌ</span>

### English

Blessed is the One in Whose Hands rests all authority. And He is Most Capable of everything.
