### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ثُمَّ ٱرْجِعِ ٱلْبَصَرَ كَرَّتَيْنِ يَنقَلِبْ إِلَيْكَ ٱلْبَصَرُ خَاسِئًا وَهُوَ حَسِيرٌ</span>

### English

Then look again and again—your sight will return frustrated and weary.
