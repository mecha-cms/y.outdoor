### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَمْطَرْنَا عَلَيْهِم مَّطَرًا ۖ فَسَآءَ مَطَرُ ٱلْمُنذَرِينَ</span>

### English

And We poured upon them a rain ˹of brimstone˺. How evil was the rain of those who had been warned!
