### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّ رَبَّكَ يَقْضِى بَيْنَهُم بِحُكْمِهِۦ ۚ وَهُوَ ٱلْعَزِيزُ ٱلْعَلِيمُ</span>

### English

Your Lord will certainly judge between them by His justice, for He is the Almighty, All-Knowing.
