### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قُلْ سِيرُوا۟ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ فَٱنظُرُوا۟ كَيْفَ كَانَ عَـٰقِبَةُ ٱلْمُجْرِمِينَ</span>

### English

Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “Travel throughout the land and see the fate of the wicked.”
