### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِنَّ رَبَّكَ لَيَعْلَمُ مَا تُكِنُّ صُدُورُهُمْ وَمَا يُعْلِنُونَ</span>

### English

And surely your Lord knows what their hearts conceal and what they reveal.
