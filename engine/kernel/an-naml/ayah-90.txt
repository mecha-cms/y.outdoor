### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَن جَآءَ بِٱلسَّيِّئَةِ فَكُبَّتْ وُجُوهُهُمْ فِى ٱلنَّارِ هَلْ تُجْزَوْنَ إِلَّا مَا كُنتُمْ تَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

And whoever comes with an evil deed will be hurled face-first into the Fire. Are you rewarded except for what you used to do?
