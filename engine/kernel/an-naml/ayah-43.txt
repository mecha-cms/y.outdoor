### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَصَدَّهَا مَا كَانَت تَّعْبُدُ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ ۖ إِنَّهَا كَانَتْ مِن قَوْمٍ كَـٰفِرِينَ</span>

### English

But she had been hindered by what she used to worship instead of Allah, for she was indeed from a disbelieving people.
