### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ فَمَا كَانَ جَوَابَ قَوْمِهِۦٓ إِلَّآ أَن قَالُوٓا۟ أَخْرِجُوٓا۟ ءَالَ لُوطٍ مِّن قَرْيَتِكُمْ ۖ إِنَّهُمْ أُنَاسٌ يَتَطَهَّرُونَ</span>

### English

But his people’s only response was to say, “Expel Lot’s followers from your land! They are a people who wish to remain chaste!”
