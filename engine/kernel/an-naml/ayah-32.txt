### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَتْ يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلْمَلَؤُا۟ أَفْتُونِى فِىٓ أَمْرِى مَا كُنتُ قَاطِعَةً أَمْرًا حَتَّىٰ تَشْهَدُونِ</span>

### English

She said, “O chiefs! Advise me in this matter of mine, for I would never make any decision without you.”
