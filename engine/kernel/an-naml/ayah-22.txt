### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَمَكَثَ غَيْرَ بَعِيدٍ فَقَالَ أَحَطتُ بِمَا لَمْ تُحِطْ بِهِۦ وَجِئْتُكَ مِن سَبَإٍۭ بِنَبَإٍ يَقِينٍ</span>

### English

It was not long before the bird came and said, “I have found out something you do not know. I have just come to you from Sheba with sure news.
