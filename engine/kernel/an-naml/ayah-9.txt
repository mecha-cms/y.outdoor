### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَـٰمُوسَىٰٓ إِنَّهُۥٓ أَنَا ٱللَّهُ ٱلْعَزِيزُ ٱلْحَكِيمُ</span>

### English

O  Moses! It is truly I. I am Allah—the Almighty, All-Wise.
