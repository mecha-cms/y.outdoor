### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنِّى وَجَدتُّ ٱمْرَأَةً تَمْلِكُهُمْ وَأُوتِيَتْ مِن كُلِّ شَىْءٍ وَلَهَا عَرْشٌ عَظِيمٌ</span>

### English

Indeed, I found a woman ruling over them, who has been given everything ˹she needs˺, and who has a magnificent throne.
