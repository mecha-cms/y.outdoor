### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَوَقَعَ ٱلْقَوْلُ عَلَيْهِم بِمَا ظَلَمُوا۟ فَهُمْ لَا يَنطِقُونَ</span>

### English

And the decree ˹of torment˺ will be justified against them for their wrongdoing, leaving them speechless.
