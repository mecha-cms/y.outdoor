### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا مِنْ غَآئِبَةٍ فِى ٱلسَّمَآءِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ إِلَّا فِى كِتَـٰبٍ مُّبِينٍ</span>

### English

For there is nothing hidden in the heavens or the earth without being ˹written˺ in a perfect Record.
