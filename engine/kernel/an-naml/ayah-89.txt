### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">مَن جَآءَ بِٱلْحَسَنَةِ فَلَهُۥ خَيْرٌ مِّنْهَا وَهُم مِّن فَزَعٍ يَوْمَئِذٍ ءَامِنُونَ</span>

### English

Whoever comes with a good deed will be rewarded with what is better, and they will be secure from the horror on that Day.
