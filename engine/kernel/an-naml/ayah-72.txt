### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قُلْ عَسَىٰٓ أَن يَكُونَ رَدِفَ لَكُم بَعْضُ ٱلَّذِى تَسْتَعْجِلُونَ</span>

### English

Say, ˹O Prophet,˺ “Perhaps some of what you seek to hasten is close at hand.”
