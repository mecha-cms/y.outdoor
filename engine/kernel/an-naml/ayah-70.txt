### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا تَحْزَنْ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا تَكُن فِى ضَيْقٍ مِّمَّا يَمْكُرُونَ</span>

### English

Do not grieve for them, nor be distressed by their schemes.
