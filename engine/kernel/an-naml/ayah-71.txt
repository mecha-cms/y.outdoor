### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَيَقُولُونَ مَتَىٰ هَـٰذَا ٱلْوَعْدُ إِن كُنتُمْ صَـٰدِقِينَ</span>

### English

They ask ˹the believers˺, “When will this threat come to pass, if what you say is true?”
