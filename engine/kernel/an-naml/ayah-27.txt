### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ قَالَ سَنَنظُرُ أَصَدَقْتَ أَمْ كُنتَ مِنَ ٱلْكَـٰذِبِينَ</span>

### English

Solomon said, “We will see whether you are telling the truth or lying.
