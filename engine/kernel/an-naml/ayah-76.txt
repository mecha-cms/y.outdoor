### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّ هَـٰذَا ٱلْقُرْءَانَ يَقُصُّ عَلَىٰ بَنِىٓ إِسْرَٰٓءِيلَ أَكْثَرَ ٱلَّذِى هُمْ فِيهِ يَخْتَلِفُونَ</span>

### English

Indeed, this Quran clarifies for the Children of Israel most of what they differ over.
