### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلْمَلَؤُا۟ أَيُّكُمْ يَأْتِينِى بِعَرْشِهَا قَبْلَ أَن يَأْتُونِى مُسْلِمِينَ</span>

### English

Solomon asked, “O chiefs! Which of you can bring me her throne before they come to me in ˹full˺ submission?”
