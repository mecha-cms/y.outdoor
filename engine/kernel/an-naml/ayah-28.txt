### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱذْهَب بِّكِتَـٰبِى هَـٰذَا فَأَلْقِهْ إِلَيْهِمْ ثُمَّ تَوَلَّ عَنْهُمْ فَٱنظُرْ مَاذَا يَرْجِعُونَ</span>

### English

Go with this letter of mine and deliver it to them, then stand aside and see how they will respond.”
