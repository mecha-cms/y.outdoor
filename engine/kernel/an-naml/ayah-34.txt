### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَتْ إِنَّ ٱلْمُلُوكَ إِذَا دَخَلُوا۟ قَرْيَةً أَفْسَدُوهَا وَجَعَلُوٓا۟ أَعِزَّةَ أَهْلِهَآ أَذِلَّةً ۖ وَكَذَٰلِكَ يَفْعَلُونَ</span>

### English

She reasoned, “Indeed, when kings invade a land, they ruin it and debase its nobles. They really do so!
