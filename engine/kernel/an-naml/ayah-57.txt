### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَنجَيْنَـٰهُ وَأَهْلَهُۥٓ إِلَّا ٱمْرَأَتَهُۥ قَدَّرْنَـٰهَا مِنَ ٱلْغَـٰبِرِينَ</span>

### English

So We delivered him and his family, except his wife. We had destined her to be one of the doomed.
