### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلَمَّا جَآءَتْهُمْ ءَايَـٰتُنَا مُبْصِرَةً قَالُوا۟ هَـٰذَا سِحْرٌ مُّبِينٌ</span>

### English

But when Our enlightening signs came to them, they said, “This is pure magic.”
