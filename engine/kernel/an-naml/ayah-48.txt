### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَكَانَ فِى ٱلْمَدِينَةِ تِسْعَةُ رَهْطٍ يُفْسِدُونَ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ وَلَا يُصْلِحُونَ</span>

### English

And there were in the city nine ˹elite˺ men who spread corruption in the land, never doing what is right.
