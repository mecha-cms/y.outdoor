### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَحُشِرَ لِسُلَيْمَـٰنَ جُنُودُهُۥ مِنَ ٱلْجِنِّ وَٱلْإِنسِ وَٱلطَّيْرِ فَهُمْ يُوزَعُونَ</span>

### English

Solomon’s forces of jinn, humans, and birds were rallied for him, perfectly organized.
