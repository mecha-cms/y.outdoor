### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ ٱلَّذِينَ لَهُمْ سُوٓءُ ٱلْعَذَابِ وَهُمْ فِى ٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ هُمُ ٱلْأَخْسَرُونَ</span>

### English

It is they who will suffer a dreadful torment, and in the Hereafter they will ˹truly˺ be the greatest losers.
