### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِنَّ رَبَّكَ لَذُو فَضْلٍ عَلَى ٱلنَّاسِ وَلَـٰكِنَّ أَكْثَرَهُمْ لَا يَشْكُرُونَ</span>

### English

Surely your Lord is ever Bountiful to humanity, but most of them are ungrateful.
