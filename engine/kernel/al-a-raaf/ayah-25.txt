### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ فِيهَا تَحْيَوْنَ وَفِيهَا تَمُوتُونَ وَمِنْهَا تُخْرَجُونَ</span>

### English

He added, “There you will live, there you will die, and from there you will be resurrected.”
