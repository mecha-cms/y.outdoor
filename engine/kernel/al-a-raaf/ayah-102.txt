### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا وَجَدْنَا لِأَكْثَرِهِم مِّنْ عَهْدٍ ۖ وَإِن وَجَدْنَآ أَكْثَرَهُمْ لَفَـٰسِقِينَ</span>

### English

We did not find most of them true to their covenant. Rather, We found most of them truly rebellious.
