### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِخْوَٰنُهُمْ يَمُدُّونَهُمْ فِى ٱلْغَىِّ ثُمَّ لَا يُقْصِرُونَ</span>

### English

But the devils persistently plunge their ˹human˺ associates deeper into wickedness, sparing no effort.
