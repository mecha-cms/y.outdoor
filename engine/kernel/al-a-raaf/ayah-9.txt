### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَنْ خَفَّتْ مَوَٰزِينُهُۥ فَأُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ ٱلَّذِينَ خَسِرُوٓا۟ أَنفُسَهُم بِمَا كَانُوا۟ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَا يَظْلِمُونَ</span>

### English

But those whose scale is light, they have doomed themselves for wrongfully denying Our signs.
