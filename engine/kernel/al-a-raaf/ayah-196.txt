### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّ وَلِـِّۧىَ ٱللَّهُ ٱلَّذِى نَزَّلَ ٱلْكِتَـٰبَ ۖ وَهُوَ يَتَوَلَّى ٱلصَّـٰلِحِينَ</span>

### English

“Indeed, my Protector is Allah Who has revealed this Book. For He ˹alone˺ protects the righteous.
