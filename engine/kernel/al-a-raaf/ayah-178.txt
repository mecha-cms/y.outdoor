### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">مَن يَهْدِ ٱللَّهُ فَهُوَ ٱلْمُهْتَدِى ۖ وَمَن يُضْلِلْ فَأُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْخَـٰسِرُونَ</span>

### English

Whoever Allah guides is truly guided. And whoever He leaves to stray, they are the ˹true˺ losers.
