### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ ٱلَّذِينَ ٱسْتَكْبَرُوٓا۟ إِنَّا بِٱلَّذِىٓ ءَامَنتُم بِهِۦ كَـٰفِرُونَ</span>

### English

The arrogant said, “We surely reject what you believe in.”
