### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَنجَيْنَـٰهُ وَأَهْلَهُۥٓ إِلَّا ٱمْرَأَتَهُۥ كَانَتْ مِنَ ٱلْغَـٰبِرِينَ</span>

### English

So We saved him and his family except his wife, who was one of the doomed.
