### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلَمَّا كَشَفْنَا عَنْهُمُ ٱلرِّجْزَ إِلَىٰٓ أَجَلٍ هُم بَـٰلِغُوهُ إِذَا هُمْ يَنكُثُونَ</span>

### English

But as soon as We removed the torment from them—until they met their inevitable fate—they broke their promise.
