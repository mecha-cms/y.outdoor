### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَفَأَمِنُوا۟ مَكْرَ ٱللَّهِ ۚ فَلَا يَأْمَنُ مَكْرَ ٱللَّهِ إِلَّا ٱلْقَوْمُ ٱلْخَـٰسِرُونَ</span>

### English

Did they feel secure against Allah’s planning? None would feel secure from Allah’s planning except the losers.
