### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱدْعُوا۟ رَبَّكُمْ تَضَرُّعًا وَخُفْيَةً ۚ إِنَّهُۥ لَا يُحِبُّ ٱلْمُعْتَدِينَ</span>

### English

Call upon your Lord humbly and secretly. Surely He does not like the transgressors.
