### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ أَلْقُوا۟ ۖ فَلَمَّآ أَلْقَوْا۟ سَحَرُوٓا۟ أَعْيُنَ ٱلنَّاسِ وَٱسْتَرْهَبُوهُمْ وَجَآءُو بِسِحْرٍ عَظِيمٍ</span>

### English

Moses said, “You first.” So when they did, they deceived the eyes of the people, stunned them, and made a great display of magic.
