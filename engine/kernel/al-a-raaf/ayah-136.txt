### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَٱنتَقَمْنَا مِنْهُمْ فَأَغْرَقْنَـٰهُمْ فِى ٱلْيَمِّ بِأَنَّهُمْ كَذَّبُوا۟ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَا وَكَانُوا۟ عَنْهَا غَـٰفِلِينَ</span>

### English

So We inflicted punishment upon them, drowning them in the sea for denying Our signs and being heedless of them.
