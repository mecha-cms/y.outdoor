### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَوَأَمِنَ أَهْلُ ٱلْقُرَىٰٓ أَن يَأْتِيَهُم بَأْسُنَا ضُحًى وَهُمْ يَلْعَبُونَ</span>

### English

Or did they feel secure that Our punishment would not come upon them by day while they were at play?
