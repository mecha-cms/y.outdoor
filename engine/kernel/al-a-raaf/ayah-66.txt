### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ ٱلْمَلَأُ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ مِن قَوْمِهِۦٓ إِنَّا لَنَرَىٰكَ فِى سَفَاهَةٍ وَإِنَّا لَنَظُنُّكَ مِنَ ٱلْكَـٰذِبِينَ</span>

### English

The disbelieving chiefs of his people responded, “We surely see you as a fool, and we certainly think you are a liar.”
