### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلَنَقُصَّنَّ عَلَيْهِم بِعِلْمٍ ۖ وَمَا كُنَّا غَآئِبِينَ</span>

### English

Then We will give them a full account with sure knowledge—for We were never absent.
