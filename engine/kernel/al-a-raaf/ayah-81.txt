### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّكُمْ لَتَأْتُونَ ٱلرِّجَالَ شَهْوَةً مِّن دُونِ ٱلنِّسَآءِ ۚ بَلْ أَنتُمْ قَوْمٌ مُّسْرِفُونَ</span>

### English

You lust after men instead of women! You are certainly transgressors.”
