### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ إِن كُنتَ جِئْتَ بِـَٔايَةٍ فَأْتِ بِهَآ إِن كُنتَ مِنَ ٱلصَّـٰدِقِينَ</span>

### English

Pharaoh said, “If you have come with a sign, then bring it if what you say is true.”
