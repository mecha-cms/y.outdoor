### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمِن قَوْمِ مُوسَىٰٓ أُمَّةٌ يَهْدُونَ بِٱلْحَقِّ وَبِهِۦ يَعْدِلُونَ</span>

### English

There are some among the people of Moses who guide with the truth and establish justice accordingly.
