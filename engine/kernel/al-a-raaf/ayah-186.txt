### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">مَن يُضْلِلِ ٱللَّهُ فَلَا هَادِىَ لَهُۥ ۚ وَيَذَرُهُمْ فِى طُغْيَـٰنِهِمْ يَعْمَهُونَ</span>

### English

Whoever Allah allows to stray, none can guide, leaving them to wander blindly in their defiance.
