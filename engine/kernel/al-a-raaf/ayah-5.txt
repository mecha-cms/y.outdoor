### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَمَا كَانَ دَعْوَىٰهُمْ إِذْ جَآءَهُم بَأْسُنَآ إِلَّآ أَن قَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّا كُنَّا ظَـٰلِمِينَ</span>

### English

Their only cry—when overwhelmed by Our torment—was, “We have indeed been wrongdoers.”
