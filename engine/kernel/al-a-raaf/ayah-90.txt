### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقَالَ ٱلْمَلَأُ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ مِن قَوْمِهِۦ لَئِنِ ٱتَّبَعْتُمْ شُعَيْبًا إِنَّكُمْ إِذًا لَّخَـٰسِرُونَ</span>

### English

The disbelieving chiefs of his people threatened, “If you follow Shu’aib, you will surely be losers!”
