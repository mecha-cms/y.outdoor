### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلِكُلِّ أُمَّةٍ أَجَلٌ ۖ فَإِذَا جَآءَ أَجَلُهُمْ لَا يَسْتَأْخِرُونَ سَاعَةً ۖ وَلَا يَسْتَقْدِمُونَ</span>

### English

For each community there is an appointed term. When their time arrives, they can neither delay it for a moment, nor could they advance it.
