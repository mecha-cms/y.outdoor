### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">خُذِ ٱلْعَفْوَ وَأْمُرْ بِٱلْعُرْفِ وَأَعْرِضْ عَنِ ٱلْجَـٰهِلِينَ</span>

### English

Be gracious, enjoin what is right, and turn away from those who act ignorantly.
