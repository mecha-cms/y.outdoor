### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَأُقَطِّعَنَّ أَيْدِيَكُمْ وَأَرْجُلَكُم مِّنْ خِلَـٰفٍ ثُمَّ لَأُصَلِّبَنَّكُمْ أَجْمَعِينَ</span>

### English

I will certainly cut off your hands and feet on opposite sides, then crucify you all.”
