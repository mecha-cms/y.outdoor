### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَلْقَىٰ عَصَاهُ فَإِذَا هِىَ ثُعْبَانٌ مُّبِينٌ</span>

### English

So Moses threw down his staff and—behold!—it became a real snake.
