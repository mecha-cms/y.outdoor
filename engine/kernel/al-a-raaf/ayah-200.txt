### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِمَّا يَنزَغَنَّكَ مِنَ ٱلشَّيْطَـٰنِ نَزْغٌ فَٱسْتَعِذْ بِٱللَّهِ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ سَمِيعٌ عَلِيمٌ</span>

### English

If you are tempted by Satan, then seek refuge with Allah. Surely He is All-Hearing, All-Knowing.
