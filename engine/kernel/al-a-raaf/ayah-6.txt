### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلَنَسْـَٔلَنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ أُرْسِلَ إِلَيْهِمْ وَلَنَسْـَٔلَنَّ ٱلْمُرْسَلِينَ</span>

### English

We will surely question those who received messengers and We will question the messengers ˹themselves˺.
