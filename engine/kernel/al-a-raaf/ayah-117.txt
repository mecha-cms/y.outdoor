### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ وَأَوْحَيْنَآ إِلَىٰ مُوسَىٰٓ أَنْ أَلْقِ عَصَاكَ ۖ فَإِذَا هِىَ تَلْقَفُ مَا يَأْفِكُونَ</span>

### English

Then We inspired Moses, “Throw down your staff,” and—behold!—it devoured the objects of their illusion!
