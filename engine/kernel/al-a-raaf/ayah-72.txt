### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَنجَيْنَـٰهُ وَٱلَّذِينَ مَعَهُۥ بِرَحْمَةٍ مِّنَّا وَقَطَعْنَا دَابِرَ ٱلَّذِينَ كَذَّبُوا۟ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَا ۖ وَمَا كَانُوا۟ مُؤْمِنِينَ</span>

### English

So We saved him and those with him by Our mercy and uprooted those who denied Our signs. They were not believers.
