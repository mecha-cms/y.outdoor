### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمِمَّنْ خَلَقْنَآ أُمَّةٌ يَهْدُونَ بِٱلْحَقِّ وَبِهِۦ يَعْدِلُونَ</span>

### English

And among those We created is a group that guides with the truth and establishes justice accordingly.
