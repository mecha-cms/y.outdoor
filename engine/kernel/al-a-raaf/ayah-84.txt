### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَمْطَرْنَا عَلَيْهِم مَّطَرًا ۖ فَٱنظُرْ كَيْفَ كَانَ عَـٰقِبَةُ ٱلْمُجْرِمِينَ</span>

### English

We poured upon them a rain ˹of brimstone˺. See what was the end of the wicked!
