### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَفَأَمِنَ أَهْلُ ٱلْقُرَىٰٓ أَن يَأْتِيَهُم بَأْسُنَا بَيَـٰتًا وَهُمْ نَآئِمُونَ</span>

### English

Did the people of those societies feel secure that Our punishment would not come upon them by night while they were asleep?
