### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَجَآءَ ٱلسَّحَرَةُ فِرْعَوْنَ قَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّ لَنَا لَأَجْرًا إِن كُنَّا نَحْنُ ٱلْغَـٰلِبِينَ</span>

### English

The magicians came to Pharaoh, saying, “Shall we receive a ˹suitable˺ reward if we prevail?”
