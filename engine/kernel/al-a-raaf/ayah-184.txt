### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَوَلَمْ يَتَفَكَّرُوا۟ ۗ مَا بِصَاحِبِهِم مِّن جِنَّةٍ ۚ إِنْ هُوَ إِلَّا نَذِيرٌ مُّبِينٌ</span>

### English

Have they not ever given it a thought? Their fellow man is not insane. He is only sent with a clear warning.
