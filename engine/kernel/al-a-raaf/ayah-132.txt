### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقَالُوا۟ مَهْمَا تَأْتِنَا بِهِۦ مِنْ ءَايَةٍ لِّتَسْحَرَنَا بِهَا فَمَا نَحْنُ لَكَ بِمُؤْمِنِينَ</span>

### English

They said, “No matter what sign you may bring to deceive us, we will never believe in you.”
