### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ أَغَيْرَ ٱللَّهِ أَبْغِيكُمْ إِلَـٰهًا وَهُوَ فَضَّلَكُمْ عَلَى ٱلْعَـٰلَمِينَ</span>

### English

He added, “Shall I seek for you a god other than Allah, while He has honoured you above the others?”
