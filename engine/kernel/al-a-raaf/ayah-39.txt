### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقَالَتْ أُولَىٰهُمْ لِأُخْرَىٰهُمْ فَمَا كَانَ لَكُمْ عَلَيْنَا مِن فَضْلٍ فَذُوقُوا۟ ٱلْعَذَابَ بِمَا كُنتُمْ تَكْسِبُونَ</span>

### English

Then the leaders will say to their followers, “You were no better than us! So taste the torment for what you used to commit.”
