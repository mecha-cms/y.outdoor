### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَقَدْ مَكَّنَّـٰكُمْ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ وَجَعَلْنَا لَكُمْ فِيهَا مَعَـٰيِشَ ۗ قَلِيلًا مَّا تَشْكُرُونَ</span>

### English

We have indeed established you on earth and provided you with a means of livelihood. ˹Yet˺ you seldom give any thanks.
