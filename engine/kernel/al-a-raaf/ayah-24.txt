### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ ٱهْبِطُوا۟ بَعْضُكُمْ لِبَعْضٍ عَدُوٌّ ۖ وَلَكُمْ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ مُسْتَقَرٌّ وَمَتَـٰعٌ إِلَىٰ حِينٍ</span>

### English

Allah said, “Descend as enemies to each other. You will find in the earth a residence and provision for your appointed stay.”
