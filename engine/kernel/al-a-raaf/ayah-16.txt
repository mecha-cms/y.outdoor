### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ فَبِمَآ أَغْوَيْتَنِى لَأَقْعُدَنَّ لَهُمْ صِرَٰطَكَ ٱلْمُسْتَقِيمَ</span>

### English

He said, “For leaving me to stray I will lie in ambush for them on Your Straight Path.
