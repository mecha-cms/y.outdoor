### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱتَّبِعُوا۟ مَآ أُنزِلَ إِلَيْكُم مِّن رَّبِّكُمْ وَلَا تَتَّبِعُوا۟ مِن دُونِهِۦٓ أَوْلِيَآءَ ۗ قَلِيلًا مَّا تَذَكَّرُونَ</span>

### English

Follow what has been sent down to you from your Lord, and do not take others as guardians besides Him. How seldom are you mindful!
