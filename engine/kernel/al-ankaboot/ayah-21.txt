### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يُعَذِّبُ مَن يَشَآءُ وَيَرْحَمُ مَن يَشَآءُ ۖ وَإِلَيْهِ تُقْلَبُونَ</span>

### English

He punishes whoever He wills, and shows mercy to whoever He wills. And you will ˹all˺ be returned to Him.
