### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَحَسِبَ ٱلنَّاسُ أَن يُتْرَكُوٓا۟ أَن يَقُولُوٓا۟ ءَامَنَّا وَهُمْ لَا يُفْتَنُونَ</span>

### English

Do people think once they say, “We believe,” that they will be left without being put to the test?
