### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّا مُنزِلُونَ عَلَىٰٓ أَهْلِ هَـٰذِهِ ٱلْقَرْيَةِ رِجْزًا مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ بِمَا كَانُوا۟ يَفْسُقُونَ</span>

### English

We are certainly bringing down a punishment from heaven upon the people of this city for their rebelliousness.”
