### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَتِلْكَ ٱلْأَمْثَـٰلُ نَضْرِبُهَا لِلنَّاسِ ۖ وَمَا يَعْقِلُهَآ إِلَّا ٱلْعَـٰلِمُونَ</span>

### English

These are the parables We set forth for humanity, but none will understand them except the people of knowledge.
