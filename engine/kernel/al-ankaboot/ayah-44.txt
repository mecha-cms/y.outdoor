### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">خَلَقَ ٱللَّهُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضَ بِٱلْحَقِّ ۚ إِنَّ فِى ذَٰلِكَ لَـَٔايَةً لِّلْمُؤْمِنِينَ</span>

### English

Allah created the heavens and the earth for a purpose. Surely in this is a sign for the people of faith.
