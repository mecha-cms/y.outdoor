### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَـٰعِبَادِىَ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓا۟ إِنَّ أَرْضِى وَٰسِعَةٌ فَإِيَّـٰىَ فَٱعْبُدُونِ</span>

### English

O My believing servants! My earth is truly spacious, so worship Me ˹alone˺.
