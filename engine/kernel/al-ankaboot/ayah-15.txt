### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَنجَيْنَـٰهُ وَأَصْحَـٰبَ ٱلسَّفِينَةِ وَجَعَلْنَـٰهَآ ءَايَةً لِّلْعَـٰلَمِينَ</span>

### English

But We delivered him and those in the Ark, making it a sign for all people.
