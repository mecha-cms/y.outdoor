### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱصْلَوْهَا فَٱصْبِرُوٓا۟ أَوْ لَا تَصْبِرُوا۟ سَوَآءٌ عَلَيْكُمْ ۖ إِنَّمَا تُجْزَوْنَ مَا كُنتُمْ تَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

Burn in it! It is the same whether you endure ˹it˺ patiently or not. You are only rewarded for what you used to do.”
