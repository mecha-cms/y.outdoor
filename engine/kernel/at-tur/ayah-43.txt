### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَمْ لَهُمْ إِلَـٰهٌ غَيْرُ ٱللَّهِ ۚ سُبْحَـٰنَ ٱللَّهِ عَمَّا يُشْرِكُونَ</span>

### English

Or do they have a god other than Allah? Glorified is Allah far above what they associate ˹with Him˺!
