### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَمْ يَقُولُونَ تَقَوَّلَهُۥ ۚ بَل لَّا يُؤْمِنُونَ</span>

### English

Or do they say, “He made this ˹Quran˺ up!”? In fact, they have no faith.
