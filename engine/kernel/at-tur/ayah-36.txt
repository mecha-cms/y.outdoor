### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَمْ خَلَقُوا۟ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضَ ۚ بَل لَّا يُوقِنُونَ</span>

### English

Or did they create the heavens and the earth? In fact, they have no firm belief ˹in Allah˺.
