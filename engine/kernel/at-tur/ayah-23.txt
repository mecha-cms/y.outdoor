### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَتَنَـٰزَعُونَ فِيهَا كَأْسًا لَّا لَغْوٌ فِيهَا وَلَا تَأْثِيمٌ</span>

### English

They will pass around to each other a drink ˹of pure wine,˺ which leads to no idle talk or sinfulness.
