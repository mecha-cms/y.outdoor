### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ وَيَطُوفُ عَلَيْهِمْ غِلْمَانٌ لَّهُمْ كَأَنَّهُمْ لُؤْلُؤٌ مَّكْنُونٌ</span>

### English

And they will be waited on by their youthful servants like spotless pearls.
