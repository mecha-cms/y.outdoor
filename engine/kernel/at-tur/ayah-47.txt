### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِنَّ لِلَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا۟ عَذَابًا دُونَ ذَٰلِكَ وَلَـٰكِنَّ أَكْثَرَهُمْ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ</span>

### English

Also, the wrongdoers will certainly have another torment before that ˹Day˺, but most of them do not know.
