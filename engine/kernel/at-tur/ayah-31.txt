### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قُلْ تَرَبَّصُوا۟ فَإِنِّى مَعَكُم مِّنَ ٱلْمُتَرَبِّصِينَ</span>

### English

Say, “Keep waiting! I too am waiting with you.”
