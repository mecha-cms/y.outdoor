### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَن يَهْدِ ٱللَّهُ فَمَا لَهُۥ مِن مُّضِلٍّ ۗ أَلَيْسَ ٱللَّهُ بِعَزِيزٍ ذِى ٱنتِقَامٍ</span>

### English

And whoever Allah guides, none can lead astray. Is Allah not Almighty, capable of punishment?
