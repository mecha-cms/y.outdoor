### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">بَلَىٰ قَدْ جَآءَتْكَ ءَايَـٰتِى فَكَذَّبْتَ بِهَا وَٱسْتَكْبَرْتَ وَكُنتَ مِنَ ٱلْكَـٰفِرِينَ</span>

### English

Not at all! My revelations had already come to you, but you rejected them, acted arrogantly, and were one of the disbelievers.”
