### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لِيُكَفِّرَ ٱللَّهُ عَنْهُمْ أَسْوَأَ ٱلَّذِى عَمِلُوا۟ وَيَجْزِيَهُمْ أَجْرَهُم بِأَحْسَنِ ٱلَّذِى كَانُوا۟ يَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

As such, Allah will absolve them of ˹even˺ the worst of what they did and reward them according to the best of what they used to do.
