### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">كَذَّبَ ٱلَّذِينَ مِن قَبْلِهِمْ فَأَتَىٰهُمُ ٱلْعَذَابُ مِنْ حَيْثُ لَا يَشْعُرُونَ</span>

### English

Those before them ˹also˺ rejected ˹the truth˺, then the torment came upon them from where they least expected.
