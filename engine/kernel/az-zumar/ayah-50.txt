### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَدْ قَالَهَا ٱلَّذِينَ مِن قَبْلِهِمْ فَمَآ أَغْنَىٰ عَنْهُم مَّا كَانُوا۟ يَكْسِبُونَ</span>

### English

The same had already been said by those ˹destroyed˺ before them, but their ˹worldly˺ gains were of no benefit to them.
