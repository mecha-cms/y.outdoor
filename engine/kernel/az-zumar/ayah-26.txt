### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَذَاقَهُمُ ٱللَّهُ ٱلْخِزْىَ فِى ٱلْحَيَوٰةِ ٱلدُّنْيَا ۖ وَلَعَذَابُ ٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ أَكْبَرُ ۚ لَوْ كَانُوا۟ يَعْلَمُونَ</span>

### English

So Allah made them taste humiliation in this worldly life, but far worse is the punishment of the Hereafter, if only they knew.
