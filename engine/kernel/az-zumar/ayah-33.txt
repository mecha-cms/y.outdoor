### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱلَّذِى جَآءَ بِٱلصِّدْقِ وَصَدَّقَ بِهِۦٓ ۙ أُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْمُتَّقُونَ</span>

### English

And the one who has brought the truth and those who embrace it—it is they who are the righteous.
