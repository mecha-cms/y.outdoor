### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَوُفِّيَتْ كُلُّ نَفْسٍ مَّا عَمِلَتْ وَهُوَ أَعْلَمُ بِمَا يَفْعَلُونَ</span>

### English

Every soul will be paid in full for its deeds, for Allah knows best what they have done.
