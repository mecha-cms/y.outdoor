### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَهُم مَّا يَشَآءُونَ عِندَ رَبِّهِمْ ۚ ذَٰلِكَ جَزَآءُ ٱلْمُحْسِنِينَ</span>

### English

They will have whatever they desire with their Lord. That is the reward of the good-doers.
