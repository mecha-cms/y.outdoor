### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱعْلَمُوٓا۟ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ شَدِيدُ ٱلْعِقَابِ وَأَنَّ ٱللَّهَ غَفُورٌ رَّحِيمٌ</span>

### English

Know that Allah is severe in punishment and that He is All-Forgiving, Most Merciful.
