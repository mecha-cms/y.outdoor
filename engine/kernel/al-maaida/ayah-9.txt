### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَعَدَ ٱللَّهُ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَعَمِلُوا۟ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ ۙ لَهُم مَّغْفِرَةٌ وَأَجْرٌ عَظِيمٌ</span>

### English

Allah has promised those who believe and do good ˹His˺ forgiveness and a great reward.
