### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَطَوَّعَتْ لَهُۥ نَفْسُهُۥ قَتْلَ أَخِيهِ فَقَتَلَهُۥ فَأَصْبَحَ مِنَ ٱلْخَـٰسِرِينَ</span>

### English

Yet Cain convinced himself to kill his brother, so he killed him—becoming a loser.
