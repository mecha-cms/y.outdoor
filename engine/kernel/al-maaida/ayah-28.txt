### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَئِنۢ بَسَطتَ إِلَىَّ يَدَكَ لِتَقْتُلَنِى مَآ أَنَا۠ بِبَاسِطٍ يَدِىَ إِلَيْكَ لِأَقْتُلَكَ ۖ إِنِّىٓ أَخَافُ ٱللَّهَ رَبَّ ٱلْعَـٰلَمِينَ</span>

### English

If you raise your hand to kill me, I will not raise mine to kill you, because I fear Allah—the Lord of all worlds.
