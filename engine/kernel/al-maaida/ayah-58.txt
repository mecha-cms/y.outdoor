### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِذَا نَادَيْتُمْ إِلَى ٱلصَّلَوٰةِ ٱتَّخَذُوهَا هُزُوًا وَلَعِبًا ۚ ذَٰلِكَ بِأَنَّهُمْ قَوْمٌ لَّا يَعْقِلُونَ</span>

### English

When you call to prayer, they mock it in amusement. This is because they are a people without understanding.
