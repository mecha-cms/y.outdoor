### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يُرِيدُونَ أَن يَخْرُجُوا۟ مِنَ ٱلنَّارِ وَمَا هُم بِخَـٰرِجِينَ مِنْهَا ۖ وَلَهُمْ عَذَابٌ مُّقِيمٌ</span>

### English

They will be desperate to get out of the Fire but they will never be able to. And they will suffer an everlasting punishment.
