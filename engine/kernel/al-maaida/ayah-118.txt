### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِن تُعَذِّبْهُمْ فَإِنَّهُمْ عِبَادُكَ ۖ وَإِن تَغْفِرْ لَهُمْ فَإِنَّكَ أَنتَ ٱلْعَزِيزُ ٱلْحَكِيمُ</span>

### English

If You punish them, they belong to You after all. But if You forgive them, You are surely the Almighty, All-Wise.”
