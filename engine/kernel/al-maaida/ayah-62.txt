### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَتَرَىٰ كَثِيرًا مِّنْهُمْ يُسَـٰرِعُونَ فِى ٱلْإِثْمِ وَٱلْعُدْوَٰنِ وَأَكْلِهِمُ ٱلسُّحْتَ ۚ لَبِئْسَ مَا كَانُوا۟ يَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

You see many of them racing towards sin, transgression, and consumption of forbidden gain. Evil indeed are their actions!
