### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا لَنَا لَا نُؤْمِنُ بِٱللَّهِ وَمَا جَآءَنَا مِنَ ٱلْحَقِّ وَنَطْمَعُ أَن يُدْخِلَنَا رَبُّنَا مَعَ ٱلْقَوْمِ ٱلصَّـٰلِحِينَ</span>

### English

Why should we not believe in Allah and the truth that has come to us? And we long for our Lord to include us in the company of the righteous.”
