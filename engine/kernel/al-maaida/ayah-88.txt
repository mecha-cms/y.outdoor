### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَكُلُوا۟ مِمَّا رَزَقَكُمُ ٱللَّهُ حَلَـٰلًا طَيِّبًا ۚ وَٱتَّقُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَنتُم بِهِۦ مُؤْمِنُونَ</span>

### English

Eat of the good, lawful things provided to you by Allah. And be mindful of Allah in Whom you believe.
