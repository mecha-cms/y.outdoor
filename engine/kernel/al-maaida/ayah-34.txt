### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ تَابُوا۟ مِن قَبْلِ أَن تَقْدِرُوا۟ عَلَيْهِمْ ۖ فَٱعْلَمُوٓا۟ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ غَفُورٌ رَّحِيمٌ</span>

### English

As for those who repent before you seize them, then know that Allah is All-Forgiving, Most Merciful.
