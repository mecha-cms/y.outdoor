### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَدْ سَأَلَهَا قَوْمٌ مِّن قَبْلِكُمْ ثُمَّ أَصْبَحُوا۟ بِهَا كَـٰفِرِينَ</span>

### English

Some people before you asked such questions then denied their answers.
