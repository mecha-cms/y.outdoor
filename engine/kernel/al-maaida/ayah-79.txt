### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">كَانُوا۟ لَا يَتَنَاهَوْنَ عَن مُّنكَرٍ فَعَلُوهُ ۚ لَبِئْسَ مَا كَانُوا۟ يَفْعَلُونَ</span>

### English

They did not forbid one another from doing evil. Evil indeed was what they did!
