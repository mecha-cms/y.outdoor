### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">كَبُرَ مَقْتًا عِندَ ٱللَّهِ أَن تَقُولُوا۟ مَا لَا تَفْعَلُونَ</span>

### English

How despicable it is in the sight of Allah that you say what you do not do!
