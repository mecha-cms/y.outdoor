### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَمَهِّلِ ٱلْكَـٰفِرِينَ أَمْهِلْهُمْ رُوَيْدًۢا</span>

### English

So bear with the disbelievers ˹O Prophet˺. Let them be for ˹just˺ a little while.
