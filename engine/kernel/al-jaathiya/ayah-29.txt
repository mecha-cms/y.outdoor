### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">هَـٰذَا كِتَـٰبُنَا يَنطِقُ عَلَيْكُم بِٱلْحَقِّ ۚ إِنَّا كُنَّا نَسْتَنسِخُ مَا كُنتُمْ تَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

This record of Ours speaks the truth about you. Indeed, We always had your deeds recorded ˹by the angels˺.”
