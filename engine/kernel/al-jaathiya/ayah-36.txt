### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَلِلَّهِ ٱلْحَمْدُ رَبِّ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَرَبِّ ٱلْأَرْضِ رَبِّ ٱلْعَـٰلَمِينَ</span>

### English

So all praise is for Allah—Lord of the heavens and Lord of the earth, Lord of all worlds.
