### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَفِى خَلْقِكُمْ وَمَا يَبُثُّ مِن دَآبَّةٍ ءَايَـٰتٌ لِّقَوْمٍ يُوقِنُونَ</span>

### English

And in your own creation, and whatever living beings He dispersed, are signs for people of sure faith.
