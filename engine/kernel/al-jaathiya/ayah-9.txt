### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِذَا عَلِمَ مِنْ ءَايَـٰتِنَا شَيْـًٔا ٱتَّخَذَهَا هُزُوًا ۚ أُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ لَهُمْ عَذَابٌ مُّهِينٌ</span>

### English

And whenever they learn anything of Our revelations, they make a mockery of it. It is they who will suffer a humiliating punishment.
