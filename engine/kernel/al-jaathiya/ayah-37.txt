### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَهُ ٱلْكِبْرِيَآءُ فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ۖ وَهُوَ ٱلْعَزِيزُ ٱلْحَكِيمُ</span>

### English

To Him belongs ˹all˺ Majesty in the heavens and the earth. And He is the Almighty, All-Wise.
