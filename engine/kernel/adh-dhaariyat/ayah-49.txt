### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمِن كُلِّ شَىْءٍ خَلَقْنَا زَوْجَيْنِ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَذَكَّرُونَ</span>

### English

And We created pairs of all things so perhaps you would be mindful.
