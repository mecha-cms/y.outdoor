### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ هُوَ ٱلرَّزَّاقُ ذُو ٱلْقُوَّةِ ٱلْمَتِينُ</span>

### English

Indeed, Allah ˹alone˺ is the Supreme Provider—Lord of all Power, Ever Mighty.
