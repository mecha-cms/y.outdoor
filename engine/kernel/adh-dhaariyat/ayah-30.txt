### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالُوا۟ كَذَٰلِكِ قَالَ رَبُّكِ ۖ إِنَّهُۥ هُوَ ٱلْحَكِيمُ ٱلْعَلِيمُ</span>

### English

They replied, “Such has your Lord decreed. He is truly the All-Wise, All-Knowing.”
