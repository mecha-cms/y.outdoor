### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">مَا تَذَرُ مِن شَىْءٍ أَتَتْ عَلَيْهِ إِلَّا جَعَلَتْهُ كَٱلرَّمِيمِ</span>

### English

There was nothing it came upon that it did not reduce to ashes.
