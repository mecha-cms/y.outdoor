### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَخَذْنَـٰهُ وَجُنُودَهُۥ فَنَبَذْنَـٰهُمْ فِى ٱلْيَمِّ وَهُوَ مُلِيمٌ</span>

### English

So We seized him and his soldiers, casting them into the sea while he was blameworthy.
