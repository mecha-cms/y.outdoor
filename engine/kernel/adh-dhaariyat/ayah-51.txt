### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا تَجْعَلُوا۟ مَعَ ٱللَّهِ إِلَـٰهًا ءَاخَرَ ۖ إِنِّى لَكُم مِّنْهُ نَذِيرٌ مُّبِينٌ</span>

### English

And do not set up another god with Allah. I am truly sent by Him with a clear warning to you.”
