### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">كَذَٰلِكَ مَآ أَتَى ٱلَّذِينَ مِن قَبْلِهِم مِّن رَّسُولٍ إِلَّا قَالُوا۟ سَاحِرٌ أَوْ مَجْنُونٌ</span>

### English

Similarly, no messenger came to those before them without being told: “A magician or a madman!”
