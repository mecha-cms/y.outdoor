### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱلْأَرْضَ فَرَشْنَـٰهَا فَنِعْمَ ٱلْمَـٰهِدُونَ</span>

### English

As for the earth, We spread it out. How superbly did We smooth it out!
