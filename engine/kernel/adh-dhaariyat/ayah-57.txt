### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">مَآ أُرِيدُ مِنْهُم مِّن رِّزْقٍ وَمَآ أُرِيدُ أَن يُطْعِمُونِ</span>

### English

I seek no provision from them, nor do I need them to feed Me.
