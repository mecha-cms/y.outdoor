### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا خَلَقْتُ ٱلْجِنَّ وَٱلْإِنسَ إِلَّا لِيَعْبُدُونِ</span>

### English

I did not create jinn and humans except to worship Me.
