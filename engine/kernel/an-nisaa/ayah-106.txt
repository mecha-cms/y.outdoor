### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱسْتَغْفِرِ ٱللَّهَ ۖ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ كَانَ غَفُورًا رَّحِيمًا</span>

### English

And seek Allah’s forgiveness—indeed, Allah is All-Forgiving, Most Merciful.
