### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَرُسُلًا قَدْ قَصَصْنَـٰهُمْ عَلَيْكَ مِن قَبْلُ وَرُسُلًا لَّمْ نَقْصُصْهُمْ عَلَيْكَ ۚ وَكَلَّمَ ٱللَّهُ مُوسَىٰ تَكْلِيمًا</span>

### English

There are messengers whose stories We have told you already and others We have not. And to Moses Allah spoke directly.
