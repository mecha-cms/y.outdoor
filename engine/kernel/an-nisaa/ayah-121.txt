### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ مَأْوَىٰهُمْ جَهَنَّمُ وَلَا يَجِدُونَ عَنْهَا مَحِيصًا</span>

### English

It is they who will have Hell as their home, and they will find no escape from it!
