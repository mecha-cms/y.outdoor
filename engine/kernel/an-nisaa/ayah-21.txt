### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَكَيْفَ تَأْخُذُونَهُۥ وَقَدْ أَفْضَىٰ بَعْضُكُمْ إِلَىٰ بَعْضٍ وَأَخَذْنَ مِنكُم مِّيثَـٰقًا غَلِيظًا</span>

### English

And how could you take it back after having enjoyed each other intimately and she has taken from you a firm commitment?
