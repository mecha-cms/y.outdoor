### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱللَّهُ أَعْلَمُ بِأَعْدَآئِكُمْ ۚ وَكَفَىٰ بِٱللَّهِ وَلِيًّا وَكَفَىٰ بِٱللَّهِ نَصِيرًا</span>

### English

Allah knows best who your enemies are! And Allah is sufficient as a Guardian, and He is sufficient as a Helper.
