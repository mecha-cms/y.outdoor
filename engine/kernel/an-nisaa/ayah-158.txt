### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">بَل رَّفَعَهُ ٱللَّهُ إِلَيْهِ ۚ وَكَانَ ٱللَّهُ عَزِيزًا حَكِيمًا</span>

### English

Rather, Allah raised him up to Himself. And Allah is Almighty, All-Wise.
