### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَمِنْهُم مَّنْ ءَامَنَ بِهِۦ وَمِنْهُم مَّن صَدَّ عَنْهُ ۚ وَكَفَىٰ بِجَهَنَّمَ سَعِيرًا</span>

### English

Yet some believed in him while others turned away from him. Hell is sufficient as a torment!
