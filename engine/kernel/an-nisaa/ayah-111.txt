### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَن يَكْسِبْ إِثْمًا فَإِنَّمَا يَكْسِبُهُۥ عَلَىٰ نَفْسِهِۦ ۚ وَكَانَ ٱللَّهُ عَلِيمًا حَكِيمًا</span>

### English

And whoever commits a sin—it is only to their own loss. Allah is All-Knowing, All-Wise.
