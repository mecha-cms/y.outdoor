### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَكَيْفَ إِذَا جِئْنَا مِن كُلِّ أُمَّةٍۭ بِشَهِيدٍ وَجِئْنَا بِكَ عَلَىٰ هَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ شَهِيدًا</span>

### English

So how will it be when We bring a witness from every faith-community and bring you ˹O Prophet˺ as a witness against yours?
