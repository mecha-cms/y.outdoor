### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱنظُرْ كَيْفَ يَفْتَرُونَ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ ٱلْكَذِبَ ۖ وَكَفَىٰ بِهِۦٓ إِثْمًا مُّبِينًا</span>

### English

See how they fabricate lies against Allah—this alone is a blatant sin.
