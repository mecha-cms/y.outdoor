### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِن يَشَأْ يُذْهِبْكُمْ أَيُّهَا ٱلنَّاسُ وَيَأْتِ بِـَٔاخَرِينَ ۚ وَكَانَ ٱللَّهُ عَلَىٰ ذَٰلِكَ قَدِيرًا</span>

### English

If it is His Will, He can remove you altogether, O  humanity, and replace you with others. And Allah is Most Capable to do so.
