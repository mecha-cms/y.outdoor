### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِن تُبْدُوا۟ خَيْرًا أَوْ تُخْفُوهُ أَوْ تَعْفُوا۟ عَن سُوٓءٍ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ كَانَ عَفُوًّا قَدِيرًا</span>

### English

Whether you reveal or conceal a good or pardon an evil—surely Allah is Ever-Pardoning, Most Capable.
