### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا تُجَـٰدِلْ عَنِ ٱلَّذِينَ يَخْتَانُونَ أَنفُسَهُمْ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَا يُحِبُّ مَن كَانَ خَوَّانًا أَثِيمًا</span>

### English

Do not advocate for those who wrong themselves. Surely Allah does not like those who are deceitful, sinful.
