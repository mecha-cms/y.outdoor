### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَمْ لَهُمْ نَصِيبٌ مِّنَ ٱلْمُلْكِ فَإِذًا لَّا يُؤْتُونَ ٱلنَّاسَ نَقِيرًا</span>

### English

Do they have control over shares of the kingdom? If so, they would not have given anyone so much as the speck on a date stone.
