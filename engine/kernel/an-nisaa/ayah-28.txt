### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يُرِيدُ ٱللَّهُ أَن يُخَفِّفَ عَنكُمْ ۚ وَخُلِقَ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنُ ضَعِيفًا</span>

### English

And it is Allah’s Will to lighten your burdens, for humankind was created weak.
