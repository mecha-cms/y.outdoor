### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَن يَفْعَلْ ذَٰلِكَ عُدْوَٰنًا وَظُلْمًا فَسَوْفَ نُصْلِيهِ نَارًا ۚ وَكَانَ ذَٰلِكَ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ يَسِيرًا</span>

### English

And whoever does this sinfully and unjustly, We will burn them in the Fire. That is easy for Allah.
