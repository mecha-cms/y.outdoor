### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقَالُوٓا۟ أَءِذَا كُنَّا عِظَـٰمًا وَرُفَـٰتًا أَءِنَّا لَمَبْعُوثُونَ خَلْقًا جَدِيدًا</span>

### English

And they say ˹mockingly˺, “When we are reduced to bones and ashes, will we really be raised as a new creation?”
