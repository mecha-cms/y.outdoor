### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">سُنَّةَ مَن قَدْ أَرْسَلْنَا قَبْلَكَ مِن رُّسُلِنَا ۖ وَلَا تَجِدُ لِسُنَّتِنَا تَحْوِيلًا</span>

### English

˹This has been˺ Our way with the messengers We sent before you. And you will never find any change in Our way.
