### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا تَقْتُلُوٓا۟ أَوْلَـٰدَكُمْ خَشْيَةَ إِمْلَـٰقٍ ۖ نَّحْنُ نَرْزُقُهُمْ وَإِيَّاكُمْ ۚ إِنَّ قَتْلَهُمْ كَانَ خِطْـًٔا كَبِيرًا</span>

### English

Do not kill your children for fear of poverty. We provide for them and for you. Surely killing them is a heinous sin.
