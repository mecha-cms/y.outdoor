### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">كُلًّا نُّمِدُّ هَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ وَهَـٰٓؤُلَآءِ مِنْ عَطَآءِ رَبِّكَ ۚ وَمَا كَانَ عَطَآءُ رَبِّكَ مَحْظُورًا</span>

### English

We provide both the former and the latter from the bounty of your Lord. And the bounty of your Lord can never be withheld.
