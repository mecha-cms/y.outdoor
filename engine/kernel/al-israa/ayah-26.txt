### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَءَاتِ ذَا ٱلْقُرْبَىٰ حَقَّهُۥ وَٱلْمِسْكِينَ وَٱبْنَ ٱلسَّبِيلِ وَلَا تُبَذِّرْ تَبْذِيرًا</span>

### English

Give to close relatives their due, as well as the poor and ˹needy˺ travellers. And do not spend wastefully.
