### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا مَنَعَ ٱلنَّاسَ أَن يُؤْمِنُوٓا۟ إِذْ جَآءَهُمُ ٱلْهُدَىٰٓ إِلَّآ أَن قَالُوٓا۟ أَبَعَثَ ٱللَّهُ بَشَرًا رَّسُولًا</span>

### English

And nothing has prevented people from believing when guidance comes to them except their protest: “Has Allah sent a human as a messenger?”
