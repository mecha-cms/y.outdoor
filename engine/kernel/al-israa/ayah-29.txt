### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا تَجْعَلْ يَدَكَ مَغْلُولَةً إِلَىٰ عُنُقِكَ وَلَا تَبْسُطْهَا كُلَّ ٱلْبَسْطِ فَتَقْعُدَ مَلُومًا مَّحْسُورًا</span>

### English

Do not be so tight-fisted, for you will be blameworthy; nor so open-handed, for you will end up in poverty.
