### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِذَآ أَنْعَمْنَا عَلَى ٱلْإِنسَـٰنِ أَعْرَضَ وَنَـَٔا بِجَانِبِهِۦ ۖ وَإِذَا مَسَّهُ ٱلشَّرُّ كَانَ يَـُٔوسًا</span>

### English

When We grant people Our favours, they turn away, acting arrogantly. But when touched with evil, they lose all hope.
