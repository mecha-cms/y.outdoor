### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱخْفِضْ لَهُمَا جَنَاحَ ٱلذُّلِّ مِنَ ٱلرَّحْمَةِ وَقُل رَّبِّ ٱرْحَمْهُمَا كَمَا رَبَّيَانِى صَغِيرًا</span>

### English

And be humble with them out of mercy, and pray, “My Lord! Be merciful to them as they raised me when I was young.”
