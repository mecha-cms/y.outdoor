### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمِنَ ٱلَّيْلِ فَتَهَجَّدْ بِهِۦ نَافِلَةً لَّكَ عَسَىٰٓ أَن يَبْعَثَكَ رَبُّكَ مَقَامًا مَّحْمُودًا</span>

### English

And rise at ˹the last˺ part of the night, offering additional prayers, so your Lord may raise you to a station of praise.
