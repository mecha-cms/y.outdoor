### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَنُنَزِّلُ مِنَ ٱلْقُرْءَانِ مَا هُوَ شِفَآءٌ وَرَحْمَةٌ لِّلْمُؤْمِنِينَ ۙ وَلَا يَزِيدُ ٱلظَّـٰلِمِينَ إِلَّا خَسَارًا</span>

### English

We send down the Quran as a healing and mercy for the believers, but it only increases the wrongdoers in loss.
