### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقُل رَّبِّ أَدْخِلْنِى مُدْخَلَ صِدْقٍ وَأَخْرِجْنِى مُخْرَجَ صِدْقٍ وَٱجْعَل لِّى مِن لَّدُنكَ سُلْطَـٰنًا نَّصِيرًا</span>

### English

And say, “My Lord! Grant me an honourable entrance and an honourable exit and give me a supporting authority from Yourself.”
