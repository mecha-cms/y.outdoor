### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱنظُرْ كَيْفَ فَضَّلْنَا بَعْضَهُمْ عَلَىٰ بَعْضٍ ۚ وَلَلْـَٔاخِرَةُ أَكْبَرُ دَرَجَـٰتٍ وَأَكْبَرُ تَفْضِيلًا</span>

### English

See how We have favoured some over others ˹in this life˺, but the Hereafter is certainly far greater in rank and in favour.
