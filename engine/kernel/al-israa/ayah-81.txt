### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقُلْ جَآءَ ٱلْحَقُّ وَزَهَقَ ٱلْبَـٰطِلُ ۚ إِنَّ ٱلْبَـٰطِلَ كَانَ زَهُوقًا</span>

### English

And declare, “The truth has come and falsehood has vanished. Indeed, falsehood is bound to vanish.”
