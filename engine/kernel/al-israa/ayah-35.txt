### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَوْفُوا۟ ٱلْكَيْلَ إِذَا كِلْتُمْ وَزِنُوا۟ بِٱلْقِسْطَاسِ ٱلْمُسْتَقِيمِ ۚ ذَٰلِكَ خَيْرٌ وَأَحْسَنُ تَأْوِيلًا</span>

### English

Give in full when you measure, and weigh with an even balance. That is fairest and best in the end.
