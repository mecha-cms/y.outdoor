### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا تَقْرَبُوا۟ ٱلزِّنَىٰٓ ۖ إِنَّهُۥ كَانَ فَـٰحِشَةً وَسَآءَ سَبِيلًا</span>

### English

Do not go near adultery. It is truly a shameful deed and an evil way.
