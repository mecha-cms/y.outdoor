### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَيَدْعُ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنُ بِٱلشَّرِّ دُعَآءَهُۥ بِٱلْخَيْرِ ۖ وَكَانَ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنُ عَجُولًا</span>

### English

And humans ˹swiftly˺ pray for evil as they pray for good. For humankind is ever hasty.
