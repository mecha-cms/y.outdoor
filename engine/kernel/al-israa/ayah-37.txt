### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا تَمْشِ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ مَرَحًا ۖ إِنَّكَ لَن تَخْرِقَ ٱلْأَرْضَ وَلَن تَبْلُغَ ٱلْجِبَالَ طُولًا</span>

### English

And do not walk on the earth arrogantly. Surely you can neither crack the earth nor stretch to the height of the mountains.
