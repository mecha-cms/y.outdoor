### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَّا تَجْعَلْ مَعَ ٱللَّهِ إِلَـٰهًا ءَاخَرَ فَتَقْعُدَ مَذْمُومًا مَّخْذُولًا</span>

### English

Do not set up any other god with Allah, or you will end up condemned, abandoned.
