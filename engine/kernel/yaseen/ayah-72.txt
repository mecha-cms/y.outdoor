### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَذَلَّلْنَـٰهَا لَهُمْ فَمِنْهَا رَكُوبُهُمْ وَمِنْهَا يَأْكُلُونَ</span>

### English

And We have subjected these ˹animals˺ to them, so they may ride some and eat others.
