### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَسَوَآءٌ عَلَيْهِمْ ءَأَنذَرْتَهُمْ أَمْ لَمْ تُنذِرْهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ</span>

### English

It is the same whether you warn them or not—they will never believe.
