### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّمَآ أَمْرُهُۥٓ إِذَآ أَرَادَ شَيْـًٔا أَن يَقُولَ لَهُۥ كُن فَيَكُونُ</span>

### English

All it takes, when He wills something ˹to be˺, is simply to say to it: “Be!” And it is!
