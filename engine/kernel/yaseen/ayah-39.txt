### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱلْقَمَرَ قَدَّرْنَـٰهُ مَنَازِلَ حَتَّىٰ عَادَ كَٱلْعُرْجُونِ ٱلْقَدِيمِ</span>

### English

As for the moon, We have ordained ˹precise˺ phases for it, until it ends up like an old, curved palm stalk.
