### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ وَمَآ أَنزَلْنَا عَلَىٰ قَوْمِهِۦ مِنۢ بَعْدِهِۦ مِن جُندٍ مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ وَمَا كُنَّا مُنزِلِينَ</span>

### English

We did not send any soldiers from the heavens against his people after his death, nor did We need to.
