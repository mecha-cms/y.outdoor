### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَنُفِخَ فِى ٱلصُّورِ فَإِذَا هُم مِّنَ ٱلْأَجْدَاثِ إِلَىٰ رَبِّهِمْ يَنسِلُونَ</span>

### English

The Trumpet will be blown ˹a second time˺, then—behold!—they will rush from the graves to their Lord.
