### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَءَايَةٌ لَّهُمُ ٱلَّيْلُ نَسْلَخُ مِنْهُ ٱلنَّهَارَ فَإِذَا هُم مُّظْلِمُونَ</span>

### English

There is also a sign for them in the night: We strip from it daylight, then—behold!—they are in darkness.
