### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَن نُّعَمِّرْهُ نُنَكِّسْهُ فِى ٱلْخَلْقِ ۖ أَفَلَا يَعْقِلُونَ</span>

### English

And whoever We grant a long life, We reverse them in development. Will they not then understand?
