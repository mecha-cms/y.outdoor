### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِن كَانَتْ إِلَّا صَيْحَةً وَٰحِدَةً فَإِذَا هُمْ جَمِيعٌ لَّدَيْنَا مُحْضَرُونَ</span>

### English

It will only take one Blast, then at once they will all be brought before Us.
