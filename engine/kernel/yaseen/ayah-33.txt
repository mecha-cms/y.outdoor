### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَءَايَةٌ لَّهُمُ ٱلْأَرْضُ ٱلْمَيْتَةُ أَحْيَيْنَـٰهَا وَأَخْرَجْنَا مِنْهَا حَبًّا فَمِنْهُ يَأْكُلُونَ</span>

### English

There is a sign for them in the dead earth: We give it life, producing grain from it for them to eat.
