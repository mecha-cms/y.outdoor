### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَوْ نَشَآءُ لَمَسَخْنَـٰهُمْ عَلَىٰ مَكَانَتِهِمْ فَمَا ٱسْتَطَـٰعُوا۟ مُضِيًّا وَلَا يَرْجِعُونَ</span>

### English

And had We willed, We could have transfigured them on the spot, so they could neither progress forward nor turn back.
