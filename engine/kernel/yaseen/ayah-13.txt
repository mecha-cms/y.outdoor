### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱضْرِبْ لَهُم مَّثَلًا أَصْحَـٰبَ ٱلْقَرْيَةِ إِذْ جَآءَهَا ٱلْمُرْسَلُونَ</span>

### English

Give them an example ˹O Prophet˺ of the residents of a town, when the messengers came to them.
