### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَـٰحَسْرَةً عَلَى ٱلْعِبَادِ ۚ مَا يَأْتِيهِم مِّن رَّسُولٍ إِلَّا كَانُوا۟ بِهِۦ يَسْتَهْزِءُونَ</span>

### English

Oh pity, such beings! No messenger ever came to them without being mocked.
