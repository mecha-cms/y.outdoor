### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَٱلْيَوْمَ لَا تُظْلَمُ نَفْسٌ شَيْـًٔا وَلَا تُجْزَوْنَ إِلَّا مَا كُنتُمْ تَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

On that Day no soul will be wronged in the least, nor will you be rewarded except for what you used to do.
