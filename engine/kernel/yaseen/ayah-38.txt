### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱلشَّمْسُ تَجْرِى لِمُسْتَقَرٍّ لَّهَا ۚ ذَٰلِكَ تَقْدِيرُ ٱلْعَزِيزِ ٱلْعَلِيمِ</span>

### English

The sun travels for its fixed term. That is the design of the Almighty, All-Knowing.
