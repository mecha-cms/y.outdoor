### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَهُمْ فِيهَا مَنَـٰفِعُ وَمَشَارِبُ ۖ أَفَلَا يَشْكُرُونَ</span>

### English

And they derive from them other benefits and drinks. Will they not then give thanks?
