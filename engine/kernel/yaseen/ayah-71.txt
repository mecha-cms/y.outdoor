### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَوَلَمْ يَرَوْا۟ أَنَّا خَلَقْنَا لَهُم مِّمَّا عَمِلَتْ أَيْدِينَآ أَنْعَـٰمًا فَهُمْ لَهَا مَـٰلِكُونَ</span>

### English

Do they not see that We singlehandedly created for them, among other things, cattle which are under their control?
