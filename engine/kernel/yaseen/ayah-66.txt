### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَوْ نَشَآءُ لَطَمَسْنَا عَلَىٰٓ أَعْيُنِهِمْ فَٱسْتَبَقُوا۟ ٱلصِّرَٰطَ فَأَنَّىٰ يُبْصِرُونَ</span>

### English

Had We willed, We could have easily blinded their eyes, so they would struggle to find their way. How then could they see?
