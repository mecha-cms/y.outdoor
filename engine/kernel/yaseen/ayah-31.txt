### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَلَمْ يَرَوْا۟ كَمْ أَهْلَكْنَا قَبْلَهُم مِّنَ ٱلْقُرُونِ أَنَّهُمْ إِلَيْهِمْ لَا يَرْجِعُونَ</span>

### English

Have the deniers not considered how many peoples We destroyed before them who never came back to life again?
