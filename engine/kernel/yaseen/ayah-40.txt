### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَا ٱلشَّمْسُ يَنۢبَغِى لَهَآ أَن تُدْرِكَ ٱلْقَمَرَ وَلَا ٱلَّيْلُ سَابِقُ ٱلنَّهَارِ ۚ وَكُلٌّ فِى فَلَكٍ يَسْبَحُونَ</span>

### English

It is not for the sun to catch up with the moon, nor does the night outrun the day. Each is travelling in an orbit of their own.
