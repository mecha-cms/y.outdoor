### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَا يَسْتَطِيعُونَ نَصْرَهُمْ وَهُمْ لَهُمْ جُندٌ مُّحْضَرُونَ</span>

### English

They cannot help the pagans, even though they serve the idols as dedicated guards.
