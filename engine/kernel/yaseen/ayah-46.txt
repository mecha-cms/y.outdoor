### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا تَأْتِيهِم مِّنْ ءَايَةٍ مِّنْ ءَايَـٰتِ رَبِّهِمْ إِلَّا كَانُوا۟ عَنْهَا مُعْرِضِينَ</span>

### English

Whenever a sign comes to them from their Lord, they turn away from it.
