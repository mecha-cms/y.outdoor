### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَقَدْ حَقَّ ٱلْقَوْلُ عَلَىٰٓ أَكْثَرِهِمْ فَهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ</span>

### English

The decree ˹of torment˺ has already been justified against most of them, for they will never believe.
