### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا لِىَ لَآ أَعْبُدُ ٱلَّذِى فَطَرَنِى وَإِلَيْهِ تُرْجَعُونَ</span>

### English

And why should I not worship the One Who has originated me, and to Whom you will be returned.
