### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّ أَصْحَـٰبَ ٱلْجَنَّةِ ٱلْيَوْمَ فِى شُغُلٍ فَـٰكِهُونَ</span>

### English

Indeed, on that Day the residents of Paradise will be busy enjoying themselves.
