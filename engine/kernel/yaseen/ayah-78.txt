### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَضَرَبَ لَنَا مَثَلًا وَنَسِىَ خَلْقَهُۥ ۖ قَالَ مَن يُحْىِ ٱلْعِظَـٰمَ وَهِىَ رَمِيمٌ</span>

### English

And they argue with Us—forgetting they were created—saying, “Who will give life to decayed bones?”
