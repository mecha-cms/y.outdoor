### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَوَلَمْ يَرَ ٱلْإِنسَـٰنُ أَنَّا خَلَقْنَـٰهُ مِن نُّطْفَةٍ فَإِذَا هُوَ خَصِيمٌ مُّبِينٌ</span>

### English

Do people not see that We have created them from a sperm-drop, then—behold!—they openly challenge ˹Us˺?
