### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">كَذَٰلِكَ يُبَيِّنُ ٱللَّهُ لَكُمْ ءَايَـٰتِهِۦ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَعْقِلُونَ</span>

### English

This is how Allah makes His revelations clear to you, so perhaps you will understand.
