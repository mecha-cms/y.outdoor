### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَقِيمُوا۟ ٱلصَّلَوٰةَ وَءَاتُوا۟ ٱلزَّكَوٰةَ وَٱرْكَعُوا۟ مَعَ ٱلرَّٰكِعِينَ</span>

### English

Establish prayer, pay alms-tax, and bow down with those who bow down.
