### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">خَـٰلِدِينَ فِيهَا ۖ لَا يُخَفَّفُ عَنْهُمُ ٱلْعَذَابُ وَلَا هُمْ يُنظَرُونَ</span>

### English

They will be in Hell forever. Their punishment will not be lightened, nor will they be delayed ˹from it˺.
