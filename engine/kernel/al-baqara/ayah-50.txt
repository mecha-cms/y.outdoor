### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِذْ فَرَقْنَا بِكُمُ ٱلْبَحْرَ فَأَنجَيْنَـٰكُمْ وَأَغْرَقْنَآ ءَالَ فِرْعَوْنَ وَأَنتُمْ تَنظُرُونَ</span>

### English

And ˹remember˺ when We parted the sea, rescued you, and drowned Pharaoh’s people before your very eyes.
