### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِذْ قَتَلْتُمْ نَفْسًا فَٱدَّٰرَْٰٔتُمْ فِيهَا ۖ وَٱللَّهُ مُخْرِجٌ مَّا كُنتُمْ تَكْتُمُونَ</span>

### English

˹This is˺ when a man was killed and you disputed who the killer was, but Allah revealed what you concealed.
