### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا تَلْبِسُوا۟ ٱلْحَقَّ بِٱلْبَـٰطِلِ وَتَكْتُمُوا۟ ٱلْحَقَّ وَأَنتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ</span>

### English

Do not mix truth with falsehood or hide the truth knowingly.
