### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ثُمَّ بَعَثْنَـٰكُم مِّنۢ بَعْدِ مَوْتِكُمْ لَعَلَّكُمْ تَشْكُرُونَ</span>

### English

Then We brought you back to life after your death, so that perhaps you would be grateful.
