### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَوَكُلَّمَا عَـٰهَدُوا۟ عَهْدًا نَّبَذَهُۥ فَرِيقٌ مِّنْهُم ۚ بَلْ أَكْثَرُهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ</span>

### English

Why is it that every time they make a covenant, a group of them casts it aside? In fact, most of them do not believe.
