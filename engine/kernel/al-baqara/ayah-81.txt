### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">بَلَىٰ مَن كَسَبَ سَيِّئَةً وَأَحَـٰطَتْ بِهِۦ خَطِيٓـَٔتُهُۥ فَأُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ أَصْحَـٰبُ ٱلنَّارِ ۖ هُمْ فِيهَا خَـٰلِدُونَ</span>

### English

But no! Those who commit evil and are engrossed in sin will be the residents of the Fire. They will be there forever.
