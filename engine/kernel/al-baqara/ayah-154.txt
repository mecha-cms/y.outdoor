### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا تَقُولُوا۟ لِمَن يُقْتَلُ فِى سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ أَمْوَٰتٌۢ ۚ بَلْ أَحْيَآءٌ وَلَـٰكِن لَّا تَشْعُرُونَ</span>

### English

Never say that those martyred in the cause of Allah are dead—in fact, they are alive! But you do not perceive it.
