### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلِلْمُطَلَّقَـٰتِ مَتَـٰعٌۢ بِٱلْمَعْرُوفِ ۖ حَقًّا عَلَى ٱلْمُتَّقِينَ</span>

### English

Reasonable provisions must be made for divorced women—a duty on those mindful ˹of Allah˺.
