### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ عَلَيْهِمْ صَلَوَٰتٌ مِّن رَّبِّهِمْ وَرَحْمَةٌ ۖ وَأُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْمُهْتَدُونَ</span>

### English

They are the ones who will receive Allah’s blessings and mercy. And it is they who are ˹rightly˺ guided.
