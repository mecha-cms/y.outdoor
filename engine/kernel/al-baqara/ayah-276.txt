### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَمْحَقُ ٱللَّهُ ٱلرِّبَوٰا۟ وَيُرْبِى ٱلصَّدَقَـٰتِ ۗ وَٱللَّهُ لَا يُحِبُّ كُلَّ كَفَّارٍ أَثِيمٍ</span>

### English

Allah has made interest fruitless and charity fruitful. And Allah does not like any ungrateful evildoer.
