### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ قَوْلٌ مَّعْرُوفٌ وَمَغْفِرَةٌ خَيْرٌ مِّن صَدَقَةٍ يَتْبَعُهَآ أَذًى ۗ وَٱللَّهُ غَنِىٌّ حَلِيمٌ</span>

### English

Kind words and forgiveness are better than charity followed by injury. And Allah is Self-Sufficient, Most Forbearing.
