### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ وَلَقَدْ جَآءَكُم مُّوسَىٰ بِٱلْبَيِّنَـٰتِ ثُمَّ ٱتَّخَذْتُمُ ٱلْعِجْلَ مِنۢ بَعْدِهِۦ وَأَنتُمْ ظَـٰلِمُونَ</span>

### English

Indeed, Moses came to you with clear proofs, then you worshipped the calf in his absence, acting wrongfully.
