### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">خَتَمَ ٱللَّهُ عَلَىٰ قُلُوبِهِمْ وَعَلَىٰ سَمْعِهِمْ ۖ وَعَلَىٰٓ أَبْصَـٰرِهِمْ غِشَـٰوَةٌ ۖ وَلَهُمْ عَذَابٌ عَظِيمٌ</span>

### English

Allah has sealed their hearts and their hearing, and their sight is covered. They will suffer a tremendous punishment.
