### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ ٱلَّذِينَ ٱشْتَرَوُا۟ ٱلضَّلَـٰلَةَ بِٱلْهُدَىٰ فَمَا رَبِحَت تِّجَـٰرَتُهُمْ وَمَا كَانُوا۟ مُهْتَدِينَ</span>

### English

They are the ones who trade guidance for misguidance. But this trade is profitless, and they are not ˹rightly˺ guided.
