### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِنْ عَزَمُوا۟ ٱلطَّلَـٰقَ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ سَمِيعٌ عَلِيمٌ</span>

### English

But if they settle on divorce, then Allah is indeed All-Hearing, All-Knowing.
