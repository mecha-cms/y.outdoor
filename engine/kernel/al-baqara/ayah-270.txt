### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَآ أَنفَقْتُم مِّن نَّفَقَةٍ أَوْ نَذَرْتُم مِّن نَّذْرٍ فَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ يَعْلَمُهُۥ ۗ وَمَا لِلظَّـٰلِمِينَ مِنْ أَنصَارٍ</span>

### English

Whatever charities you give or vows you make are surely known to Allah. And the wrongdoers will have no helpers.
