### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَجَعَلْنَـٰهَا نَكَـٰلًا لِّمَا بَيْنَ يَدَيْهَا وَمَا خَلْفَهَا وَمَوْعِظَةً لِّلْمُتَّقِينَ</span>

### English

So We made their fate an example to present and future generations, and a lesson to the God-fearing.
