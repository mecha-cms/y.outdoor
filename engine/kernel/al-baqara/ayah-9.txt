### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يُخَـٰدِعُونَ ٱللَّهَ وَٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَمَا يَخْدَعُونَ إِلَّآ أَنفُسَهُمْ وَمَا يَشْعُرُونَ</span>

### English

They seek to deceive Allah and the believers, yet they only deceive themselves, but they fail to perceive it.
