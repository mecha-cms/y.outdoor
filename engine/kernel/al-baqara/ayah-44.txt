### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ أَتَأْمُرُونَ ٱلنَّاسَ بِٱلْبِرِّ وَتَنسَوْنَ أَنفُسَكُمْ وَأَنتُمْ تَتْلُونَ ٱلْكِتَـٰبَ ۚ أَفَلَا تَعْقِلُونَ</span>

### English

Do you preach righteousness and fail to practice it yourselves, although you read the Scripture? Do you not understand?
