### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمِنَ ٱلنَّاسِ مَن يَقُولُ ءَامَنَّا بِٱللَّهِ وَبِٱلْيَوْمِ ٱلْـَٔاخِرِ وَمَا هُم بِمُؤْمِنِينَ</span>

### English

And there are some who say, “We believe in Allah and the Last Day,” yet they are not ˹true˺ believers.
