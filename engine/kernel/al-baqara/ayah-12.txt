### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَلَآ إِنَّهُمْ هُمُ ٱلْمُفْسِدُونَ وَلَـٰكِن لَّا يَشْعُرُونَ</span>

### English

Indeed, it is they who are the corruptors, but they fail to perceive it.
