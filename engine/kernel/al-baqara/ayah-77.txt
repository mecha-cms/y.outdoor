### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَوَلَا يَعْلَمُونَ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ يَعْلَمُ مَا يُسِرُّونَ وَمَا يُعْلِنُونَ</span>

### English

Do they not know that Allah is aware of what they conceal and what they reveal?
