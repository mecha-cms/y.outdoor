### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّمَا يَأْمُرُكُم بِٱلسُّوٓءِ وَٱلْفَحْشَآءِ وَأَن تَقُولُوا۟ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ مَا لَا تَعْلَمُونَ</span>

### English

He only incites you to commit evil and indecency, and to claim against Allah what you do not know.
