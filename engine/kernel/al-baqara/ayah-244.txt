### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقَـٰتِلُوا۟ فِى سَبِيلِ ٱللَّهِ وَٱعْلَمُوٓا۟ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ سَمِيعٌ عَلِيمٌ</span>

### English

Fight in the cause of Allah, and know that Allah is All-Hearing, All-Knowing.
