### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">حَـٰفِظُوا۟ عَلَى ٱلصَّلَوَٰتِ وَٱلصَّلَوٰةِ ٱلْوُسْطَىٰ وَقُومُوا۟ لِلَّهِ قَـٰنِتِينَ</span>

### English

Observe the ˹five obligatory˺ prayers—especially the middle prayer—and stand in true devotion to Allah.
