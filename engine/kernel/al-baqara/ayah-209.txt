### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَإِن زَلَلْتُم مِّنۢ بَعْدِ مَا جَآءَتْكُمُ ٱلْبَيِّنَـٰتُ فَٱعْلَمُوٓا۟ أَنَّ ٱللَّهَ عَزِيزٌ حَكِيمٌ</span>

### English

If you falter after receiving the clear proofs, then know that Allah is indeed Almighty, All-Wise.
