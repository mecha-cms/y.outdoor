### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِى جَعَلَ لَكُمُ ٱلَّيْلَ لِبَاسًا وَٱلنَّوْمَ سُبَاتًا وَجَعَلَ ٱلنَّهَارَ نُشُورًا</span>

### English

He is the One Who has made the night for you as a cover, and ˹made˺ sleep for resting, and the day for rising.
