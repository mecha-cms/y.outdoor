### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يُضَـٰعَفْ لَهُ ٱلْعَذَابُ يَوْمَ ٱلْقِيَـٰمَةِ وَيَخْلُدْ فِيهِۦ مُهَانًا</span>

### English

Their punishment will be multiplied on the Day of Judgment, and they will remain in it forever, in disgrace.
