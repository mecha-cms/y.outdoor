### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقَالَ ٱلرَّسُولُ يَـٰرَبِّ إِنَّ قَوْمِى ٱتَّخَذُوا۟ هَـٰذَا ٱلْقُرْءَانَ مَهْجُورًا</span>

### English

The Messenger has cried, “O my Lord! My people have indeed received this Quran with neglect.”
