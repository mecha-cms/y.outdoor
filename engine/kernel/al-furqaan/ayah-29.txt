### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَّقَدْ أَضَلَّنِى عَنِ ٱلذِّكْرِ بَعْدَ إِذْ جَآءَنِى ۗ وَكَانَ ٱلشَّيْطَـٰنُ لِلْإِنسَـٰنِ خَذُولًا</span>

### English

It was he who truly made me stray from the Reminder after it had reached me.” And Satan has always betrayed humanity.
