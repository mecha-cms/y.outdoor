### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">تَبَارَكَ ٱلَّذِى نَزَّلَ ٱلْفُرْقَانَ عَلَىٰ عَبْدِهِۦ لِيَكُونَ لِلْعَـٰلَمِينَ نَذِيرًا</span>

### English

Blessed is the One Who sent down the Standard to His servant, so that he may be a warner to the whole world.
