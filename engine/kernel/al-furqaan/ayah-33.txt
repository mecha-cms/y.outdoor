### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا يَأْتُونَكَ بِمَثَلٍ إِلَّا جِئْنَـٰكَ بِٱلْحَقِّ وَأَحْسَنَ تَفْسِيرًا</span>

### English

Whenever they bring you an argument, We come to you with the right refutation and the best explanation.
