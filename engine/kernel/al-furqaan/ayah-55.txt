### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَيَعْبُدُونَ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ مَا لَا يَنفَعُهُمْ وَلَا يَضُرُّهُمْ ۗ وَكَانَ ٱلْكَافِرُ عَلَىٰ رَبِّهِۦ ظَهِيرًا</span>

### English

Yet they worship besides Allah what can neither benefit nor harm them. And the disbeliever always collaborates against their Lord.
