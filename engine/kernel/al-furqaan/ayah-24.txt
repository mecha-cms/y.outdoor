### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَصْحَـٰبُ ٱلْجَنَّةِ يَوْمَئِذٍ خَيْرٌ مُّسْتَقَرًّا وَأَحْسَنُ مَقِيلًا</span>

### English

˹But˺ on that Day the residents of Paradise will have the best settlement and the finest place to rest.
