### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِذَا رَأَتْهُم مِّن مَّكَانٍۭ بَعِيدٍ سَمِعُوا۟ لَهَا تَغَيُّظًا وَزَفِيرًا</span>

### English

Once it sees them from a distance, they will hear it fuming and growling.
