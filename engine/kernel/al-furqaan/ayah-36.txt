### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَقُلْنَا ٱذْهَبَآ إِلَى ٱلْقَوْمِ ٱلَّذِينَ كَذَّبُوا۟ بِـَٔايَـٰتِنَا فَدَمَّرْنَـٰهُمْ تَدْمِيرًا</span>

### English

We had ordered ˹them˺, “Go to the people who would deny Our signs.” Then We annihilated the deniers entirely.
