### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱلَّذِينَ لَا يَشْهَدُونَ ٱلزُّورَ وَإِذَا مَرُّوا۟ بِٱللَّغْوِ مَرُّوا۟ كِرَامًا</span>

### English

˹They are˺ those who do not bear false witness, and when they come across falsehood, they pass ˹it˺ by with dignity.
