### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقَدِمْنَآ إِلَىٰ مَا عَمِلُوا۟ مِنْ عَمَلٍ فَجَعَلْنَـٰهُ هَبَآءً مَّنثُورًا</span>

### English

Then We will turn to whatever ˹good˺ deeds they did, reducing them to scattered dust.
