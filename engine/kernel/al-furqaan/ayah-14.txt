### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَّا تَدْعُوا۟ ٱلْيَوْمَ ثُبُورًا وَٰحِدًا وَٱدْعُوا۟ ثُبُورًا كَثِيرًا</span>

### English

˹They will be told,˺ “Do not cry only once for destruction, but cry many times over!”
