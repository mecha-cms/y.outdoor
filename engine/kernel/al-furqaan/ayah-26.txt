### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱلْمُلْكُ يَوْمَئِذٍ ٱلْحَقُّ لِلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ۚ وَكَانَ يَوْمًا عَلَى ٱلْكَـٰفِرِينَ عَسِيرًا</span>

### English

True authority on that Day will belong ˹only˺ to the Most Compassionate. And it will be a hard day for the disbelievers.
