### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَّهُمْ فِيهَا مَا يَشَآءُونَ خَـٰلِدِينَ ۚ كَانَ عَلَىٰ رَبِّكَ وَعْدًا مَّسْـُٔولًا</span>

### English

There they will have whatever they wish for, forever. That is a promise ˹to be sought after˺, binding on your Lord.”
