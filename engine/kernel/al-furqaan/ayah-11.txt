### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">بَلْ كَذَّبُوا۟ بِٱلسَّاعَةِ ۖ وَأَعْتَدْنَا لِمَن كَذَّبَ بِٱلسَّاعَةِ سَعِيرًا</span>

### English

In fact, they deny the Hour. And for the deniers of the Hour, We have prepared a blazing Fire.
