### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَرَءَيْتَ مَنِ ٱتَّخَذَ إِلَـٰهَهُۥ هَوَىٰهُ أَفَأَنتَ تَكُونُ عَلَيْهِ وَكِيلًا</span>

### English

Have you seen ˹O Prophet˺ the one who has taken their own desires as their god? Will you then be a keeper over them?
