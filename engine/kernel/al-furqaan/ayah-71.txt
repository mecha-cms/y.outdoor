### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَن تَابَ وَعَمِلَ صَـٰلِحًا فَإِنَّهُۥ يَتُوبُ إِلَى ٱللَّهِ مَتَابًا</span>

### English

And whoever repents and does good has truly turned to Allah properly.
