### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَأَخَذَهُ ٱللَّهُ نَكَالَ ٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ وَٱلْأُولَىٰٓ</span>
