### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">كَأَنَّهُمْ يَوْمَ يَرَوْنَهَا لَمْ يَلْبَثُوٓا۟ إِلَّا عَشِيَّةً أَوْ ضُحَىٰهَا</span>

### English

On the Day they see it, it will be as if they had stayed ˹in the world˺ no more than one evening or its morning.
