### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَلَمْ تَرَ إِلَى ٱلَّذِينَ يُجَـٰدِلُونَ فِىٓ ءَايَـٰتِ ٱللَّهِ أَنَّىٰ يُصْرَفُونَ</span>

### English

Have you not seen how those who dispute Allah’s signs are turned away?
