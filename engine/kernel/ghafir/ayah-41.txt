### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ وَيَـٰقَوْمِ مَا لِىٓ أَدْعُوكُمْ إِلَى ٱلنَّجَوٰةِ وَتَدْعُونَنِىٓ إِلَى ٱلنَّارِ</span>

### English

O  my people! How is it that I invite you to salvation, while you invite me to the Fire!
