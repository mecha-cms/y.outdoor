### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱدْخُلُوٓا۟ أَبْوَٰبَ جَهَنَّمَ خَـٰلِدِينَ فِيهَا ۖ فَبِئْسَ مَثْوَى ٱلْمُتَكَبِّرِينَ</span>

### English

Enter the gates of Hell, to stay there forever. What an evil home for the arrogant!”
