### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">مَا يُجَـٰدِلُ فِىٓ ءَايَـٰتِ ٱللَّهِ إِلَّا ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ فَلَا يَغْرُرْكَ تَقَلُّبُهُمْ فِى ٱلْبِلَـٰدِ</span>

### English

None disputes the signs of Allah except the disbelievers, so do not be deceived by their prosperity throughout the land.
