### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَٱدْعُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ مُخْلِصِينَ لَهُ ٱلدِّينَ وَلَوْ كَرِهَ ٱلْكَـٰفِرُونَ</span>

### English

So call upon Allah with sincere devotion, even to the dismay of the disbelievers.
