### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَيُرِيكُمْ ءَايَـٰتِهِۦ فَأَىَّ ءَايَـٰتِ ٱللَّهِ تُنكِرُونَ</span>

### English

And He shows you His signs. Now which of Allah’s signs will you deny?
