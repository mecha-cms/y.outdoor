### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَخَلْقُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ أَكْبَرُ مِنْ خَلْقِ ٱلنَّاسِ وَلَـٰكِنَّ أَكْثَرَ ٱلنَّاسِ لَا يَعْلَمُونَ</span>

### English

The creation of the heavens and the earth is certainly greater than the re-creation of humankind, but most people do not know.
