### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّ ٱلسَّاعَةَ لَـَٔاتِيَةٌ لَّا رَيْبَ فِيهَا وَلَـٰكِنَّ أَكْثَرَ ٱلنَّاسِ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ</span>

### English

The Hour is certainly coming, there is no doubt about it. But most people do not believe.
