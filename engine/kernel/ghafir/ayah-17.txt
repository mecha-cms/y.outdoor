### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱلْيَوْمَ تُجْزَىٰ كُلُّ نَفْسٍۭ بِمَا كَسَبَتْ ۚ لَا ظُلْمَ ٱلْيَوْمَ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ سَرِيعُ ٱلْحِسَابِ</span>

### English

Today every soul will be rewarded for what it has done. No injustice Today! Surely Allah is swift in reckoning.”
