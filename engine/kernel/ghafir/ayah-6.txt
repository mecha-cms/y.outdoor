### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَكَذَٰلِكَ حَقَّتْ كَلِمَتُ رَبِّكَ عَلَى ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوٓا۟ أَنَّهُمْ أَصْحَـٰبُ ٱلنَّارِ</span>

### English

And so your Lord’s decree has been proven true against the disbelievers—that they will be the inmates of the Fire.
