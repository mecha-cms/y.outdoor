### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَمَن يَعْمَلْ مِثْقَالَ ذَرَّةٍ خَيْرًا يَرَهُۥ</span>

### English

So whoever does an atom’s weight of good will see it.
