### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">رَبَّنَا ٱغْفِرْ لِى وَلِوَٰلِدَىَّ وَلِلْمُؤْمِنِينَ يَوْمَ يَقُومُ ٱلْحِسَابُ</span>

### English

Our Lord! Forgive me, my parents, and the believers on the Day when the judgment will come to pass.”
