### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَسَخَّرَ لَكُمُ ٱلشَّمْسَ وَٱلْقَمَرَ دَآئِبَيْنِ ۖ وَسَخَّرَ لَكُمُ ٱلَّيْلَ وَٱلنَّهَارَ</span>

### English

He has ˹also˺ subjected for you the sun and the moon, both constantly orbiting, and has subjected the day and night for you.
