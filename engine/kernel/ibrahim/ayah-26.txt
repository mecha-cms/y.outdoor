### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَثَلُ كَلِمَةٍ خَبِيثَةٍ كَشَجَرَةٍ خَبِيثَةٍ ٱجْتُثَّتْ مِن فَوْقِ ٱلْأَرْضِ مَا لَهَا مِن قَرَارٍ</span>

### English

And the parable of an evil word is that of an evil tree, uprooted from the earth, having no stability.
