### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لِيَجْزِىَ ٱللَّهُ كُلَّ نَفْسٍ مَّا كَسَبَتْ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ سَرِيعُ ٱلْحِسَابِ</span>

### English

As such, Allah will reward every soul for what it has committed. Surely Allah is swift in reckoning.
