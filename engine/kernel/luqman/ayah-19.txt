### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَٱقْصِدْ فِى مَشْيِكَ وَٱغْضُضْ مِن صَوْتِكَ ۚ إِنَّ أَنكَرَ ٱلْأَصْوَٰتِ لَصَوْتُ ٱلْحَمِيرِ</span>

### English

Be moderate in your pace. And lower your voice, for the ugliest of all voices is certainly the braying of donkeys.”
