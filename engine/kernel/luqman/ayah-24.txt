### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">نُمَتِّعُهُمْ قَلِيلًا ثُمَّ نَضْطَرُّهُمْ إِلَىٰ عَذَابٍ غَلِيظٍ</span>

### English

We allow them enjoyment for a little while, then ˹in time˺ We will force them into a harsh torment.
