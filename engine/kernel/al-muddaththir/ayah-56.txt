### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا يَذْكُرُونَ إِلَّآ أَن يَشَآءَ ٱللَّهُ ۚ هُوَ أَهْلُ ٱلتَّقْوَىٰ وَأَهْلُ ٱلْمَغْفِرَةِ</span>

### English

But they cannot do so unless Allah wills. He ˹alone˺ is worthy to be feared and entitled to forgive.
