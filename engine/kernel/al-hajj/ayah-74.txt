### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">مَا قَدَرُوا۟ ٱللَّهَ حَقَّ قَدْرِهِۦٓ ۗ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَقَوِىٌّ عَزِيزٌ</span>

### English

They have not shown Allah the reverence He deserves. Surely Allah is All-Powerful, Almighty.
