### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَنَّ ٱلسَّاعَةَ ءَاتِيَةٌ لَّا رَيْبَ فِيهَا وَأَنَّ ٱللَّهَ يَبْعَثُ مَن فِى ٱلْقُبُورِ</span>

### English

And certainly the Hour is coming, there is no doubt about it. And Allah will surely resurrect those in the graves.
