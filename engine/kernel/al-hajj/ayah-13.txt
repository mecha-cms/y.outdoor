### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَدْعُوا۟ لَمَن ضَرُّهُۥٓ أَقْرَبُ مِن نَّفْعِهِۦ ۚ لَبِئْسَ ٱلْمَوْلَىٰ وَلَبِئْسَ ٱلْعَشِيرُ</span>

### English

They invoke those whose worship leads to harm, not benefit. What an evil patron and what an evil associate!
