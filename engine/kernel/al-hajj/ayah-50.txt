### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَعَمِلُوا۟ ٱلصَّـٰلِحَـٰتِ لَهُم مَّغْفِرَةٌ وَرِزْقٌ كَرِيمٌ</span>

### English

So those who believe and do good will have forgiveness and an honourable provision.
