### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَكُمْ فِيهَا مَنَـٰفِعُ إِلَىٰٓ أَجَلٍ مُّسَمًّى ثُمَّ مَحِلُّهَآ إِلَى ٱلْبَيْتِ ٱلْعَتِيقِ</span>

### English

You may benefit from sacrificial animals for an appointed term, then their place of sacrifice is at the Ancient House.
