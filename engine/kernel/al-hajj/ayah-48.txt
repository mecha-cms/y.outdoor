### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَكَأَيِّن مِّن قَرْيَةٍ أَمْلَيْتُ لَهَا وَهِىَ ظَالِمَةٌ ثُمَّ أَخَذْتُهَا وَإِلَىَّ ٱلْمَصِيرُ</span>

### English

Many are the societies whose end We delayed while they did wrong, then seized them. And to Me is the final return.
