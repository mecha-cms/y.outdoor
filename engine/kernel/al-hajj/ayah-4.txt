### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">كُتِبَ عَلَيْهِ أَنَّهُۥ مَن تَوَلَّاهُ فَأَنَّهُۥ يُضِلُّهُۥ وَيَهْدِيهِ إِلَىٰ عَذَابِ ٱلسَّعِيرِ</span>

### English

It has been decreed for such devils that whoever takes them as a guide will be misguided and led by them into the torment of the Blaze.
