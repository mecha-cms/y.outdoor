### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَدْعُوا۟ مِن دُونِ ٱللَّهِ مَا لَا يَضُرُّهُۥ وَمَا لَا يَنفَعُهُۥ ۚ ذَٰلِكَ هُوَ ٱلضَّلَـٰلُ ٱلْبَعِيدُ</span>

### English

They call besides Allah what can neither harm nor benefit them. That is ˹truly˺ the farthest one can stray.
