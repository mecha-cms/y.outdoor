### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ يُدَٰفِعُ عَنِ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوٓا۟ ۗ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَا يُحِبُّ كُلَّ خَوَّانٍ كَفُورٍ</span>

### English

Indeed, Allah defends those who believe. Surely Allah does not like whoever is deceitful, ungrateful.
