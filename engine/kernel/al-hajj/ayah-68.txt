### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِن جَـٰدَلُوكَ فَقُلِ ٱللَّهُ أَعْلَمُ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

But if they argue with you, then say, “Allah knows best what you do.”
