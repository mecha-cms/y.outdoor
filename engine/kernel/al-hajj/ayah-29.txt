### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ثُمَّ لْيَقْضُوا۟ تَفَثَهُمْ وَلْيُوفُوا۟ نُذُورَهُمْ وَلْيَطَّوَّفُوا۟ بِٱلْبَيْتِ ٱلْعَتِيقِ</span>

### English

Then let them groom themselves, fulfil their vows, and circle the Ancient House.”
