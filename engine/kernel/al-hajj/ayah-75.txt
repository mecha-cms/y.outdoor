### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱللَّهُ يَصْطَفِى مِنَ ٱلْمَلَـٰٓئِكَةِ رُسُلًا وَمِنَ ٱلنَّاسِ ۚ إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ سَمِيعٌۢ بَصِيرٌ</span>

### English

Allah selects messengers from both angels and people, for Allah is truly All-Hearing, All-Seeing.
