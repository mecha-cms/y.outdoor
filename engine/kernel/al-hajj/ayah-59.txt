### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَيُدْخِلَنَّهُم مُّدْخَلًا يَرْضَوْنَهُۥ ۗ وَإِنَّ ٱللَّهَ لَعَلِيمٌ حَلِيمٌ</span>

### English

He will certainly admit them into a place they will be pleased with. For Allah is truly All-Knowing, Most Forbearing.
