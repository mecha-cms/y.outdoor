### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلِكُلٍّ دَرَجَـٰتٌ مِّمَّا عَمِلُوا۟ ۖ وَلِيُوَفِّيَهُمْ أَعْمَـٰلَهُمْ وَهُمْ لَا يُظْلَمُونَ</span>

### English

Each ˹of the two groups˺ will be ranked according to what they have done so He may fully reward all. And none will be wronged.
