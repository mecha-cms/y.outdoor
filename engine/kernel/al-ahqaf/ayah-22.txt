### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالُوٓا۟ أَجِئْتَنَا لِتَأْفِكَنَا عَنْ ءَالِهَتِنَا فَأْتِنَا بِمَا تَعِدُنَآ إِن كُنتَ مِنَ ٱلصَّـٰدِقِينَ</span>

### English

They argued, “Have you come to turn us away from our gods? Bring us then whatever you threaten us with, if what you say is true.”
