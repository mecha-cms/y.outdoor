### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ أَصْحَـٰبُ ٱلْجَنَّةِ خَـٰلِدِينَ فِيهَا جَزَآءًۢ بِمَا كَانُوا۟ يَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

It is they who will be the residents of Paradise, staying there forever, as a reward for what they used to do.
