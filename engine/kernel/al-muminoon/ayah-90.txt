### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">بَلْ أَتَيْنَـٰهُم بِٱلْحَقِّ وَإِنَّهُمْ لَكَـٰذِبُونَ</span>

### English

In fact, We have brought them the truth, and they are certainly liars.
