### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَهُوَ ٱلَّذِىٓ أَنشَأَ لَكُمُ ٱلسَّمْعَ وَٱلْأَبْصَـٰرَ وَٱلْأَفْـِٔدَةَ ۚ قَلِيلًا مَّا تَشْكُرُونَ</span>

### English

He is the One Who created for you hearing, sight, and intellect. ˹Yet˺ you hardly give any thanks.
