### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَا نُكَلِّفُ نَفْسًا إِلَّا وُسْعَهَا ۖ وَلَدَيْنَا كِتَـٰبٌ يَنطِقُ بِٱلْحَقِّ ۚ وَهُمْ لَا يُظْلَمُونَ</span>

### English

We never require of any soul more than what it can afford. And with Us is a record which speaks the truth. None will be wronged.
