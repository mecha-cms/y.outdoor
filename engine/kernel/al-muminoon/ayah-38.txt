### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنْ هُوَ إِلَّا رَجُلٌ ٱفْتَرَىٰ عَلَى ٱللَّهِ كَذِبًا وَمَا نَحْنُ لَهُۥ بِمُؤْمِنِينَ</span>

### English

He is no more than a man who has fabricated a lie about Allah, and we will never believe in him.”
