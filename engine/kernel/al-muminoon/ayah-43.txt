### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">مَا تَسْبِقُ مِنْ أُمَّةٍ أَجَلَهَا وَمَا يَسْتَـْٔخِرُونَ</span>

### English

No people can advance their doom, nor can they delay it.
