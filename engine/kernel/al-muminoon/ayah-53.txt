### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَتَقَطَّعُوٓا۟ أَمْرَهُم بَيْنَهُمْ زُبُرًا ۖ كُلُّ حِزْبٍۭ بِمَا لَدَيْهِمْ فَرِحُونَ</span>

### English

Yet the people have divided it into different sects, each rejoicing in what they have.
