### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقُل رَّبِّ أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ هَمَزَٰتِ ٱلشَّيَـٰطِينِ</span>

### English

And say, “My Lord! I seek refuge in You from the temptations of the devils.
