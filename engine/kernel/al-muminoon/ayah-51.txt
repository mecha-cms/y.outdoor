### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَـٰٓأَيُّهَا ٱلرُّسُلُ كُلُوا۟ مِنَ ٱلطَّيِّبَـٰتِ وَٱعْمَلُوا۟ صَـٰلِحًا ۖ إِنِّى بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ عَلِيمٌ</span>

### English

O messengers! Eat from what is good and lawful, and act righteously. Indeed, I fully know what you do.
