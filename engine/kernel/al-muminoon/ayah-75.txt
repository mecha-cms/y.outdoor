### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ وَلَوْ رَحِمْنَـٰهُمْ وَكَشَفْنَا مَا بِهِم مِّن ضُرٍّ لَّلَجُّوا۟ فِى طُغْيَـٰنِهِمْ يَعْمَهُونَ</span>

### English

˹Even˺ if We had mercy on them and removed their affliction, they would still persist in their transgression, wandering blindly.
