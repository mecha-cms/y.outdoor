### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَقَدْ وُعِدْنَا نَحْنُ وَءَابَآؤُنَا هَـٰذَا مِن قَبْلُ إِنْ هَـٰذَآ إِلَّآ أَسَـٰطِيرُ ٱلْأَوَّلِينَ</span>

### English

We have already been promised this, as well as our forefathers earlier. This is nothing but ancient fables!”
