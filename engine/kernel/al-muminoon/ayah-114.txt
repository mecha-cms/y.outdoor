### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَـٰلَ إِن لَّبِثْتُمْ إِلَّا قَلِيلًا ۖ لَّوْ أَنَّكُمْ كُنتُمْ تَعْلَمُونَ</span>

### English

He will say, “You only remained for a little while, if only you knew.
