### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَمَن ثَقُلَتْ مَوَٰزِينُهُۥ فَأُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْمُفْلِحُونَ</span>

### English

As for those whose scale is heavy ˹with good deeds˺, it is they who will be successful.
