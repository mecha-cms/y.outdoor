### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَلَمْ تَكُنْ ءَايَـٰتِى تُتْلَىٰ عَلَيْكُمْ فَكُنتُم بِهَا تُكَذِّبُونَ</span>

### English

˹It will be said,˺ “Were My revelations not recited to you, but you used to deny them?”
