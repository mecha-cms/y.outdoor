### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَقَدْ خَلَقْنَا فَوْقَكُمْ سَبْعَ طَرَآئِقَ وَمَا كُنَّا عَنِ ٱلْخَلْقِ غَـٰفِلِينَ</span>

### English

And indeed, We created above you seven levels ˹of heaven˺. We are never unmindful of ˹Our˺ creation.
