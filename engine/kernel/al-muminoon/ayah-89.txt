### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">سَيَقُولُونَ لِلَّهِ ۚ قُلْ فَأَنَّىٰ تُسْحَرُونَ</span>

### English

They will reply, “Allah.” Say, “How are you then so deluded?”
