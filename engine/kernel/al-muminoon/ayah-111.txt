### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنِّى جَزَيْتُهُمُ ٱلْيَوْمَ بِمَا صَبَرُوٓا۟ أَنَّهُمْ هُمُ ٱلْفَآئِزُونَ</span>

### English

Today I have indeed rewarded them for their perseverance: they are certainly the triumphant.”
