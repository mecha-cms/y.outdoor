### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَفَحَسِبْتُمْ أَنَّمَا خَلَقْنَـٰكُمْ عَبَثًا وَأَنَّكُمْ إِلَيْنَا لَا تُرْجَعُونَ</span>

### English

Did you then think that We had created you without purpose, and that you would never be returned to Us?”
