### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱدْفَعْ بِٱلَّتِى هِىَ أَحْسَنُ ٱلسَّيِّئَةَ ۚ نَحْنُ أَعْلَمُ بِمَا يَصِفُونَ</span>

### English

Respond to evil with what is best. We know well what they claim.
