### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَأَنزَلْنَا مِنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءًۢ بِقَدَرٍ فَأَسْكَنَّـٰهُ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ ۖ وَإِنَّا عَلَىٰ ذَهَابٍۭ بِهِۦ لَقَـٰدِرُونَ</span>

### English

We send down rain from the sky in perfect measure, causing it to soak into the earth. And We are surely able to take it away.
