### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَا فَتَحْنَا عَلَيْهِم بَابًا ذَا عَذَابٍ شَدِيدٍ إِذَا هُمْ فِيهِ مُبْلِسُونَ</span>

### English

But as soon as We open for them a gate of severe punishment, they will be utterly desperate.
