### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَقَالُوٓا۟ أَنُؤْمِنُ لِبَشَرَيْنِ مِثْلِنَا وَقَوْمُهُمَا لَنَا عَـٰبِدُونَ</span>

### English

They argued, “Will we believe in two humans, like ourselves, whose people are slaves to us?”
