### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنْ هِىَ إِلَّا حَيَاتُنَا ٱلدُّنْيَا نَمُوتُ وَنَحْيَا وَمَا نَحْنُ بِمَبْعُوثِينَ</span>

### English

There is nothing beyond our worldly life. We die, others are born, and none will be resurrected.
