### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">سَيَقُولُونَ لِلَّهِ ۚ قُلْ أَفَلَا تَذَكَّرُونَ</span>

### English

They will reply, “To Allah!” Say, “Why are you not then mindful?”
