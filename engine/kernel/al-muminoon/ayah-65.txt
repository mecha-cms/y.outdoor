### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَا تَجْـَٔرُوا۟ ٱلْيَوْمَ ۖ إِنَّكُم مِّنَّا لَا تُنصَرُونَ</span>

### English

˹They will be told,˺ “Do not cry for help today. Surely you will never be saved from Us.
