### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقُل رَّبِّ أَنزِلْنِى مُنزَلًا مُّبَارَكًا وَأَنتَ خَيْرُ ٱلْمُنزِلِينَ</span>

### English

And pray, “My Lord! Allow me a blessed landing, for You are the best accommodator.”
