### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَيَعِدُكُمْ أَنَّكُمْ إِذَا مِتُّمْ وَكُنتُمْ تُرَابًا وَعِظَـٰمًا أَنَّكُم مُّخْرَجُونَ</span>

### English

Does he promise you that once you are dead and reduced to dust and bones, you will be brought forth ˹alive˺?
