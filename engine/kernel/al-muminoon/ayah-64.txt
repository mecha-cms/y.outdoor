### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">حَتَّىٰٓ إِذَآ أَخَذْنَا مُتْرَفِيهِم بِٱلْعَذَابِ إِذَا هُمْ يَجْـَٔرُونَ</span>

### English

But as soon as We seize their elite with torment, they start to cry for help.
