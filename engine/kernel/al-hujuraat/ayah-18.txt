### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">إِنَّ ٱللَّهَ يَعْلَمُ غَيْبَ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ۚ وَٱللَّهُ بَصِيرٌۢ بِمَا تَعْمَلُونَ</span>

### English

Surely Allah knows the unseen of the heavens and earth. And Allah is All-Seeing of what you do.”
