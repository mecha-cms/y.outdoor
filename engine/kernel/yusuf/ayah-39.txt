### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَـٰصَـٰحِبَىِ ٱلسِّجْنِ ءَأَرْبَابٌ مُّتَفَرِّقُونَ خَيْرٌ أَمِ ٱللَّهُ ٱلْوَٰحِدُ ٱلْقَهَّارُ</span>

### English

O  my fellow-prisoners! Which is far better: many different lords or Allah—the One, the Supreme?
