### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ هَلْ عَلِمْتُم مَّا فَعَلْتُم بِيُوسُفَ وَأَخِيهِ إِذْ أَنتُمْ جَـٰهِلُونَ</span>

### English

He asked, “Do you remember what you did to Joseph and his brother in your ignorance?”
