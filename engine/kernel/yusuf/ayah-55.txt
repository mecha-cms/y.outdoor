### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ ٱجْعَلْنِى عَلَىٰ خَزَآئِنِ ٱلْأَرْضِ ۖ إِنِّى حَفِيظٌ عَلِيمٌ</span>

### English

Joseph proposed, “Put me in charge of the store-houses of the land, for I am truly reliable and adept.”
