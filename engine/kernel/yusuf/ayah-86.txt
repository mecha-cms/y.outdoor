### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ إِنَّمَآ أَشْكُوا۟ بَثِّى وَحُزْنِىٓ إِلَى ٱللَّهِ وَأَعْلَمُ مِنَ ٱللَّهِ مَا لَا تَعْلَمُونَ</span>

### English

He replied, “I complain of my anguish and sorrow only to Allah, and I know from Allah what you do not know.
