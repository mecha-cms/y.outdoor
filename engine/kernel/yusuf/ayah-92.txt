### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالَ لَا تَثْرِيبَ عَلَيْكُمُ ٱلْيَوْمَ ۖ يَغْفِرُ ٱللَّهُ لَكُمْ ۖ وَهُوَ أَرْحَمُ ٱلرَّٰحِمِينَ</span>

### English

Joseph said, “There is no blame on you today. May Allah forgive you! He is the Most Merciful of the merciful!
