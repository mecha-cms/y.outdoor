### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَإِن لَّمْ تَأْتُونِى بِهِۦ فَلَا كَيْلَ لَكُمْ عِندِى وَلَا تَقْرَبُونِ</span>

### English

But if you do not bring him to me ˹next time˺, I will have no grain for you, nor will you ever come close to me again.”
