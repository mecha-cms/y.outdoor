### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">قَالُوا۟ يَـٰٓأَبَانَا مَا لَكَ لَا تَأْمَ۫نَّا عَلَىٰ يُوسُفَ وَإِنَّا لَهُۥ لَنَـٰصِحُونَ</span>

### English

They said, “O our father! Why do you not trust us with Joseph, although we truly wish him well?
