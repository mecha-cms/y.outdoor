### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَجَآءَ إِخْوَةُ يُوسُفَ فَدَخَلُوا۟ عَلَيْهِ فَعَرَفَهُمْ وَهُمْ لَهُۥ مُنكِرُونَ</span>

### English

And Joseph’s brothers came and entered his presence. He recognized them but they were unaware of who he really was.
