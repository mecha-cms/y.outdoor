### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ثُمَّ يَأْتِى مِنۢ بَعْدِ ذَٰلِكَ سَبْعٌ شِدَادٌ يَأْكُلْنَ مَا قَدَّمْتُمْ لَهُنَّ إِلَّا قَلِيلًا مِّمَّا تُحْصِنُونَ</span>

### English

Then after that will come seven years of great hardship which will consume whatever you have saved, except the little you will store ˹for seed˺.
