### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱذْهَبُوا۟ بِقَمِيصِى هَـٰذَا فَأَلْقُوهُ عَلَىٰ وَجْهِ أَبِى يَأْتِ بَصِيرًا وَأْتُونِى بِأَهْلِكُمْ أَجْمَعِينَ</span>

### English

Go with this shirt of mine and cast it over my father’s face, and he will regain his sight. Then come back to me with your whole family.”
