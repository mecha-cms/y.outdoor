### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">أَرْسِلْهُ مَعَنَا غَدًا يَرْتَعْ وَيَلْعَبْ وَإِنَّا لَهُۥ لَحَـٰفِظُونَ</span>

### English

Send him out with us tomorrow so that he may enjoy himself and play. And we will really watch over him.”
