### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَكَأَيِّن مِّنْ ءَايَةٍ فِى ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ يَمُرُّونَ عَلَيْهَا وَهُمْ عَنْهَا مُعْرِضُونَ</span>

### English

How many signs in the heavens and the earth do they pass by with indifference!
