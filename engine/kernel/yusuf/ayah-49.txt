### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ثُمَّ يَأْتِى مِنۢ بَعْدِ ذَٰلِكَ عَامٌ فِيهِ يُغَاثُ ٱلنَّاسُ وَفِيهِ يَعْصِرُونَ</span>

### English

Then after that will come a year in which people will receive abundant rain and they will press ˹oil and wine˺.”
