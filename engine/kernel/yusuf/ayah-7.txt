### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">۞ لَّقَدْ كَانَ فِى يُوسُفَ وَإِخْوَتِهِۦٓ ءَايَـٰتٌ لِّلسَّآئِلِينَ</span>

### English

Indeed, in the story of Joseph and his brothers there are lessons for all who ask.
