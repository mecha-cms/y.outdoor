### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَلَأَجْرُ ٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ خَيْرٌ لِّلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَكَانُوا۟ يَتَّقُونَ</span>

### English

And the reward of the Hereafter is far better for those who are faithful and are mindful ˹of Allah˺.
