### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَإِن كَانَ قَمِيصُهُۥ قُدَّ مِن دُبُرٍ فَكَذَبَتْ وَهُوَ مِنَ ٱلصَّـٰدِقِينَ</span>

### English

But if it is torn from the back, then she has lied and he is truthful.”
