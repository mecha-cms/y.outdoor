### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَمَا يُؤْمِنُ أَكْثَرُهُم بِٱللَّهِ إِلَّا وَهُم مُّشْرِكُونَ</span>

### English

And most of them do not believe in Allah without associating others with Him ˹in worship˺.
