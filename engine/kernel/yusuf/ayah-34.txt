### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">فَٱسْتَجَابَ لَهُۥ رَبُّهُۥ فَصَرَفَ عَنْهُ كَيْدَهُنَّ ۚ إِنَّهُۥ هُوَ ٱلسَّمِيعُ ٱلْعَلِيمُ</span>

### English

So his Lord responded to him, turning their cunning away from him. Surely He is the All-Hearing, All-Knowing.
