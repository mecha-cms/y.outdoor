### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">سَلَـٰمٌ عَلَيْكُم بِمَا صَبَرْتُمْ ۚ فَنِعْمَ عُقْبَى ٱلدَّارِ</span>

### English

“Peace be upon you for your perseverance. How excellent is the ultimate abode!”
