### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">يَمْحُوا۟ ٱللَّهُ مَا يَشَآءُ وَيُثْبِتُ ۖ وَعِندَهُۥٓ أُمُّ ٱلْكِتَـٰبِ</span>

### English

Allah eliminates and confirms what He wills, and with Him is the Master Record.
