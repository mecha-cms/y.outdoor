### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">ٱللَّهُ يَعْلَمُ مَا تَحْمِلُ كُلُّ أُنثَىٰ وَمَا تَغِيضُ ٱلْأَرْحَامُ وَمَا تَزْدَادُ ۖ وَكُلُّ شَىْءٍ عِندَهُۥ بِمِقْدَارٍ</span>

### English

Allah knows what every female bears and what increases and decreases in the wombs. And with Him everything is determined with precision.
