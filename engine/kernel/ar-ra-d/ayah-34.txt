### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">لَّهُمْ عَذَابٌ فِى ٱلْحَيَوٰةِ ٱلدُّنْيَا ۖ وَلَعَذَابُ ٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ أَشَقُّ ۖ وَمَا لَهُم مِّنَ ٱللَّهِ مِن وَاقٍ</span>

### English

For them is punishment in this worldly life, but the punishment of the Hereafter is truly far worse. And none can shield them from Allah.
