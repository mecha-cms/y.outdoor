### Arabic

<span style="direction: rtl; display: block; font-size: 150%; text-align: right;">وَقَوْمَ نُوحٍ مِّن قَبْلُ ۖ إِنَّهُمْ كَانُوا۟ هُمْ أَظْلَمَ وَأَطْغَىٰ</span>

### English

And before ˹that He destroyed˺ the people of Noah, who were truly far worse in wrongdoing and transgression.
